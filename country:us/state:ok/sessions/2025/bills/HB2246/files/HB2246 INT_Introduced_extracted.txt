Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to special education; requiring State
Department of Education develop certain standards;
8 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 13-101A of Title 70, unless
14 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 The State Department of Education shall develop standards to
16 implement the least restrictive environment requirements in the
17 Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) to ensure
18 students with special needs are not unnecessarily excluded from the
19 classroom.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
