I move to amend House Bill No. 1628 as follows:
1. On Page 15, Line 23, by deleting after the word “contractor”, the words “or residential roofing
contractor”;
2. On Page 19, Line 13, by deleting before the word “to”, the words “and the Construction
Industries Board,”; and
2. On Page 19, Line 14, by inserting after the period, the following language:
“Any applicant who has been registered, is in good standing with the Board, and possesses a current
commercial roofer endorsement on the effective date of this act shall be exempt from the residential
roofing examination and additional ten (10) hours of continuing education credits required to receive
a residential roofing endorsement.”
