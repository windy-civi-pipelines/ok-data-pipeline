Section 2:
Sections 8-101 and 8-101.2, as amended by Section 1,
11 Chapter 193, O.S.L. 2022 (70 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section
8-101.2), which relate to transfer students; allowing
12 certain students to transfer to adjacent school
district; directing automatic approval of transfer;
13 excepting certain capacity requirements for transfer
to adjacent school district; mandating student comply
14 with certain transfer requirements if receiving
district is not adjacent to district of residence;
15 deleting automatic transfer to any school district
for certain students; and providing an effective
16 date.

20 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 8-101, is
21 amended to read as follows:
22 Section 8-101. A. Any student residing in a school district
23 that does not offer the grade which the student is entitled to
24 pursue shall be allowed to transfer to a school district inside or

6 subsection A of this section, which shall be automatically approved
7 and not subject to capacity requirements provided in Section 8-101.2
8 of this title. Thereafter, a student may apply for any other kind

14 school district where the student resides pursuant to subsection A

16 of this title to request a transfer to another school district which
17 offers the grade the student is entitled to pursue.
