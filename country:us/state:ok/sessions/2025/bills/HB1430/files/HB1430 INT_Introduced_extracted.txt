Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to higher education; creating the
Tinnitus Research Grant Program Revolving Fund;
8 specifying source of fund; providing for
expenditures; providing purpose of fund; directing
9 the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education to
award grants; establishing permitted use of grants;
10 directing Regents to establish criteria and process
for application; directing that grants be considered
11 on statewide basis; directing Regents to promulgate
rules; providing for codification; and providing an
12 effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3235 of Title 70, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
20 fund for the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education to be
21 designated the "Tinnitus Research Grant Program Revolving Fund".
22 The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year
23 limitations, and shall consist of monies appropriated by the
24 Legislature and private dollars given to the Oklahoma State Regents
Req. No. 10198 Page 1
1 for Higher Education for the purpose of supporting the tinnitus
2 research grant program. All monies accruing to the credit of the
3 fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the
4 Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education for the purpose of
5 running and distributing the tinnitus research grant program
6 described in subsection B of this section. Expenditures from the
7 fund shall be made on warrants issued by the State Treasurer against
8 claims filed by the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education with
9 the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
10 approval and payment.
11 B. Contingent upon the amount of funds available in the
12 Tinnitus Research Grant Program Revolving Fund, the Oklahoma State
13 Regents for Higher Education shall award one or more grants annually
14 to private and public universities in Oklahoma. The grants shall be
15 used to research and find a cure for tinnitus. The Oklahoma State
16 Regents for Higher Education shall determine the criteria for and
17 establish a process for the submission of grant applications. The
18 applications shall be considered on a statewide competitive basis.
19 The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education shall promulgate
20 rules for the implementation of the grants issued pursuant to this
21 section.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
23
