7 An Act relating to elections; amending 26 O.S. 2021,

Section 2:
Section 2-111.2, which relates to the election of
8 officers of county election boards; clarifying that
the secretary shall not be a voting member of the
9 county election board; and providing an effective
date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 2-111.2, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 2-111.2. On the first Monday in June, 1983, and every
16 four (4) years thereafter, the county election board shall meet upon
17 call of the secretary to elect a chairman and vice chairman. The
18 secretary can be elected neither chairman nor vice chairman but and
19 shall not be a voting member of the county election board.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
