9 An Act relating to county officers; amending 19 O.S.

of employer liability; removing certain reference to
12 duty termination; updating statutory language; and
providing an effective date.

15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 19 O.S. 2021, Section 547.1, as
17 amended by Section 1, Chapter 14, O.S.L. 2022 (19 O.S. Supp. 2024,
18 Section 547.1), is amended to read as follows:
19 Section 547.1. A. Any Oklahoma sheriff, deputy sheriff, or a
20 reserve deputy sheriff who is assigned to perform duties associated
21 with the National Sheriffs’ Association annual conference within
22 Oklahoma County during the month of June 2024, outside of his or her
23 county of employment shall have the same powers and duties as though
24 employed by the Oklahoma County Sheriff’s Office sheriff’s office
