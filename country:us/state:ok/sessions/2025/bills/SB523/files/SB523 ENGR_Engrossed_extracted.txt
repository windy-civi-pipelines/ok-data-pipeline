6 An Act relating to county officers; amending 19 O.S.
2021, Section 547.1, as amended by Section 1, Chapter
7 14, O.S.L. 2022 (19 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 547.1),
which relates to assigned duties; authorizing certain
8 duties outside county of employment; modifying scope
of employer liability; removing certain reference to
9 duty termination; updating statutory language; and
providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 19 O.S. 2021, Section 547.1, as
14 amended by Section 1, Chapter 14, O.S.L. 2022 (19 O.S. Supp. 2024,
15 Section 547.1), is amended to read as follows:
16 Section 547.1. A. Any Oklahoma sheriff, deputy sheriff, or a
17 reserve deputy sheriff who is assigned to perform duties associated
18 with the National Sheriffs’ Association annual conference within
19 Oklahoma County during the month of June 2024, outside of his or her
20 county of employment shall have the same powers and duties as though
21 employed by the Oklahoma County Sheriff’s Office sheriff’s office
22 for which he or she is performing duties. Liability for the conduct
23 of any sheriff, deputy sheriff, or reserve deputy sheriff, while
24 performing duties during the National Sheriffs’ Association annual
ENGR. S. B. NO. 523 Page 1
1 conf erence or any activities in conjunction with that conference for
2 June 2024 outside of his or her county of employment, shall remain
3 the responsibility of the counties of county in which they are he or
4 she is regularly employed. Salaries, insurance, and other benefits
5 shall be provided in the regular manner by the county in which the
6 sheriffs sheriff, deputy sheriffs sheriff, and or reserve deputy
7 sheriffs are sheriff is regularly employed.
8 B. Any duties assigned to sheriffs, deputy sheriffs, and
9 reserve deputy sheriffs as a result of this section shall terminate
10 on July 1, 2024.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
12 Passed the Senate the 10th day of March, 2025.
