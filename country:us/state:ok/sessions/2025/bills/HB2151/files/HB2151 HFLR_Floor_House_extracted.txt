10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to education; creating the Federal
Education Guidance Disclosure Act; requiring the
12 State Department of Education to publish certain
documents on a website; prescribing publication
13 timeframe; construing website creation timeline;
mandating use of hyperlinks; directing retention of
14 certain rescinded documents; stating retention
requirements; defining term; providing for
15 codification; and providing an effective date.
16
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 24-162 of Title 70, unless there
21 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 A. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Federal
23 Education Guidance Disclosure Act".
24
HB2151 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 B. Within five (5) days of receiving a guidance document from
2 the United States Department of Education, the State Department of
3 Education shall publish the guidance document on an Internet website
4 designated by the State Superintendent of Public Instruction unless
5 the guidance document contains sensitive or confidential information
6 not legal to disclose due to privacy laws.
7 C. 1. The State Department of Education shall create the
8 designated Internet website for publication described in subsection
9 B of this section no later than ninety (90) days after the effective
10 date of this act.
11 2. The State Department of Education shall include hyperlinks
12 on the Internet website, in a conspicuous manner, that provide
13 access to the guidance documents received pursuant to subsection B
14 of this section.
15 D. If a guidance document is rescinded by the United States
16 Department of Education, the State Department of Education shall
17 maintain the rescinded guidance document at the location described
18 in subsection C of this section and indicate within fifteen (15)
19 days of such rescission:
20 1. That the document has been rescinded; and
21 2. The date the document was rescinded.
22 E. For purposes of this section, "guidance document" means a
23 federal agency statement of general applicability, other than a rule
24 that has the force and effect of law promulgated in accordance with
HB2151 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 the notice and comment procedures under 5 U.S.C., Section 553, that
2 is designated by an agency official as setting forth a policy or
3 interpretation on a statutory, regulatory, or technical issue. A
4 guidance document may include, but is not limited to, a memorandum,
5 notice, bulletin, directive, letter, or no-action letter. The term
6 guidance document shall be construed broadly to effectuate the
7 purpose and intent of this act.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
9
10 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION OVERSIGHT, dated
02/26/2025 - DO PASS, As Amended and Coauthored.
