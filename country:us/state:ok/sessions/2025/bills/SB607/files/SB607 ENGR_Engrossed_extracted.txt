6 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Evidence Code;
providing for admissibility of certain statements in
7 certain proceedings; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
8
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2803.3 of Title 12, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A statement that purports to narrate, describe, report, or
16 explain an incident or incidents of domestic abuse as defined in
17 Section 60.1 of Title 22 of the Oklahoma Statutes:
18 1. Made by the victim of domestic abuse to a law enforcement
19 officer within one (1) week of the incident;
20 2. On an application for a protective order by the victim of
21 domestic abuse within one (1) week of the incident; or
22 3. Given as testimony of the victim of domestic abuse made at a
23 hearing on application for a protective order,
24
ENGR. S. B. NO. 607 Page 1
1 shal l be admissible in pre-trial or post-trial criminal and juvenile
2 delinquent domestic abuse prosecutions including preliminary
3 hearings, prosecutive merit hearings, or hearings on the revocation
4 of probation or acceleration of a deferred judgment.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
6 Passed the Senate the 10th day of March, 2025.
