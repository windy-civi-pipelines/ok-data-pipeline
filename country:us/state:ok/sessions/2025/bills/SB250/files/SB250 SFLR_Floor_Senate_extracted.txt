8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Minimum Wage Act;
amending 40 O.S. 2021, Section 197.16, which relates
9 to tips, gratuities, meals, or lodging; updating
statutory reference; allowing for alternate
10 computation for wage under certain conditions;
updating statutory language; and providing an
11 effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 40 O.S. 2021, Section 197.16, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 197.16. A. To compute the minimum wage of any employee
18 coming within the purview of this act the Oklahoma Minimum Wage Act,
19 credit toward the minimum required wage must shall be given for any
20 tips or gratuities, meals, or lodging received by the employee up to
21 but not exceeding fifty percent (50%) of said the wage.
22 B. To compute the minimum wage of any employee coming within
23 the purview of the Oklahoma Minimum Wage Act should the minimum wage
24 exceed the current federal minimum wage, credit toward the minimum
