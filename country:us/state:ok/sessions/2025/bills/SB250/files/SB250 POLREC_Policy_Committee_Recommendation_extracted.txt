9 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
10 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Minimum Wage Act;
amending 40 O.S. 2021, Section 197.16, which relates
11 to tips, gratuities, meals, or lodging; updating
statutory reference; allowing for alternate
12 computation for wage under certain conditions;
updating statutory language; and providing an
13 effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 40 O.S. 2021, Section 197.16, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 197.16. A. To compute the minimum wage of any employee
20 coming within the purview of this act the Oklahoma Minimum Wage Act,
21 credit toward the minimum required wage must shall be given for any
22 tips or gratuities, meals, or lodging received by the employee up to
23 but not exceeding fifty percent (50%) of said wage provided that the
24
Req. No. 13458 Page 1
1 empl oyer pays a cash wage not less than required under 29 C.F.R.
2 Section 531.50(a)(1).
3 B. To compute the minimum wage of any employee coming within
4 the purview of the Oklahoma Minimum Wage Act should the minimum wage
5 exceed the current federal minimum wage, credit toward the minimum
6 required wage may be given for any tips or gratuities, meals, or
7 lodging received by the employee.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
