Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to revenue and taxation; amending 68
O.S. 2021, Section 1503.1, which relates to
8 exemptions from coin-operated device licensing fees;
providing exemption for certain coin-operated devices
9 manufactured prior to specified date; and providing
an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 68 O.S. 2021, Section 1503.1, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 1503.1. The following coin-operated vending devices
16 shall be exempt from the provisions of this article, Section 1501 et
17 seq. of this title:
18 1. All coin-operated vending devices owned by and located in a
19 public or private school, a church, or a governmental entity;
20 2. All coin-operated vending devices which dispense only
21 newspapers or periodicals;
22 3. All coin-operated vending devices which dispense only
23 postage stamps; and
24
Req. No. 11573 Page 1
1 4. All coin-operated vending devices installed on federal
2 military bases; and
3 5. All coin-operated devices manufactured prior to January 1,
4 2000, the price per play of which does not exceed fifty cents
5 ($0.50) if the device is designed to provide entertainment in the
6 form of a game or music and which does not involve gambling. As
7 used in this paragraph, a device involves "gambling" if the outcome
8 produced by the device is based on pure chance without any physical
9 or intellectual skills or efforts being required of the game player
10 or participant.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
