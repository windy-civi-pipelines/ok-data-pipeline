47 O.S. 2021, Section 1135.5, as last amended by
6 Section 1, Chapter 88, O.S.L. 2024 (47 O.S. Supp.
2024, Section 1135.5), which relates to the issuance
7 of special license plates; updating statutory
language; modifying certain special license plate;
8 repealing 47 O.S. 2021, Section 1104.13, which
relates to the Oklahoma Zoological Society Revolving
9 Fund; and providing an effective date.

12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 47 O.S. 2021, Section 1135.5, as
14 last amended by Section 1, Chapter 88, O.S.L. 2024 (47 O.S. Supp.
15 2024, Section 1135.5), is amended to read as follows:
16 Section 1135.5. A. Service Oklahoma is hereby authorized to
17 design and issue appropriate official special license plates to
18 persons wishing to demonstrate support and provide financial
19 assistance as provided by this section.
20 Special license plates shall not be transferred to any other
21 person but shall be removed from the vehicle upon transfer of
22 ownership and retained. The special license plate may then be used
23 on another vehicle but only after such other vehicle has been
24 registered for the current year with a licensed operator.

1 Special license plates shall be renewed each year by Service

7 presentation to a licensed operator or Service Oklahoma. The
8 license plates shall be issued on a staggered system.

20 and renewals shall be paid out of the Service Oklahoma Reimbursement

22 licensed operator pursuant to subsection E of Section 1141.1 of this
23 title.
