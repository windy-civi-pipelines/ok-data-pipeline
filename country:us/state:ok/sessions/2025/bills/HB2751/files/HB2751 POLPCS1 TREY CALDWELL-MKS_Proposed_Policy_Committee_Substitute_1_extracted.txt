8 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to electric transmission facilities;
granting certain authority to the Oklahoma
10 Corporation Commission; determining conditions for
when certain utilities shall construct, own, or
11 maintain certain facilities; determining connection
of transmission facilities to generation facilities;
12 requiring competitive bids for specific construction
projects; requiring the use of an independent
13 evaluator; protecting use of right of way; requiring
certain Certificates of Authority for certain
14 entities; providing for codification; providing an
effective date; and declaring an emergency.
15
16
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 296 of Title 17, unless there is
21 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 A. The Commission shall have the authority to ensure the
23 development of a new high-voltage transmission line of three hundred
24 kilovolts (300kV) or greater approved for construction in a
Req. No. 12533 Page 1
1 Sout hwest Power Pool transmission plan provides reliable service at
2 the lowest reasonable cost to Oklahoma retail electric consumers.
3 B. For a retail electric supplier or rural electric cooperative
4 with an approved extension of its existing electric transmission
5 facility or to the facilities within or through any territory
6 already served by or connecting to facilities owned by it, the
7 retail electric supplier or rural electric cooperative shall be
8 permitted to construct, own, and maintain such facilities meet the
9 following conditions:
10 1. The retail electric supplier or rural electric cooperative
11 identifies any energy resource to which the electric transmission
12 facility is to be directly connected or, to the extent known,
13 resources to which the electric transmission facility could be
14 connected to integrate new or existing natural gas generation;
15 2. The retail electric supplier or rural electric cooperative
16 solicits competitive bids for construction of an extension of the
17 electric transmission facility. Transmission developers, as defined
18 in Section 291 of Title 17 of the Oklahoma Statutes, shall not be
19 precluded from submitting bids through the solicitation described in
20 this section; and
21 3. Such solicitation for competitive bids is overseen by an
22 independent evaluator appointed by the Commission. The Commission's
23 independent evaluator shall consider factors including, but not
24
Req. No. 12533 Page 2
1 limi ted to, cost of construction, cost of operation and maintenance,
2 reliability, and decommissioning in its evaluation.
3 C. Nothing in this section is intended to supersede the rights
4 of any person, firm, corporation, entity, or incumbent electric
5 transmission owner described in any other statute, alter an
6 incumbent electric transmission owner's use and control of its
7 existing right of way, or eliminate any requirement for a
8 transmission developer to seek a Certificate of Authority pursuant
9 to Enrolled House Bill No. 2756 of the 60th Regular Session of the
10 Oklahoma Legislature.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
12 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
13 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
14 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
15 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
