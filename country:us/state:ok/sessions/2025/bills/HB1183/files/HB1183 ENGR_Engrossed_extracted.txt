10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 68 O.S. 2021, Section 2104, as
12 amended by Section 237, Chapter 282, O.S.L. 2022 (68 O.S. Supp.
13 2024, Section 2104), is amended to read as follows:
14 Section 2104. A. The value of any motor vehicle, except a
15 manufactured home, for the purposes of the excise tax levied by
16 Section 2103 of this title, shall be determined as of the time the
17 person applying for a certificate of title thereto obtained either
18 ownership or possession of the vehicle, which shall be presumed to
19 be the actual date of the sale or other transfer of ownership, and
20 assignment of the certificate of title.
21 B. The value of any vehicle, for purposes of the excise tax
22 levied by Section 2103 of this title, shall be the actual sales
23 price of such a vehicle before any discounts or credits are given
24 for a trade-in. However, the value of the vehicle prior to the
ENGR. H. B. NO. 1183 Page 1
1 subt raction of such discounts or credits for a trade-in shall be
2 required to be within twenty percent (20%) of the average retail
3 price value of such vehicle as listed in the automotive reference
4 material prescribed by Service Oklahoma. The actual sales price of
5 the vehicle, which total shall be the basis of the motor vehicle
6 excise tax, as well as the number of tires on the vehicle and the
7 tire rim diameters, shall be entered on the bill of sale furnished
8 by the seller to the purchaser, or on such other form as may be
9 prescribed by Service Oklahoma.
10 Upon receipt of the properly completed bill of sale or other
11 form as prescribed by Service Oklahoma, and the payment of all
12 applicable taxes and fees, Service Oklahoma or an appointed licensed
13 operator shall issue a vehicle certificate of title in accordance
14 with the provisions of the Oklahoma Vehicle License and Registration
15 Act.
16 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
17 Passed the House of Representatives the 13th day of March, 2025.
