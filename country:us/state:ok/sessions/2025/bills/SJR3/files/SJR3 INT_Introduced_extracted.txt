18 Oklahoma Constitution to read as follows:

19 Section 8C. A. Despite any provision to the contrary,
19
20 beginning January 1, 2005, through December 31, 2025, the fair cash
20
21 value, as determined by law, on each homestead of an individual head

22 of household whose gross household income from all sources for the

23 preceding calendar year did not exceed an amount as provided in
23
24 subsection B of this section, and which individual head of household

4 older and had gross household income from all sources which did not
4
5 exceed an amount as provided in subsection B of this section.
5

6
7 shall not exceed such amount as long as the individual head of

8 household who is sixty-five (65) years of age or older owns and
