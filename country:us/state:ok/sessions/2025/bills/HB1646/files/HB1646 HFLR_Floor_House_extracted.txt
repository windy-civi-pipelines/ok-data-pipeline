12 An Act relating to professions and occupations;

13 which relate to real estate appraisers; removing
minimum educational classroom hours; requiring
14 educational requirements as specified by the

15 Foundation; removing requirement that cost of classes
be established by the Board; removing requirement
16 that complaints must be signed by the person filing
the complaint; providing that complaints may be made
17 on a paper or electronic form; and providing an
effective date.

7 cause exists for violations of the code of ethics set forth in this
8 act.

17 1. "Substantially relates" means the nature of criminal conduct

21 2. "Poses a reasonable threat" means the nature of criminal
22 conduct for which the person was convicted involved an act or threat
23 of harm against another and has a bearing on the fitness or ability
24 to serve the public or work with others in the occupation.
