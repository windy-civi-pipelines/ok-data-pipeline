9 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
10 An Act relating to construction by political
subdivisions; amending 61 O.S. 2021, Section 108,
11 which relates to written statements under oath to
accompany bids; removing requirement for certain
12 statements to be disclosed under oath; and declaring
an emergency.
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 61 O.S. 2021, Section 108, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 108. Each bidder shall accompany the bid with a written
20 statement under oath disclosing the following information:
21 1. The nature of any partnership, joint venture or other
22 business relationships then in effect or which existed within one
23 (1) year prior to the date of such statement with the architect,
24 engineer or other party to the project;
Req. No. 13629 Page 1
1 2. Any such business relationship then in effect or which
2 existed within one (1) year prior to the date of such statement
3 between any officer or director of the bidding company and any
4 officer or director of the architectural or engineering firm or
5 other party to the project; and
6 3. The names of all persons having any such business
7 relationships and the positions they hold with their respective
8 companies or firms. If none of the business relationships
9 hereinabove mentioned exist, then a statement to that effect.
10 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
11 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
12 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
13 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
