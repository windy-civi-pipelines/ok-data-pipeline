An Act relating to The Governmental Tort Claims Act;
amending 51 O.S. 2021, Sections 152, as last amended
by Section 111, Chapter 452, O.S.L. 2024, and 154 (51
O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 152), which relate to
definitions and extent of liability; defining term;
modifying definitions; increasing limits on liability
for certain claims; requiring certain increases for
liability limits after specified time periods;
updating statutory references and language; making
language gender neutral; and providing an effective
date.
