9 An Act relating to state government; amending Section
1, Chapter 425, O.S.L. 2024 (74 O.S. Supp. 2024,
10 Section 2294.1), which relates to the Oklahoma Museum
of Popular Culture Supplemental Revolving Fund;
11 modifying determined funds provided for expenditures;
and providing an effective date.

13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 425, O.S.L.
18 2024 (74 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 2294.1), is amended to read as
19 follows:
20 Section 2294.1. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a
21 revolving fund for the Oklahoma Historical Society, to be designated
22 the "Oklahoma Museum of Popular Culture Supplemental Revolving
23 Fund". The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal
24 year limitations, and shall consist of all monies received by the
Req. No. 12559 Page 1
1 Okla homa Historical Society from appropriations of the Legislature
2 designated for deposit in this fund. All monies accruing to the
3 credit of the fund are hereby appropriated and shall be budgeted and
4 expended to provide funding for repairs, planning, staging,
5 refurbishments, and improvements to real and personal property, and
6 funding for construction of the Oklahoma Museum of Popular Culture.
7 No expenditures shall be made from the fund until the State
8 Treasurer determines that funds provided by local and county
9 governments, the federal government, or through monetary donations
10 or enforceable pledges for monetary donations in the amount of
11 Eighteen Million Dollars ($18,000,000.00) are made for repairs,
12 planning, staging, refurbishments, and improvements to real and
13 personal property, and funding for construction of the Oklahoma
14 Museum of Popular Culture; provided, no funds collected or received
15 prior to May 25, 2023, shall be counted toward the amount.
16 Expenditures from the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the
17 State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with the
18 Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
19 approval and payment. On November 15, 2025, if no determination is
20 made by the State Treasurer pursuant to the provisions of this
21 section authorizing expenditures from this fund, all unencumbered
22 funds remaining in the Oklahoma Museum of Popular Culture
23 Supplemental Revolving Fund shall be transferred to the State
24
Req. No. 12559 Page 2
1 Trea surer for deposit in the General Revenue Fund of the State
2 Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2024.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
