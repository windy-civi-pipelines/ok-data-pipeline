10 An Act relating to state government; amending Section
1, Chapter 425, O.S.L. 2024 (74 O.S. Supp. 2024,
11 Section 2294.1), which relates to the Oklahoma Museum
of Popular Culture Supplemental Revolving Fund;
12 modifying determined funds provided for expenditures;
and providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 425, O.S.L.
17 2024 (74 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 2294.1), is amended to read as
18 follows:
19 Section 2294.1. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a
20 revolving fund for the Oklahoma Historical Society, to be designated
21 the "Oklahoma Museum of Popular Culture Supplemental Revolving
22 Fund". The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal
23 year limitations, and shall consist of all monies received by the
24 Oklahoma Historical Society from appropriations of the Legislature
HB2673 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 desi gnated for deposit in this fund. All monies accruing to the
2 credit of the fund are hereby appropriated and shall be budgeted and
3 expended to provide funding for repairs, planning, staging,
4 refurbishments, and improvements to real and personal property, and
5 funding for construction of the Oklahoma Museum of Popular Culture.
6 No expenditures shall be made from the fund until the State
7 Treasurer determines that funds provided by local and county
8 governments, the federal government, or through monetary donations
9 or enforceable pledges for monetary donations in the amount of
10 Eighteen Million Dollars ($18,000,000.00) are made for repairs,
11 planning, staging, refurbishments, and improvements to real and
12 personal property, and funding for construction of the Oklahoma
13 Museum of Popular Culture; provided, no funds collected or received
14 prior to May 25, 2023, shall be counted toward the amount.
15 Expenditures from the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the
16 State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with the
17 Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
18 approval and payment. On November 15, 2025, if no determination is
19 made by the State Treasurer pursuant to the provisions of this
20 section authorizing expenditures from this fund, all unencumbered
21 funds remaining in the Oklahoma Museum of Popular Culture
22 Supplemental Revolving Fund shall be transferred to the State
23 Treasurer for deposit in the General Revenue Fund of the State
24 Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2024.
