9 An Act relating to state contract employees;
directing state agencies report number of contract
10 employees; requiring reports to include pay;
directing for reports to be made through existing
11 software; granting the Office of Management and
Enterprise Services certain rulemaking authority;
12 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 61.9 of Title 74, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. All state agencies shall report to the Office of Management
20 and Enterprise Services the number of contract employees within its
21 agency.
22 B. Reports shall include the pay of each contract employee.
23 C. Reporting shall be made through existing payroll software,
24 including, but not limited to, Workday VNDLY.
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB1607 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 D. The Office of Management and Enterprise Services shall have
2 the authority to promulgate rules to carry out the provisions of
3 this act.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
