14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 63 O.S. 2021, Section 5060, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 5060. A. Where practicable and in accordance with
18 state and federal law, the state Medicaid program shall not contract
19 with an out-of-state medical provider for treatment that is
20 available from one or more providers licensed and practicing in the
21 State of Oklahoma. For the purposes of this section, "treatment"
22 shall refer to services that require the physical presence of the
23 patient and the direct care of a medical provider. Services that
