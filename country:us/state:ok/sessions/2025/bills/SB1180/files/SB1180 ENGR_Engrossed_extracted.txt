7 An Act relating to the Legislative Service Bureau;
making an appropriation; identifying source of funds;
8 stating purpose; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Legislative
13 Service Bureau from monies not otherwise appropriated from the ARPA
14 Administrative Costs Fund created in Section 255.5 of Title 62 of
15 the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of Two Hundred Forty-eight Thousand
16 Dollars ($248,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to pay
17 obligations that may arise out of contracts during Fiscal Year 2026
18 related to the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
20 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
21 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
23 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
