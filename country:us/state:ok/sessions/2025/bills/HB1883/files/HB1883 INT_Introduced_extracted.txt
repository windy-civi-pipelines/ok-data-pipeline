7 An Act relating to professions and occupations;
amending 59 O.S. 2021, Section 2095.6, as amended by
8 Section 4, Chapter 218, O.S.L. 2024 (59 O.S. Supp.
2024, Section 2095.6), which relates to mortgage
9 lender licensing and registration; modifying the
annual fee for mortgage broker licenses; and
10 providing an effective date.

14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 59 O.S. 2021, Section 2095.6, as
16 amended by Section 4, Chapter 218, O.S.L. 2024 (59 O.S. Supp. 2024,
17 Section 2095.6), is amended to read as follows:
18 Section 2095.6. A. Applicants for a license shall apply on a
19 form as prescribed by the Administrator of Consumer Credit.
20 B. In order to fulfill the purposes of the Oklahoma Secure and
21 Fair Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act, the Administrator is
22 authorized to establish relationships or contracts with the
23 Nationwide Multistate Licensing System and Registry or other
24 entities designated by the Nationwide Multistate Licensing System

1 and Registry to collect and maintain records and process transaction
2 fees or other fees related to licensees or other entities or
3 individuals subject to the Oklahoma Secure and Fair Enforcement for
4 Mortgage Licensing Act.
5 C. In connection with an application for licensing as a
6 mortgage loan originator, the applicant shall, at a minimum, furnish
7 to the Nationwide Multistate Licensing System and Registry
8 information concerning the applicant's identity including:
9 1. Fingerprints for submission to the Federal Bureau of
10 Investigation and any governmental agency or entity authorized to
11 receive such information for a state, national and international
12 criminal history background check; and
13 2. Personal history and experience in a form prescribed by the
14 Nationwide Multistate Licensing System and Registry and the
15 Administrator to obtain:
16 a. an independent credit report obtained from a consumer
17 reporting agency defined in 15 U.S.C., Section
18 1681a(p), and
19 b. information related to any administrative, civil or
20 criminal findings by any governmental jurisdiction.
21 D. In connection with an application for licensing as a
22 mortgage broker or mortgage lender, the applicant shall, at a
23 minimum, furnish to the Nationwide Multistate Licensing System and
24
Req. No. 12182 Page 2
1 Regi stry information concerning each owner, officer, director or
2 partner, as applicable including:
3 1. Fingerprints for submission to the Federal Bureau of
4 Investigation and any governmental agency or entity authorized to
5 receive such information for a state, national and international
6 criminal history background check; and
7 2. Personal history and experience in a form prescribed by the
8 Nationwide Multistate Licensing System and Registry and the
9 Administrator to obtain:
10 a. an independent credit report obtained from a consumer
11 reporting agency described in 15 U.S.C., Section
12 1681a(p), and
13 b. information related to any administrative, civil or
14 criminal findings by any governmental jurisdiction.
15 E. For purposes of this section and in order to reduce points
16 of contact which the Federal Bureau of Investigation may have to
17 maintain for purposes of paragraph 1 and subparagraph b of paragraph
18 2 of subsection D of this section, the Administrator may use the
19 Nationwide Multistate Licensing System and Registry as a channeling
20 agent for requesting information from and distributing information
21 to the United States Department of Justice or any governmental
22 agency.
23 F. For the purposes of this section and in order to reduce the
24 points of contact which the Administrator may have to maintain for
Req. No. 12182 Page 3
1 purp oses of subparagraphs a and b of paragraph 2 of subsection D of
2 this section, the Administrator may use the Nationwide Multistate
3 Licensing System and Registry as a channeling agent for requesting
4 and distributing information to and from any source so directed by
5 the Administrator.
6 G. A license issued under the Oklahoma Secure and Fair
7 Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act shall be valid for a period
8 of one (1) year, unless otherwise revoked or suspended by the
9 Administrator as provided in the Oklahoma Secure and Fair
10 Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act.
11 H. The Administrator, on determining that the applicant is
12 qualified and upon payment of the fees by the applicant, shall issue
13 a license to the applicant. An applicant who has been denied a
14 license may not reapply for the license for sixty (60) days from the
15 date of the previous application. A new license issued on or after
16 November 1 shall be effective through December 31 of the following
17 calendar year.
18 I. A licensee shall pay the renewal fee on or before December
19 1. If the license is not renewed by December 1, the licensee shall
20 pay a late renewal fee as prescribed by rule of the Commission on
21 Consumer Credit. Licenses not renewed by December 31 shall expire
22 and the licensee shall not act as a mortgage broker, mortgage lender
23 or mortgage loan originator until a new license is issued pursuant
24 to the Oklahoma Secure and Fair Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing
Req. No. 12182 Page 4
1 Act. A license shall not be granted to the holder of an expired
2 license except as provided in the Oklahoma Secure and Fair
3 Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act for the issuance of an
4 original license.
5 J. A licensee shall prominently display the mortgage broker,
6 mortgage lender or mortgage loan originator license in the principal
7 place of business of the mortgage broker, mortgage lender or
8 mortgage loan originator and any branch office of the mortgage
9 broker or mortgage lender.
10 K. 1. An applicant for a mortgage broker or mortgage lender
11 license shall pay a fee of One Thousand Two Hundred Dollars
12 ($1,200.00). This fee shall cover the application fee and
13 examination fee for all registered locations, including any changes
14 of address.
15 2. Mortgage broker or mortgage lender licenses may be renewed
16 by submitting an annual assessment fee of One Thousand Dollars
17 ($1,000.00). Mortgage lender licenses may be renewed by submitting
18 an annual assessment fee. The annual assessment fee shall:
19 a. be based on the dollar volume of loans originated for
20 residential real property located in Oklahoma during
21 the twelve-month period ending June 30,
22 b. be based on the dollar volume of loans serviced for

6 serviced in Oklahoma, and
7 d. cover:
8 (1) the renewal fee for the principal office and any
9 branches, and
10 (2) any examination-related costs incurred by the
11 Department of Consumer Credit.

18 c. more than Seventeen Thousand Five Hundred Dollars

9 9. A fee as prescribed by rule of the Commission on Consumer

6 Administrator shall notify licensees of an annual assessment fee
7 reduction prior to November 1 of the specific license renewal
8 period. An annual assessment fee does not include an initial
9 license fee for purposes of this subsection.

11 license shall pay a fee of Four Hundred Fifty Dollars ($450.00).

14 3. A late renewal fee shall be as prescribed by rule of the
15 Commission on Consumer Credit.
16 4. A fee as prescribed by rule of the Commission shall be paid
17 by applicants and licensees into the Oklahoma Mortgage Broker and

19 2095.20 of this title, for each initial application and each renewal
20 application.
