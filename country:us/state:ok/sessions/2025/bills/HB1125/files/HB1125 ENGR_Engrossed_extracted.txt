12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 69 O.S. 2021, Section 901, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 901. The A. In municipalities with a population of one
16 hundred thousand (100,000) or less, according to the latest Federal
17 Decennial Census, the Transportation Commission shall pay the cost
18 of or perform the act of constructing, improving or maintaining
19 municipal streets where such streets are a continuation of the state
20 or federal highway system and the cost of installation, repair and
21 maintenance of curbs, inlets, inlet grates, inlet hoods, and inlet
22 structures, signs, and lane marking for municipal streets where such
23 streets are a continuation of the state or federal highway system,
24 except when performing mill and inlay projects. The Commission
ENGR. H. B. NO. 1125 Page 1
1 shal l not pay the cost of mowing the right-of-way; stormwater
2 drainage systems and facilities outside the back of curb; or curbs,
3 sidewalks or driveways of any such street. When a municipality
4 performs construction or maintenance operations within the right-of-
5 way which affects or involves the removal of the surface, grade or
6 subgrade of the highway, the municipality shall, at the expense of
7 the municipality, restore the surface, grade or subgrade of the
8 highway in accordance with the standards and specifications set
9 forth by the Department of Transportation. If the municipality
10 fails to restore the surface, grade or subgrade of the highway in
11 accordance with the standards and specifications set forth by the
12 Department of Transportation, the Department of Transportation shall
13 restore the highway and charge the municipality for all expenses
14 associated therewith. The Commission may participate in or pay the
15 cost of lighting and electronic traffic control devices for
16 municipal streets where such streets are a continuation of the state
17 or federal highway system.
18 B. When the Department undertakes a project as described in
19 subsection A of this section, the Department shall bear the full
20 cost of installing, repairing, replacing, or otherwise maintaining
21 curbs, inlets, inlet grates, inlet hoods, and inlet structures as
22 part of the project scope. The Department shall amend all existing
23 administrative rules that exclude the installation, repair,
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 1125 Page 2
1 repl acement, or otherwise maintenance of curbs in the scope of such
2 projects.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
4 Passed the House of Representatives the 12th day of March, 2025.
