8 [ transportation – construction – streets –

13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 69 O.S. 2021, Section 901, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 901. The A. In municipalities with a population of one
17 hundred thousand (100,000) or less, according to the latest Federal
18 Decennial Census, the Transportation Commission shall pay the cost
19 of or perform the act of constructing, improving or maintaining
20 municipal streets where such streets are a continuation of the state
21 or federal highway system and the cost of installation, repair and
22 maintenance of curbs, inlets, inlet grates, inlet hoods, and inlet
23 structures, signs, and lane marking for municipal streets where such
24 streets are a continuation of the state or federal highway system,
Req. No. 13258 Page 1
1 exce pt when performing mill and inlay projects. The Commission
2 shall not pay the cost of mowing the right-of-way; stormwater
3 drainage systems and facilities outside the back of curb; or curbs,
4 sidewalks or driveways of any such street. When a municipality
5 performs construction or maintenance operations within the right-of-
6 way which affects or involves the removal of the surface, grade or
7 subgrade of the highway, the municipality shall, at the expense of
8 the municipality, restore the surface, grade or subgrade of the
9 highway in accordance with the standards and specifications set
10 forth by the Department of Transportation. If the municipality
11 fails to restore the surface, grade or subgrade of the highway in
12 accordance with the standards and specifications set forth by the
13 Department of Transportation, the Department of Transportation shall
14 restore the highway and charge the municipality for all expenses
15 associated therewith. The Commission may participate in or pay the
16 cost of lighting and electronic traffic control devices for
17 municipal streets where such streets are a continuation of the state
18 or federal highway system.
19 B. When the Department undertakes a project as described in
20 subsection A of this section, the Department shall bear the full
21 cost of installing, repairing, replacing, or otherwise maintaining
22 curbs, inlets, inlet grates, inlet hoods, and inlet structures as
23 part of the project scope. The Department shall amend all existing
24 administrative rules that exclude the installation, repair,
Req. No. 13258 Page 2
1 repl acement, or otherwise maintenance of curbs in the scope of such
2 projects.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
