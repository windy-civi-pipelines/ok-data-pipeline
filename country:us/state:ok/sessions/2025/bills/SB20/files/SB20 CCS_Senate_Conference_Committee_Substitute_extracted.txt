7 provided by law.

6 attachment.

8 complies with the provisions of subsection G of Section 6-101 of
9 this title whereby a person may apply for a renewal or replacement
10 Oklahoma Class D license or Oklahoma identification card.
11 B. 1. Service Oklahoma may issue or authorize the issuance of
12 a temporary permit or license to an applicant for a driver license
13 permitting such applicant to operate a motor vehicle while Service
14 Oklahoma is completing its investigation and determination of all

17 to the applicant. Such permit or license must be in the immediate
18 possession of the driver while operating a motor vehicle, and it

6 any of the following specific farm-related service industries:
7 a. farm retail outlets and suppliers,
8 b. agri-chemical businesses,
9 c. custom harvesters, and
10 d. livestock feeders.
11 The applicant shall have held a valid driver license for at
12 least one (1) year. Applicants with more than two (2) years of
13 driving experience shall have a good driving record for the most
14 recent two (2) year two-year period and shall meet all the
15 requirements for a commercial driver license. The restricted
16 commercial driver license shall not exceed the maximum total days
17 that federal law allows. Applicants for the restricted commercial
18 driver license shall be exempt from the knowledge and skills test.
19 Application of the restricted commercial driver license does not
20 have to be used in consecutive days. The use of the permit shall be
21 declared at application.

23 mean an applicant:
24 a. has not had more than one license,

4 disqualifying offenses or serious traffic violations,

7 local law relating to motor vehicle traffic control,
8 other than a parking violation, arising in connection
9 with any traffic accident and has no record of an
10 accident in which they are he or she is at fault.
11 3. The restricted commercial driver license shall not be valid
12 for operators of commercial motor vehicles beyond one hundred fifty
13 (150) miles from the place of business or the farm currently being
14 served. Such license shall be limited to Class B or C vehicles.
15 Holders of such licenses who transport hazardous materials which are
16 required to be placarded shall be limited to the following:
17 a. diesel fuel in quantities of one thousand (1,000)
18 gallons or less,
19 b. liquid fertilizers in vehicles with total capacities

21 c. solid fertilizers that are not mixed with any organic
22 substance.
23 No other placarded hazardous materials shall be transported by
24 holders of such licenses.

6 rules. The expiration of the issued license shall be valid until
7 the same date as the expiration of the visa for the non-domiciled
8 worker. Service Oklahoma may promulgate rules for the

17 valid passport.
