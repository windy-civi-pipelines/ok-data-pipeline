10 An Act relating to the State Election Board; creating
the State Election Board Voting System Revolving
11 Fund; authorizing budgeting and expenditure of funds
for certain purposes; providing for codification;
12 providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-107.3 of Title 26, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
20 for the State Election Board to be designated the “State Election
21 Board Voting System Revolving Fund”. The fund shall be a continuing
22 fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of
23 all monies received by the State Election Board from funds
24 appropriated by the Legislature provided for the purchase,

1 inst allation, maintenance, and security of a unitary, unified,
2 integrated system of election administration for this state as
3 described in Section 21-101 of Title 26 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
4 All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby
5 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the State Election
6 Board for the purpose provided for in this section. Expenditures
7 from the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State
8 Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with the
9 Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
10 approval and payment.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
12 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
13 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
14 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
15 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
