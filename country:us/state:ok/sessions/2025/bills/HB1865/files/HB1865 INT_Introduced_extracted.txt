10 marking of ballots for persons with blindness, disability, or
11 inability to read or write as provided in subsection A of this

13 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 7-104, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 7-104. A. At every Primary, Runoff Primary and General
16 Election and any other election conducted by a county election
17 board, each polling place in the state shall open at 7:00 a.m. and
18 shall remain open continuously until 7:00 p.m., and every registered
19 voter of a precinct who presents himself between said hours shall be
20 entitled to vote, as provided by law, provided further, all
21 qualified voters who are in line waiting to vote at 7:00 p.m. shall
22 be allowed to vote.
23 B. 1. A registered voter wishing to vote on election day shall
24 appear at the polling place for his or her assigned precinct.
Req. No. 11688 Page 2
1 2. Neither the State Election Board nor any county election
2 board shall utilize vote centers for voting on election day. For
3 the purposes of this section, "vote center" means a place where any
4 voter in the entire jurisdiction can vote on election day,
5 regardless of the voter's precinct assignment. Provided, an
6 exception to this requirement may be authorized for a county
7 election board by the Secretary of the State Election Board in the
8 event of an emergency as described in Section 22-101 of this title.
9 The emergency use of a countywide vote center must be limited to the
10 precincts directly impacted by the emergency.
11 3. Nothing in this subsection shall prohibit the use of
12 subprecincts as described in Section 3-119 of this title, nor shall
13 it prohibit the assignment of precinct officials to serve more than
14 one precinct, if authorized by law, if the precincts share the same
15 election day polling place.
16 C. If any provision of federal law specifies hours for voting
17 in federal elections, the Secretary of the State Election Board
18 shall direct the county election boards to allow voting in all
19 elections held on the same day as such federal elections during the
20 hours specified by federal law.
21 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
22 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
23 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
