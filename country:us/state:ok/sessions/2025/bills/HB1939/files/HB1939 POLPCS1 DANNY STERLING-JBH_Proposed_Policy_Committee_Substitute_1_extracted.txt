17 that they may file protests against the issuance of the bonds and be
18 present at the hearing and contest the legality thereof. Such
19 notice shall be published one time not less than ten (10) forty-five
20 (45) days prior to the date named for the hearing and the hearing
21 may be adjourned from time to time in the discretion of the Court.
22 If the Court shall be satisfied that the bonds have been properly
23 authorized in accordance with this article and that when issued,
24 they will constitute valid obligations in accordance with their
