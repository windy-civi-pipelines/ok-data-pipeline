in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 6-186.1 of Title 70, unless

5
6
TeachForwardOK.
6
7

7
8
the Commission shall issue a request for proposals to select
8
9
technical assistance providers to evaluate teacher education
9
10
programs in institutions of higher education within The Oklahoma
10
11
State System of Higher Education that are accredited by the
11
12
Commission pursuant to Section 6-186 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma
12
13
Statutes.
13
14

14
15

17
18

18
19
applicant teacher education programs. Grants shall be used to

20

20
21
Commission pursuant to subsection B of this section. The provider
21
