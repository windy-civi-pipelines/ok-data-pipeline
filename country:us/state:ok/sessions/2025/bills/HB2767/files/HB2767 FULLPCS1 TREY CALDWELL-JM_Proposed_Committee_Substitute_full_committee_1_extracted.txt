10 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to the ROA-25 Revolving Fund;
requiring transfer from certain fund to the ROA-25
12 Revolving Fund; authorizing certain transfer to the
ROA-25 Revolving Fund; establishing amounts;
13 providing certain budgeting and expenditure
requirements; and declaring an emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. TRANSFER The State Treasurer shall transfer to
18 the ROA-25 Revolving Fund created pursuant to Enrolled House Bill
19 No. 2781 of the 1st Regular Session of the 60th Oklahoma
20 Legislature, from the Perform Fund created in Section 3646.5 of
21 Title 68 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of One Hundred Eighty
22 Million Dollars ($180,000,000.00) to be budgeted and expended in
23 accordance with law.
24
Req. No. 13742 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. The Oklahoma Department of Commerce is hereby
2 authorized to transfer to the ROA-25 Revolving Fund created pursuant
3 to Enrolled House Bill No. 2781 of the 1st Regular Session of the
4 60th Oklahoma Legislature, from the monies appropriated to the
5 Oklahoma Department of Commerce by Section 1 of Senate Bill 1179 of
6 the 1st Session of the 59th Oklahoma Legislature the sum of Seventy-
7 five Million Dollars ($75,000,000.00) to be budgeted and expended in
8 accordance with law.
9 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
11 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
