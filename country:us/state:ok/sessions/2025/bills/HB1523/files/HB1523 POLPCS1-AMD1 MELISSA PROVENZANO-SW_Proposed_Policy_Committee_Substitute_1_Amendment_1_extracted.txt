COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend amendment HB1523 POLPCS1
Page Section Lines
Of the Engrossed Bill
Page 2, Section 1, Line 6: By inserting after "telehealth" and before
"program" the word "pilot";
Page 2, Section 1, Lines 18 through 24 and Page 3, Section 1, Lines 1 and
2: By deleting paragraph 1 in its entirety and by inserting in lieu
thereof the following language: "1.'Health benefit plan' means a health
benefit plan as defined pursuant to Section 6060.4 of Title 36 of the
Oklahoma Statutes";
Page 4, Section 1, Lines 23 to 24: By deleting "one hundred thousand
(100,000)" and inserting in lieu thereof "twenty-five thousand (25,000)";
Page 5, Section 1, Line 17: By inserting after the period the following
language: "8. Demonstrate the ability to obtain the necessary required
parental consent prior to providing services.";
Page 6, Section 1, Line 11: By inserting after the period the following
language: "6. Obtain parental consent prior to providing services.";
Page 6, Section 1, Line 13: By inserting after "selected" and before "by"
the following language: "upon application by the districts";
Page 6, Section 1, Line 14: By inserting after the period and before "the"
the following language: "Subject to the availability of funding,";
Page 9, Section 1, Line 9: By deleting "Twenty-two dollars ($22.00)" and
inserting in lieu thereof "Fifteen dollars ($15,00)";
And by updating internal references and modifying the outline to conform.
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Melissa
Provenzano
