1 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 43A O.S. 2021, Section 12-105, is
2 amended to read as follows:
3 Section 12-105. A. The Department of Mental Health and
4 Substance Abuse Services shall act as the lead agency in
5 implementing the provisions of the Suicide Prevention Act in
6 cooperation with the Oklahoma Suicide Prevention Council.
7 B. The Department shall have primary responsibility for
8 evaluating the effectiveness of efforts designed to reduce the
9 number of attempted suicides and suicides, and shall electronically
10 submit an evaluation report to the Chair of the Oklahoma Suicide
11 Prevention Council, the Speaker of the House of Representatives, the
12 President Pro Tempore of the Senate, and the Governor no later than
13 July 1 of each year.
