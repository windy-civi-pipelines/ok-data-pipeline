I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1078, as follows:
1. On page 1, line 14, before the word “Notwithstanding”, inserting the letter “A.”
2. On page 2, line 13 1/2, adding new subsections to say:
“B. If, after the time of sale, the Environmental Protection Agency cancels the registration for the
pesticide based on its findings that the registrant intentionally withheld from or misrepresented
human health or safety information that was required to be submitted under the Federal Insecticide,
Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act, subsection A of this section shall not apply.
C. This act shall not apply to a manufacturer or seller of an American-manufactured pesticide that is
an adversarial nation to the United States of America pursuant to 15 C.F.R, Part 791.4.”
Amend title to conform
