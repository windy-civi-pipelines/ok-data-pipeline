Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to combined pesticide law;
establishing pesticide warning labels to be
9 sufficient under law when certain circumstances are
met; providing circumstances in which warning label
10 is adequate; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-97 of Title 2, unless there is
16 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 Notwithstanding any provision to the contrary, for any pesticide
18 registered with the United States Environmental Protection Agency
19 under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act, 7
20 U.S.C., Section 136 et seq., the label approved by the United States
21 Environmental Protection Agency in registering the pesticide, or a
22 label consistent with the most recent human health assessment
23 performed under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide
24 Act, or a label consistent with the United States Environmental
Req. No. 1625 Page 1
1 Prot ection Agency carcinogenicity classification for the pesticide
2 under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act, shall
3 be sufficient to satisfy any requirements for a warning regarding
4 health or safety under Oklahoma state law, including, without
5 limitation, the duty to warn, or any other common law duty to warn
6 if:
7 1. Approved by the Environmental Protection Agency under the
8 Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act and by the
9 Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry at the time
10 of sale;
11 2. Consistent with the most recent human health assessment
12 performed by Environmental Protection Agency under the Federal
13 Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act at the time of sale; or
14 3. Consistent with the Environmental Protection Agency
15 carcinogenicity classification for the pesticide under the Federal
16 Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act at the time of sale.
17 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
