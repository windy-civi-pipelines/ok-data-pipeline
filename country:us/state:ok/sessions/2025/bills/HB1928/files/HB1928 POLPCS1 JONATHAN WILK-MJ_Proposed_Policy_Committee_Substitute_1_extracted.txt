9 An Act relating to professional entities; amending 18
O.S. 2021, Section 803, as amended by Section 1,
10 Chapter 120, O.S.L. 2024 (18 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section
803), which relates to definition used in the
11 Professional Entity Act; modifying the definition of
professional service; and providing an effective
12 date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 18 O.S. 2021, Section 803, as
17 amended by Section 1, Chapter 120, O.S.L. 2024 (18 O.S. Supp. 2024,
18 Section 803), is amended to read as follows:
19 Section 803. A. As used in the Professional Entity Act, unless
20 the context clearly indicates that a different meaning is intended:
21 1. "Associated act" means the Oklahoma General Corporation Act,
22 in the case of a corporation; the Uniform Limited Partnership Act of
23 2010, in the case of a limited partnership; or the Oklahoma Limited
24 Liability Company Act, in the case of a limited liability company;
Req. No. 12274 Page 1
1 2. "Interest" means a share of stock in a corporation, a
2 partnership interest in a limited partnership or a membership
3 interest in a limited liability company;
4 3. "Owner" means a shareholder in the case of a corporation, a
5 general or limited partner in the case of a limited partnership or a
6 member in the case of a limited liability company;
7 4. "Manager" means a director or officer in the case of a
8 corporation, a general partner in the case of a limited partnership
9 or a manager in the case of a limited liability company;
10 5. "Professional entity" means a domestic or qualified foreign
11 corporation, limited partnership or limited liability company formed
12 for the purpose of rendering professional service or formed for the
13 purpose of owning a professional entity rendering professional
14 service;
15 6. "Professional service" means the personal service rendered
16 by:

1 9. "Individual", "incorporator" and "shareholder" each include
2 the trustee of an express trust created by a person duly licensed or
3 otherwise permitted to render a professional service who has the
4 right to revoke the trust and who is serving as the trustee of the
5 trust. Any certificate required by the Professional Entity Act to
6 be issued to an individual incorporator or shareholder may be issued
7 to the grantor on behalf of a trust. All references in the
8 Professional Entity Act to death and incapacity of a shareholder
9 shall include the death and incapacity of the grantor of a trust
10 which own stock in a professional corporation;
11 10. "Incapacity" of a shareholder means a determination by a
12 court of competent jurisdiction, or otherwise by two independent
13 licensed physicians, that the shareholder is fully incapacitated or
14 is partially incapacitated to the extent that the shareholder is not
15 capable of rendering the professional service for which the
16 professional corporation was organized; and
17 11. "Other personal representative" includes the successor
18 trustee of an express trust owning stock in a professional
19 corporation, which trust was created by a person duly licensed or
20 otherwise permitted to render the professional service for which the
21 professional corporation was organized who has the right to revoke
22 the trust and who is the original trustee of the trust.
