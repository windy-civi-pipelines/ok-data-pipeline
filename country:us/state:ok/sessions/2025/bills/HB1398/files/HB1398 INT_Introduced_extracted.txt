Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to county assessors; requiring cities
and towns transmit copies of building permits issued
8 to county assessors; providing timeframe; directing
for electronic copies where feasible; providing for
9 codification; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 301 of Title 19, unless there is
15 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. Cities and towns shall transmit a copy of any building
17 permit issued within its boundaries to the county assessor no later
18 than thirty (30) days after the date of issuance.
19 B. Cities and towns shall provide the county assessor with
20 electronic copies of building permits, where available and feasible.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
