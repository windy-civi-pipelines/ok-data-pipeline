FOR

7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to securitization revenue stream of
certain obligations issued by the Oklahoma
9 Development Finance Authority pursuant to the
February 2021 Regulated Utility Consumer Protection
10 Act; and pursuant to the February 2021 Unregulated
Utility Consumer Protection Act; making legislative
11 findings with regard to matters of statewide concern;
imposing restriction on certain municipalities with
12 respect to franchise fees; imposing restriction on
certain municipalities with respect to locally
13 imposed taxes; providing for noncodification;
providing for codification; and declaring an
14 emergency.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
19 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
20 The Legislature finds that it had the authority to enact the
21 provisions of the "February 2021 Regulated Utility Consumer
22 Protection Act" (hereinafter "the Act"). The Legislature finds that
23 cities and towns of the state have imposed certain franchise fees on
24 the securitization revenue stream which is required for defeasance
Req. No. 12930 Page 1
1 of t he obligations issued by the Oklahoma Development Finance
2 Authority pursuant to the Act. The Legislature finds that the
3 purpose of the Act was a matter of statewide concern and not a
4 matter of purely local or municipal concern. The Legislature finds
5 that certain municipalities have imposed franchise fees on the
6 securitization revenue stream contrary to legislative intent. The
7 Legislature finds that municipalities have imposed certain local
8 taxes upon the securitization revenue stream contrary to legislative
9 intent.
10 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
11 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 9083 of Title 74, unless there
12 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
13 A. No municipality shall impose any franchise fee upon or with
14 respect to the securitization revenue stream required pursuant to
15 the provisions of the Act, any bond indenture related to the
16 obligations issued by the Oklahoma Development Finance Authority
17 pursuant to the Act, or any order of the Oklahoma Corporation
18 Commission authorizing an affected public utility to impose charges
19 which constitute the securitization revenue stream to defease the
20 obligations issued by the Oklahoma Development Finance Authority
21 pursuant to the Act.
22 B. The provisions of this section shall be applicable to the
23 securitization revenue stream for bonds or other obligations issued
24 by the Oklahoma Development Finance Authority as described in
Req. No. 12930 Page 2
1 subs ection A of this section and shall also be applicable to the
2 securitization revenue stream for any bonds or other obligations
3 issued by the Oklahoma Development Finance Authority on or after the
4 effective date of this act for a purpose similar to the obligations
5 issued by the Oklahoma Development Finance Authority pursuant to the
6 Act.
7 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
8 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 9084 of Title 74, unless there
9 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
10 A. No municipality shall impose any sales tax, use tax or any
11 other tax authorized pursuant to the provisions of Section 2701 et
12 seq. of the Title 68 of the Oklahoma Statutes on or with respect to
13 the securitization revenue stream for bonds or other obligations
14 issued by the Oklahoma Development Finance Authority as described in
15 subsection A of Section 2 of this act.
16 B. The provisions of this section shall be applicable to the
17 securitization revenue stream for bonds or other obligations issued
18 by the Oklahoma Development Finance Authority pursuant to the Act
19 and shall also be applicable to the securitization revenue stream
20 for any bonds or other obligations issued by the Oklahoma
21 Development Finance Authority on or after the effective date of this
22 act for a purpose similar to the obligations issued by the Oklahoma
23 Development Finance Authority pursuant to the Act.
