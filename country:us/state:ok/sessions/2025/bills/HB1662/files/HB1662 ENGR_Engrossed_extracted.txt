11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 180.14 of Title 17, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 The Oklahoma Corporation Commission is hereby required to
16 prepare and submit an annual report detailing all activities and
17 actions undertaken by any representative of the Commission in
18 dealings with the Southwest Power Pool. The report shall include,
19 but shall not be limited to:
20 1. A detailed account of all trips taken by Commission
21 representatives in connection with matters involving the Southwest
22 Power Pool, including the purpose, location, and costs of such
23 trips;
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 1662 Page 1
1 2. A record of all meetings attended by Commission
2 representatives with the Southwest Power Pool or its
3 representatives, including dates, locations, and subject matters
4 discussed; and
5 3. A comprehensive summary of all votes taken by Commission
6 representatives on matters related to the Southwest Power Pool,
7 including the nature and outcomes of such votes.
8 The report shall cover all relevant activities undertaken during
9 the preceding calendar year and shall be completed and submitted no
10 later than January 31 of each year to the Speaker of the Oklahoma
11 House of Representatives and the President Pro Tempore of the
12 Oklahoma State Senate.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
14 Passed the House of Representatives the 12th day of March, 2025.
