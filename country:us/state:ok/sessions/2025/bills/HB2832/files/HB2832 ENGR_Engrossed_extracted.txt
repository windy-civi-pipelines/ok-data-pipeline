12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 69 O.S. 2021, Section 1600.1, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 1600.1. A. Highways, bridges and other facilities
16 which are part of the state highway system may be considered for
17 dedication in honor of individuals deserving of commendation for
18 their active involvement in the project or for outstanding service
19 to the nation, this state or their community. Proposals for the
20 dedication of state highway facilities should specifically state the
21 accomplishments upon which the proposal is based.
22 B. The Oklahoma Department of Transportation shall maintain a
23 digital map identifying all designations approved under the Memorial
24 Highways and Bridges Program and shall be made available on the
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2832 Page 1
1 agen cy website. This map shall provide the name of the honorees,
2 the location and description of the segment of highway, bridge, or
3 facility being named, and the reasons for designation, including any
4 historical significance. Upon the availability of funding, the map
5 shall be upgraded with interactive multimedia capabilities and other
6 enhancements to be shared with the public through a web-based
7 mapping application so that the public can learn more about the
8 individuals or groups being honored. Upon its availability, a link
9 to the enhanced mapping system shall be shared with the Oklahoma
10 Tourism and Recreation Department to promote the State of Oklahoma.
11 C. All military members and groups who have been honored in the
12 Memorial Highways and Bridges Program shall be identified in the
13 Veterans Military Trail Segment which shall be specified on the
14 interactive map.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
16 Passed the House of Representatives the 12th day of March, 2025.
