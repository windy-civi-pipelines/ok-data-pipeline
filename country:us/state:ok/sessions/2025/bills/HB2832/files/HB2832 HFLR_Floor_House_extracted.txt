9 AS INTRODUCED
10 An Act relating to transportation; amending 69 O.S.
2021, Section 1600.1, which relates to dedicating
11 state highway system property to honor individuals;
establishing procedures for making dedication to
12 military and veteran individuals; and providing an
effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 69 O.S. 2021, Section 1600.1, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 1600.1. A. Highways, bridges and other facilities
19 which are part of the state highway system may be considered for
20 dedication in honor of individuals deserving of commendation for
21 their active involvement in the project or for outstanding service
22 to the nation, this state or their community. Proposals for the
23 dedication of state highway facilities should specifically state the
24 accomplishments upon which the proposal is based.
HB2832 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 B. The Oklahoma Department of Transportation shall maintain a
2 digital map identifying all designations approved under the Memorial
3 Highways and Bridges Program and shall be made available on the
4 agency website. This map shall provide the name of the honorees,
5 the location and description of the segment of highway, bridge, or
6 facility being named, and the reasons for designation, including any
7 historical significance. Upon the availability of funding, the map
8 shall be upgraded with interactive multimedia capabilities and other
9 enhancements to be shared with the public through a web-based
10 mapping application so that the public can learn more about the
11 individuals or groups being honored. Upon its availability, a link
12 to the enhanced mapping system shall be shared with the Oklahoma
13 Department of Tourism and Recreation to promote the State of
14 Oklahoma.
15 C. All military members and groups who have been honored in the
16 Memorial Highways and Bridges Program shall be identified in the
17 Veterans Military Trail Segment which shall be specified on the
18 interactive map.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
20
21 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON COMMERCE AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT
OVERSIGHT, dated 03/04/2025 - DO PASS, As Coauthored.
