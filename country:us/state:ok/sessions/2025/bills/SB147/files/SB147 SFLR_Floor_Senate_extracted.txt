9 An Act relating to administration of elections;
amending 26 O.S. 2021, Section 3-130, which relates
10 to post-election audits; clarifying language;
requiring Secretary of the State Election Board to
11 direct all counties to conduct post-election audits
for specified elections; modifying definition;
12 updating statutory language; and providing an
effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 3-130, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 3-130. A. The Secretary of the State Election Board
19 shall have the authority to direct the secretary of a county
20 election board to conduct a post-election audit of election results
21 for any election, for the purpose of maintaining the security of the
22 election system by ensuring that voting devices and software used in
23 a particular election correctly tabulated votes. The Secretary
24 shall direct the secretary of the county election board in each
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB147 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 coun ty to conduct a post-election audit following elections held on
2 dates set forth in Sections 1-101, 1-102, 1-103, and 20-101 of this
3 title.
4 B. The method, timing, and procedures for conducting a post-
5 election audit shall be determined by the Secretary of the State
6 Election Board.
7 C. The secretary of a county election board shall report the
8 findings of a post-election audit to the Secretary of the State
9 Election Board, and such report shall be available to the public.
10 D. The Secretary of the State Election Board may promulgate
11 policies, rules, and procedures to implement the requirements of
12 this section.
13 E. For the purposes of this title, a “post-election audit” is
14 defined as means a manual or electronic examination of a limited
15 number of ballots by a secretary of a county election board or other
16 authorized election officials following an election. A post-
17 election audit shall be conducted only at the direction of the
18 Secretary of the State Election Board and only subject to the
19 Secretary’s previously promulgated prescribed policies, rules, or
20 procedures.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
