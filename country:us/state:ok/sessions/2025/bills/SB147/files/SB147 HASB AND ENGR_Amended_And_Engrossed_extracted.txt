8 An Act relating to administration of elections;
amending 26 O.S. 2021, Section 3-130, which relates
9 to post-election audits; clarifying language;
requiring Secretary of the State Election Board to
10 direct all counties to conduct post-election audits
for specified elections; modifying definition;
11 updating statutory language; and providing an
effective date.

15 AUTHORS: Remove Representative Newton as principal House author and
substitute with Representative Hall

Add the following House Coauthor: Newton

and insert:

21 "An Act relating to administration of elections;
amending 26 O.S. 2021, Section 3-130, which relates
22 to post-election audits; clarifying language;
requiring Secretary of the State Election Board to
23 direct all counties to conduct post-election audits
for specified elections; modifying definition;

3
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
5 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 3-130, is
6 amended to read as follows:
7 Section 3-130. A. The Secretary of the State Election Board
8 shall have the authority to direct the secretary of a county
9 election board to conduct a post-election audit of election results
10 for any election, for the purpose of maintaining the security of the
11 election system by ensuring that voting devices and software used in
12 a particular election correctly tabulated votes. The Secretary
13 shall direct the secretary of the county election board in each
14 county to conduct a post-election audit following elections held on
15 dates set forth in Sections 1-101, 1-102, 1-103, and 20-101 of this
16 title.
17 B. The method, timing, and procedures for conducting a post-
18 election audit shall be determined by the Secretary of the State
19 Election Board.
20 C. The secretary of a county election board shall report the
21 findings of a post-election audit to the Secretary of the State
22 Election Board, and such report shall be available to the public.
23
24
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 147 Page 2
1 D. The Secretary of the State Election Board may promulgate
2 policies, rules, and procedures to implement the requirements of
3 this section.
4 E. For the purposes of this title, a “post-election audit” is
5 defined as means a manual or electronic examination of a limited
6 number of ballots by a secretary of a county election board or other
7 authorized election officials following an election. A post-
8 election audit shall be conducted only at the direction of the
9 Secretary of the State Election Board and only subject to the
10 Secretary’s previously promulgated prescribed policies, rules, or
11 procedures.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025."
13 Passed the House of Representatives the 5th day of May, 2025.
