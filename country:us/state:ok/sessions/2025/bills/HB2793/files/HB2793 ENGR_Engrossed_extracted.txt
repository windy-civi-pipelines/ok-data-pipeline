8 An Act relating to the Emergency Medicine Revolving
Fund; making an appropriation; identifying the
9 source; establishing an amount; providing for
noncodification; providing an effective date; and
10 declaring an emergency.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 There is hereby appropriated from any monies not otherwise
17 appropriated from the Progressing Rural Economic Prosperity Fund of
18 the State Treasury created in Section 256 of Title 62 of the
19 Oklahoma Statutes the sum of Eight Million Dollars ($8,000,000.00)
20 to the Emergency Medicine Revolving Fund created pursuant to
21 Enrolled House Bill No. 2784 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma
22 Legislature.
23 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
