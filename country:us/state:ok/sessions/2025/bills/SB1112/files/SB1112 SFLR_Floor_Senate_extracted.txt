February 24, 2025
2
4
5
6 An Act relating to lodging tax; excluding discounted
or comped rooms or lodging from gross receipts upon
7 which a lodging tax is levied; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1370.11 of Title 68, unless
13 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 For any county lodging tax levied pursuant to Section 1370.9 of
15 Title 68 of the Oklahoma Statutes and any municipal lodging tax
16 levied upon the effective date of this act, the gross receipts or
17 gross proceeds upon which the tax is levied shall not include any
18 discounts or the provision of lodging or rooms at no charge to the
19 customer or employee when the seller receives no consideration or
20 reimbursement by a third party.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective January 1, 2026.
22 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON REVENUE AND TAXATION
February 24, 2025 - DO PASS
