1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to firefighters; prohibiting certain
mandatory volunteer or rural firefighter training;
9 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 325.5 of Title 74, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 No volunteer or rural firefighter shall be required to attend
17 any state or county mandatory training.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
