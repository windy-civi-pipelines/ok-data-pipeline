8 PROPOSED OVERSIGHT COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to firefighters; prohibiting
mandatory volunteer or rural firefighter training;
10 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 325.5 of Title 74, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 No volunteer or rural firefighter shall be required to attend
18 any mandatory training.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
