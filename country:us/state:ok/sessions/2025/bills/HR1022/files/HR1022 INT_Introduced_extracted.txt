RESOLUTION 1022 By: Munson
4
5

7 AS INTRODUCED
Lung Cancer Action Week.
9
10
11 WHEREAS, the American Lung Association's LUNG FORCE initiative
12 has been working to ensure that fewer people are diagnosed with lung
13 cancer and those living with the disease live longer since 2024; and
14 WHEREAS, lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer deaths; and
15 WHEREAS, lung cancer screening saves lives, and advocacy and
16 increased awareness will result in more high-risk individuals
17 getting screened; and
18 WHEREAS, in Oklahoma, only 9% of those at high risk receive a
19 lung cancer screening; and
20 WHEREAS, public support for research funding will result in new
21 treatments and better methods of early detection; and
22 WHEREAS, LUNG FORCE is a national initiative led by the American
23 Lung Association to defeat lung cancer.
24
Req. No. 13617 Page 1
1 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
2 OF THE 1ST SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
3 THAT the Oklahoma House of Representatives hereby designates the
4 second week in May as Lung Cancer Action Week throughout the state
5 and encourages all residents of Oklahoma to learn more about lung
6 cancer, risk factors and early detection, and take action.
