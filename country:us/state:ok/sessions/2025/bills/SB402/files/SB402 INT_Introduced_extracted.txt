7 An Act relating to commercial driver training;
7 amending 47 O.S. 2021, Section 804, as amended by
8 Section 28, Chapter 310, O.S.L. 2023 (47 O.S. Supp.
8 2024, Section 804), which relates to instructor’s
9 licenses; directing Service Oklahoma to promulgate
9 certain rules; and declaring an emergency.
10
10
11
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 47 O.S. 2021, Section 804, as
13
14 amended by Section 28, Chapter 310, O.S.L. 2023 (47 O.S. Supp. 2024,
14
15 Section 804), is amended to read as follows:
15
16 Section 804. A. No person shall act as an instructor unless
16
17 such person applies for and obtains from Service Oklahoma a license
17
18 in the manner and form prescribed by Service Oklahoma.
18
19 B. Rules promulgated by Service Oklahoma, in conjunction with
19
20 the Department of Public Safety, shall state the requirements for an
20
21 instructor’s license, including requirements concerning moral
21
22 character, physical condition, knowledge of the courses of
22
23 instruction, motor vehicle laws and safety principles and practices,
23
24 previous personnel and employment records, and such other matters as
24
Req. No. 611 Page 1
1 Serv ice Oklahoma, in conjunction with the Department of Public
1
2 Safety, may prescribe for the protection of the public.
2
3 C. Rules promulgated by Service Oklahoma under the provisions
3
4 of subsection B of this section shall require an applicant for the
4
5 license to have three (3) years of verifiable commercial driving
5
6 experience within ten (10) years of the application date or three
6
7 (3) years of verifiable experience as an instructor at a commercial
7
8 driver training school within ten (10) years of the application
8
9 date.
9
10 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10
11 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
11
12 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12
13 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
