Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to transportation; requiring Oklahoma
Department of Transportation consult with State
8 Legislature on certain plan priorities; requiring
Department make certain presentation; providing for
9 codification; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4014 of Title 69, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 In addition to all other sources of information used when
17 updating the Oklahoma Department of Transportation's Eight-Year
18 Construction Work Plan, the Department shall consult with the
19 members of the State Legislature regarding construction priorities
20 for the Plan. The consultation process shall include, but may not
21 be limited to, a presentation of the Plan to relevant legislative
22 committees allowing an opportunity for legislators to provide
23 feedback, recommendations, and priorities from their districts for
24 consideration in the plan.
Req. No. 11108 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
