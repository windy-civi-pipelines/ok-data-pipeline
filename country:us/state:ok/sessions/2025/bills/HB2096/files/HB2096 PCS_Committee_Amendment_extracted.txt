11 An Act relating to wildlife; authorizing the
Department of Wildlife Conservation to establish the
12 Deer Management Assistance Program Plus (DMAP+);
allowing for voluntary participation in DMAP+ by
13 certain individuals; requiring the Department to
prepare certain forms and application for
14 participation; establishing criteria for
participation; prohibiting individuals from certain
15 provisions; establishing fees; requiring participants
to submit certain report to the Department by certain
16 time; providing for promulgation of rules; providing
for codification; and declaring an emergency.
17
18
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
22 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-316 of Title 29, unless there
23 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
24
Req. No. 1980 Page 1
1 A. The Department of Wildlife Conservation is hereby authorized
2 to establish the Deer Management Assistance Program Plus (DMAP+).
3 DMAP+ shall be a voluntary wildlife management program administered
4 by the Department to provide private landowners, hunting clubs, or
5 lease operators with an extended deer gun season and a regulated
6 harvest quota system aimed at improving deer herd structure, habitat
7 sustainability, and overall population management. Individuals
8 participating in this program shall be required to adhere to
9 property-specific harvest plans established by state biologists,
10 comply with quota-based harvest limitations, and pay an enrollment
11 fee as provided for in this section.
12 B. The Department shall provide all necessary forms and
13 applications for individuals seeking to participate in DMAP+.
14 Individuals participating in DMAP+ shall:
15 1. Own or manage no fewer than one thousand (1,000) contiguous
16 acres;
17 2. Submit a completed application to the Department for
18 enrollment; and
19 3. Comply with all management guidelines as determined by the
20 Department.
21 C. DMAP+ shall allow for an extended deer gun season for
22 enrolled properties, as follows:
23 1. Antlered deer rifle season beginning November 1 and ending
24 December 31 each year; and
Req. No. 1980 Page 2
1 2. Antlerless deer rifle season beginning October 1 and ending
2 January 15 of the following year.
3 D. Each DMAP+ property shall be subject to certain harvest
4 requirements as established by the Department. Individuals who
5 exceed the established harvest requirements shall be in violation of
6 this subsection. Violations are subject to penalties as prescribed
7 by the Department.
8 E. The annual fee for participation in DMAP+ shall be as
9 follows:
10 1. For properties between one thousand (1,000) acres and five
11 thousand (5,000) acres – One Thousand Five Hundred Dollars
12 ($1,500.00); and
13 2. For properties exceeding five thousand (5,000) acres – Two
14 Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($2,500.00).
15 F. Landowners participating in DMAP+ shall electronically
16 submit an annual report to the Department detailing the number, age,
17 and sex of deer harvested on the enrolled property. Failure to
18 submit the report within thirty (30) days of the conclusion of each
19 designated season may result in suspension or removal from DMAP+
20 participation.
21 G. The Department shall promulgate all rules necessary for the
22 implementation of this section.
23 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
24 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
Req. No. 1980 Page 3
1 decl ared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
2 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
