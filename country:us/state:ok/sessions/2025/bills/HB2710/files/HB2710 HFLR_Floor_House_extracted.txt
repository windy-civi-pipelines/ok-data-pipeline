18 oversee the operations of the Oklahoma 9-1-1 Office and the Oklahoma
19 Emergency Communications Office. The Executive Coordinator shall
20 serve as the Executive Coordinator of both Offices and shall be
21 responsible for implementing the policies and directives of the
22 respective Authorities. The Board shall establish guidelines for
23 the evaluation and performance review of the Executive Coordinator.
