11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Prison
13 Acquisition Revolving Fund created in Enrolled House Bill No. 2780
14 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, from any monies
15 not otherwise appropriated from the Revenue Stabilization Fund of
16 the State Treasury, the sum of Two Hundred Thirty-eight Million
17 Seventy-seven Thousand Four Hundred Sixty-two Dollars
18 ($238,077,462.00) for expenditure by law.

20 Acquisition Revolving Fund created in Enrolled House Bill No. 2780
21 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, from any monies
22 not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of the
23 State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2023, the sum of
