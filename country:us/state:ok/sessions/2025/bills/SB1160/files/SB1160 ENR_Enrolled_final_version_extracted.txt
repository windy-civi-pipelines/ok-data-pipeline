Section 1:
ENROLLED SENATE
BILL NO. 1160 By: Hall and Haste of the
Senate
and
Caldwell (Trey) and Kane of
the House
An Act relating to the Prison Acquisition Revolving
Fund; making appropriations; stating purpose; and
declaring an emergency.
SUBJECT: Prison Acquisition Revolving Fund
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Prison
Acquisition Revolving Fund created in Enrolled House Bill No. 2780
of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, from any monies
not otherwise appropriated from the Revenue Stabilization Fund of
the State Treasury, the sum of Two Hundred Thirty-eight Million
Seventy-seven Thousand Four Hundred Sixty-two Dollars
($238,077,462.00) for expenditure by law.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. There is hereby appropriated to the Prison
Acquisition Revolving Fund created in Enrolled House Bill No. 2780
of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, from any monies
not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of the
State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2023, the sum of
Seventy-three Million Nine Hundred Twenty-two Thousand Five Hundred
Thirty-eight Dollars ($73,922,538.00) for expenditure by law.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
Passed the Senate the 20th day of May, 2025.
Presiding Officer of the Senate
Passed the House of Representatives the 22nd day of May, 2025.

An Act relating to the Prison Acquisition Revolving
Fund; making appropriations; stating purpose; and
declaring an emergency.
SUBJECT: Prison Acquisition Revolving Fund
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Prison
Acquisition Revolving Fund created in Enrolled House Bill No. 2780
of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, from any monies
not otherwise appropriated from the Revenue Stabilization Fund of
the State Treasury, the sum of Two Hundred Thirty-eight Million
Seventy-seven Thousand Four Hundred Sixty-two Dollars
($238,077,462.00) for expenditure by law.
SECTION 2. There is hereby appropriated to the Prison
Acquisition Revolving Fund created in Enrolled House Bill No. 2780
of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, from any monies
not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of the
State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2023, the sum of
Seventy-three Million Nine Hundred Twenty-two Thousand Five Hundred
Thirty-eight Dollars ($73,922,538.00) for expenditure by law.
SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
