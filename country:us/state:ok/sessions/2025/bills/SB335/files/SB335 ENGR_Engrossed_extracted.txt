6 An Act relating to retail electric suppliers;
prohibiting certain incentivization by suppliers to
7 customers; providing for codification; providing an
effective date; and declaring an emergency.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 801.10 of Title 17, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 No retail electric supplier, as defined in Section 158.22 of
15 Title 17 of the Oklahoma Statutes, shall offer ratepayer-funded
16 incentives, rebates, or inducements to its customers to promote the
17 switching of fuel sources from natural gas to electricity.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
19 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
20 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
21 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
22 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
