10 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Agriculture, Food, and Forestry; requiring that
11 portions of certain appropriated funds be used for
certain purposes; providing an effective date; and
12 declaring an emergency.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
16 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled House Bill
17 No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the
18 sum of One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) shall be used for the
19 Oklahoma State University Robert M. Kerr Food and Agricultural
20 Products Center for operational expenses.
21 SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
22 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled House Bill
23 No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the
24 sum of Five Million Two Hundred Thousand Dollars ($5,200,000.00)
Req. No. 2128 Page 1
1 shal l be used for the extension offices that are affiliated with the
2 Oklahoma State University Cooperative Extension Service.
3 SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
4 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled House Bill
5 No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the
6 sum of One Million Three Hundred Thousand Dollars ($1,300,000.00)
7 shall be used for research centers that are affiliated with Oklahoma
8 State University Agriculture Experiment Stations.
9 SECTION 4. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
10 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled House Bill
11 No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the
12 sum of Five Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($5,500,000.00)
13 shall be used for cooperative extension and agriculture research
14 programs that are affiliated with Langston University.
15 SECTION 5. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
16 SECTION 6. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
17 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
18 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
19 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
