18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 14-108.1. A. Neither a notary public nor an agent
20 working on behalf of a notary public shall be authorized to:
21 1. Request absentee ballots on behalf of a voter other than
22 himself or herself;
23 2. Assist a voter in requesting absentee ballots, other than
24 for himself or herself or a member of his or her household;
