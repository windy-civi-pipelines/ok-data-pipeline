9 An Act relating to public health and safety;
exempting public trust hospitals from the Oklahoma
10 Open Meeting Act and Oklahoma Open Records Act under
certain circumstances; authorizing executive
11 sessions; providing exception related to budget
information; defining term; providing for
12 codification; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3310 of Title 63, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 In the event a public trust hospital enters into a joint venture
20 with or acquires an interest in a not-for-profit entity to
21 effectuate the administration of the mission of the public trust,
22 that entity shall not be subject to the Oklahoma Open Meeting Act
23 and the Oklahoma Open Records Act. Any information submitted to or
24 compiled by the public trust with respect to marketing plans,
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB1738 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 fina ncial statements, trade secrets, research concepts, methods or
2 products or any other proprietary information submitted to or
3 compiled by the trust, persons, firms, associations, partnerships,
4 agencies, corporations, institutions of higher education, nonprofit
5 research institutions, or other entities shall be confidential,
6 except to the extent that the person or entity which provided such
7 information or which is the subject of such information consents to
8 disclosure. Executive sessions may be held to discuss such
9 materials if deemed necessary by the trust. The provisions of this
10 section shall not apply to budgetary information related to
11 appropriations or the appropriations process. For purposes of this
12 section, "public trust hospital" is defined as any hospital created
13 pursuant to Section 176.1 of Title 60 of the Oklahoma Statutes or
14 Section 790.1 of Title 19 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
16 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY
