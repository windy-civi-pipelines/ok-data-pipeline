Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to medical records; providing for
retention of medical records; providing for
8 codification; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5009.7 of Title 63, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall require that health
16 care providers retain, for not less than a period of ten (10) years,
17 any records necessary to disclose the extent of services the health
18 care provider, wholly owned supplier, or subcontractor, furnishes to
19 recipients.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
