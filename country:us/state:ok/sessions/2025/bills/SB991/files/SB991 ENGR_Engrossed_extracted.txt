7 An Act relating to discrimination; adopting certain
definition of anti-Semitism; stating purposes for use
8 of certain definition; construing provisions; and
providing for codification.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1911 of Title 25, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. The State of Oklahoma hereby adopts the non-legally binding
16 Working Definition of Anti-Semitism adopted by the International
17 Holocaust Remembrance Alliance (IHRA) on May 26, 2016, including the
18 contemporary examples of anti-Semitism set forth therein,
19 exclusively as a tool and guide for training, education, and
20 recognizing and combating anti-Semitic hate crimes or discrimination
21 and for tracking and reporting anti-Semitic incidents in this state.
22 B. The provisions of this act shall not be construed to
23 diminish or infringe upon any right protected under the First
