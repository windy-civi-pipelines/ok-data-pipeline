Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to appropriations; making
appropriations to the Oklahoma Department of
8 Commerce; establishing amounts; identifying source;
providing purposes; providing an effective date and
9 declaring an emergency.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
14 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
15 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
16 Commerce from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General
17 Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June,
18 30, 2026, the sum of Four Million Dollars ($4,000,000.00) or so much
19 thereof as may be necessary to provide for or facilitate the
20 construction of needed HAZMAT facilities at an industrial park in
21 this state, in an effort to secure the health and safety of the
22 residents of the state located near and potentially impacted by
23 large-scale economic activity likely to occur at such an industrial
24 park.
Req. No. 10112 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
2 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
3 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
4 Commerce from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General
5 Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June,
6 30, 2026, the sum of One Million Four Hundred Thousand Dollars
7 ($1,400,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to provide
8 for or facilitate the operation of needed HAZMAT facilities at an
9 industrial park in this state, in an effort to secure the health and
10 safety of the residents of the state located near and potentially
11 impacted by large-scale economic activity likely to occur at such an
12 industrial park.
13 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
14 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
15 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
16 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
17 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
