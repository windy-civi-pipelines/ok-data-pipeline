9 AS INTRODUCED
10 An Act relating to the Administrative Procedures Act;
amending 75 O.S. 2021, Section 308.3, as amended by
11 Section 6, Chapter 38, O.S.L. 2023 (75 O.S. Supp.
2024, Section 308.3), which relates to joint
12 resolutions of the Legislature; updating reference to
rule submission date; and providing an effective
13 date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 75 O.S. 2021, Section 308.3, as
18 amended by Section 6, Chapter 38, O.S.L. 2023 (75 O.S. Supp. 2024,
19 Section 308.3), is amended to read as follows:
20 Section 308.3. A. The Legislature shall have joint resolutions
21 prepared for consideration each session.
22 B. For the purpose of this section, a proposed permanent rule
23 may be disapproved, in whole or in part, in a joint resolution
24 considered by the Legislature.
HB2731 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 C. If any rule received on or before the date established
2 pursuant to paragraph 1 of subsection A of Section 308 of this title
3 is not subject to a joint resolution passed by both houses of the
4 Legislature and signed by the Governor or is found by the Governor
5 to have a technical legal defect preventing approval of
6 administrative rules intended to be approved by the Legislature, the
7 Governor may declare any rules received on or before April 1
8 February 1 and not subject to a joint resolution passed by both
9 houses of the Legislature to be approved or disapproved and finally
10 adopted by publishing a single declaration in "The Oklahoma
11 Register" on or before July 17. If the Governor finds that the
12 joint resolution has a technical legal defect, the Governor shall
13 make the finding in writing and submit the finding to the
14 Legislature.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
16
17 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON ADMINISTRATIVE RULES, dated
02/06/2025 - DO PASS, As Coauthored.
