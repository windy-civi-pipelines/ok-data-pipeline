FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend amendment number 1 to HB1486
Page 8 Section 19 Lines 20
Of the Engrossed Bill
by deleting the language beginning with the word "The" on page 8, line 20
through the period "." on page 9, line 2 and inserting in lieu thereof the
following language:
"The bridge on State Highway 36 crossing over West Cache Creek northeast
of Faxon in Comanche County shall be designated as the "Sgt. George Malcom
Williams Memorial Bridge". The Department of Transportation shall cause
suitable permanent markers bearing that name to be placed on the bridge."
