7 An Act relating to the Prohibition of Unfair Service
Agreements Act; amending Section 5, Chapter 148,
8 O.S.L. 2024 (16 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 305), which
relates to prohibiting the recording of unfair
9 service agreements; authorizing the refusal of
certain notice and memorandum; providing exemption
10 from liability under certain circumstances; adding
notices and memorandums to actual or constructive
11 notice provision; making unfair service agreements,
notices, and memorandums void when recorded; and
12 providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 5, Chapter 148, O.S.L.
17 2024 (16 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 305), is amended to read as
18 follows:
19 Section 305. 1. Recording an unfair service agreement is
20 prohibited. No person shall record or cause to be recorded an
21 unfair service agreement or notice or memorandum thereof in this
22 state;
23 2. A county clerk's office shall refuse to accept for
24 recordation an unfair service agreement or notice or memorandum;
ENGR. H. B. NO. 1177 Page 1
1 3. In the event that a county clerk does accidentally record an
2 unfair service agreement or notice or memorandum, the county clerk
3 shall incur no liability for doing so; and
4 4. If an unfair service agreement or notice or memorandum is
5 recorded in this state, it shall not provide actual or constructive
6 notice against an otherwise bona fide purchaser or, creditor, or
7 title company; and
8 5. If an unfair service agreement or notice or memorandum is
9 recorded in this state it shall be considered void.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
11 Passed the House of Representatives the 24th day of March, 2025.
