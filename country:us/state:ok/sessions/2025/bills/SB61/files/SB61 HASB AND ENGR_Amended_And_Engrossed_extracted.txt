designations; designating the ABLE Agent Lori Thomas
8 Memorial Bridge; designating the Donald Ray Ward
Memorial Bridge; designating the Ike Glass Memorial
9 Intersection; designating the Novak and Jean Bullard

6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
8 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
9 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.468 of Title 69, unless
10 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
11 The bridge on U.S. Highway 70 crossing over South 9th Avenue of
12 Durant in Bryan County shall be designated as the “ABLE Agent Lori
13 Thomas Memorial Bridge”. The Department of Transportation shall
14 cause suitable permanent markers bearing that name to be placed on
15 the bridge.
16 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.469 of Title 69, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 The bridge crossing over Interstate 240 on Interstate 40 in
20 Oklahoma County shall be designated as the “Donald Ray Ward Memorial
21 Bridge”. The Department of Transportation shall cause suitable
22 permanent markers bearing that name to be placed on the bridge.

24

1 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.470 of Title 69, unless
3 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 The intersection of U.S. Highway 77 and State Highway 11 in Kay
5 County shall be designated as the “Ike Glass Memorial Intersection”.
6 The Department of Transportation shall cause suitable permanent
7 markers bearing that name to be placed at the intersection.
8 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
9 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.471 of Title 69, unless
10 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
11 The bridge on U.S. Highway 69 crossing over West Main Street of
12 Durant in Bryan County shall be designated as the “Novak and Jean
13 Bullard Memorial Bridge”. The Department of Transportation shall
14 cause suitable permanent markers bearing that name to be placed on
15 the bridge.
16 SECTION 5. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.472 of Title 69, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 The bridge over the Kiamichi River on U.S. Highway 259
20 approximately one-half (1/2) mile south of the U.S. Highway 259 and
21 State Highway 63 intersection in Le Flore County shall be designated
22 as the “PFC Lewis Othel Branscum Memorial Bridge”. The Department
23 of Transportation shall cause suitable permanent markers bearing
24 that name to be placed on the bridge.

6 Memorial Road in Lincoln County shall be designated as the “SFC

8 Highway”. The Department of Transportation shall cause suitable

15 of Chickasaw Trail in Murray County shall be designated as the “SSGT
16 Kenneth Michael Pannell Memorial Highway”. The Department of
17 Transportation shall cause suitable permanent markers bearing that
18 name to be placed on the highway.
19 SECTION 8. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025."
