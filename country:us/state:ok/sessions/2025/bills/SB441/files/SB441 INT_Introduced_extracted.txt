6 AS INTRODUCED
6
7 An Act relating to the state Medicaid program;
7 requiring certain coverage of chiropractic care;
8 prohibiting certain exclusion from coverage;
8 preventing certain prior authorization requirement;
9 directing promulgation of rules; requiring Oklahoma
9 Health Care Authority to seek necessary federal
10 approval; providing for codification; providing an
10 effective date; and declaring an emergency.
11
11
12
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5029.2 of Title 63, unless there
15
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16
17 A. The state Medicaid program shall cover chiropractic care
17
18 performed by licensed chiropractic physicians when such services are
18
19 medically necessary for the diagnosis and treatment of illness or
19
20 injury or to improve the functioning of a malformed body member.
20
21 B. The state Medicaid program shall not exclude members from
21
22 coverage under this section on the basis of age.
22
23 C. No prior authorization shall be required for a member to
23
24 receive medically necessary chiropractic care.
24
Req. No. 753 Page 1
1 D. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority Board shall promulgate
1
2 rules as necessary to implement the provisions of this section.
2
3 E. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall seek any federal
3
4 approval necessary to implement the provisions of this section.
4
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
5
6 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
6
7 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
7
8 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
8
9 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
