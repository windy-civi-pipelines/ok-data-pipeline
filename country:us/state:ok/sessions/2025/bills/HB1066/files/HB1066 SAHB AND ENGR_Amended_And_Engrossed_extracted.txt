7 An Act relating to criminal procedure; amending 22
O.S. 2021, Section 517, which relates to the
8 withdrawal of guilty pleas; allowing for the
withdrawal of guilty pleas; establishing time
9 limitation for filing applications; requiring
inclusion of certain information in application; and

16 “An Act relating to bail; amending 22 O.S. 2021,

Section 2:
Section 1077, which relates to bail allowable on
17 appeal; prohibiting bail on appeal for certain
offenses; updating statutory language; updating
18 statutory reference; and providing an effective date.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 22 O.S. 2021, Section 1077, is
22 amended to read as follows:
23 Section 1077. Bail on appeal shall be allowed on appeal from a
24 judgment of conviction of a misdemeanor, or in felony cases where
ENGR. S. A. TO ENGR. H. B. NO. 1066 Page 1
1 the punishment is a fine only, and when made and approved shall stay
2 the execution of such judgment. Bail on appeal after the effective
3 date of this act April 17, 1969, shall not be allowed after
4 conviction of any of the following offenses:
5 1. Murder in any degree;
6 2. Kidnapping for purpose of extortion;
7 3. Robbery with a dangerous weapon;
8 4. Rape in any degree;
9 5. Arson in the first degree;
10 6. Shooting with intent to kill;
11 7. Manslaughter in the first degree;
12 8. Forcible sodomy;
13 9. Any felony conviction for which the evidence shows that the
14 defendant used or was in possession of a firearm or other dangerous
15 or deadly weapon during the commission of the offense;
16 10. Trafficking in illegal drugs;
17 11. Manufacturing a controlled dangerous substance;
18 12. Abuse against an intimate partner as defined by Section
19 60.1 of this title;
20 13. Abuse of a child;
21 14. Sexual abuse of a child; or
22 13. 15. Any other felony after former conviction of a felony.
23 The granting or refusal of bail after judgment of conviction in
24 all other felony cases shall rest in the discretion of the court;
ENGR. S. A. TO ENGR. H. B. NO. 1066 Page 2
1 prov ided, however, if bail is allowed, the trial court shall state
2 the reason therefor for granting bail.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.”
