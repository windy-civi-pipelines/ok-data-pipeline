8 An Act relating to criminal procedure; amending 22
O.S. 2021, Section 517, which relates to the
9 withdrawal of guilty pleas; allowing for the
withdrawal of guilty pleas; establishing time
10 limitation for filing applications; requiring
inclusion of certain information in application; and
11 providing an effective date.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 22 O.S. 2021, Section 517, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 517. The court may, at any time before judgment, upon
19 permit a plea of guilty, permit it to be withdrawn, and a plea of
20 not guilty substituted if the defendant files an application to
21 withdraw the plea of guilty within ten (10) days from the date of
22 the entry of the plea setting forth in detail the grounds for the
23 withdrawal of the plea and a request for an evidentiary hearing.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
