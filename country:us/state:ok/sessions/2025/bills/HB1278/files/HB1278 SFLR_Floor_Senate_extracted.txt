14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-580 of Title 63, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Barbara Weber
19 Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) Grant Program".
20 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
21 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-580.1 of Title 63, unless
22 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
23 There is hereby created the Barbara Weber Amyotrophic Lateral
24 Sclerosis (ALS) Grant Program which shall be administered by the
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB1278 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 Stat e Department of Health. The program shall grant funds to
2 support research for Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) and for
3 programs that help support Oklahoma residents afflicted with ALS or
4 Oklahoma residents caring for a family member afflicted with ALS.
5 Beginning July 1, 2026, the State Department of Health shall provide
6 an annual report to the Governor, the Speaker of the Oklahoma House
7 of Representatives, the President Pro Tempore of the Oklahoma State
8 Senate, Appropriations and Budget Chair of the Oklahoma House of
9 Representatives, and the Appropriations Chair of the Oklahoma State
10 Senate that includes, but is not limited to, how many grants have
11 been awarded during the previous fiscal year, which entities the
12 grants have been awarded to, and for what purposes the funds are
13 supporting.
14 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-580.2 of Title 63, unless
16 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 There is hereby created in the Oklahoma State Treasury a
18 revolving fund for the State Department of Health to be designated
19 the "Barbara Weber Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) Grant Program
20 Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject
21 to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies received
22 by the State Department of Health from appropriations, donations,
23 grants, or other sources of funding specifically designated for
24 deposit to the Barbara Weber Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS)
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB1278 SFLR Page 2
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 Gran t Program Revolving Fund. All monies accruing to the credit of
2 said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended
3 by the State Department of Health for the purpose of the Barbara
4 Weber Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) Grant Program.
5 Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants issued by
6 the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with
7 the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
8 approval and payment.
9 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
10 SECTION 5. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
11 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
12 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
13 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
14 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS
