I move to amend Senate Bill No. 722, as follows:
1. On page 3, line 1, after the word “any” and before the word “of”, by removing the word “animal”
and replacing with the words “cattle or bison younger than eighteen (18) months of age”;
2. On page 3, line 1, after the word “state” and before the period, by adding the words “without the
owner’s consent”; and
3. On page 6, line 10, after the word “state” and before the semicolon, by adding the words “without
the owner’s consent”.
