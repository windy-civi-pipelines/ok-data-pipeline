Section 1:
HB2800 FA1-A1 MartiTJ-JL(Untimely Filed)
3/26/2025 1:01:25 pm
FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend amendment number 1 to HB2800
Page 3 Section 4 Lines 12
Of the Engrossed Bill
By inserting after the period "." the following language, "Notwithstanding
any provision to the contrary, this act shall not apply to any owners
association that, prior to the effective date of this act, has adopted
governing documents, including but not limited to covenants, conditions,
and restrictions, that explicitly prohibit or regulate short-term or long-
term rentals. Such owner associations shall retain the authority to
enforce their pre-existing rental restrictions and regulations as set
forth in their governing documents, provided that those restrictions are
in compliance with applicable state and local laws."
Page 5, Section 9, Line 13:
By deleting Section 9 in its entirety and by inserting in lieu thereof the
following new section, "SECTION 9. It being immediately necessary for the
preservation of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and be in
full force from and after its passage and approval."
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
