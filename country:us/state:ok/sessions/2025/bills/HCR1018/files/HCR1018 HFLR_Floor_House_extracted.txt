3 WHEREAS, from 1999 to 2001, Pope Leo XIV served on the Board of

1 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
2 OF THE 1ST SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE, THE SENATE
3 CONCURRING THEREIN:
4 THAT the Oklahoma House of Representatives and the Oklahoma
5 State Senate honor and commend His Holiness Pope Leo XIV as the
6 first American Pope, recognizing his leadership, humility, and moral
7 clarity, and acknowledging with gratitude his contribution to the
8 State of Oklahoma during his years of service at Cascia Hall in
9 Tulsa.
10 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that a copy of this resolution be
11 delivered to the Apostolic Nunciature of the Holy See in Washington,
12 D.C., and to Cascia Hall Preparatory School in Tulsa as a reminder
13 of the profound connections that bind even global moments of history
14 to the everyday communities of our great state.
15
16 DIRECT TO CALENDAR.
