PROPOSED OVERSIGHT COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8
An Act relating to schools; amending Section 1,
9 Chapter 297, O.S.L. 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section
6-401), which relates to student communication;
10 defining term; modifying certain requirement;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
11 emergency.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 297, O.S.L.
16 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 6-401), is amended to read as
17 follows:
18 Section 6-401. A. As used in this section:
19 1. "Electronic or digital communication" includes, but is not
20 limited to, emails, text messages, instant messages, direct
21 messages, social media messages, messages sent through software
22 applications, and any other electronic or digital means of
23 communication; and
24
Req. No. 13127 Page 1
1 2. "School personnel" means teachers, coaches, administrators,
2 school bus drivers, or any other persons employed full-time or part-
3 time by a public school or charter school; and
4 3. "Student" means a person enrolled in a public school or
5 charter school in this state receiving primary or secondary
6 education to obtain a standard high school diploma or high school
7 equivalency diploma. Student does not mean a person who holds a
8 standard high school diploma or high school equivalency diploma.
9 B. School personnel engaging in electronic or digital
10 communication with an individual student shall include the student's
11 parent or legal guardian in any electronic or digital communication,
12 unless such communication is on a school-approved platform and
13 related to school and academic communications.
14 C. Exceptions to the requirement in subsection B of this
15 section may be made in case of an emergency as determined by the
16 local school board, subject to subsequent notification to the parent
17 or legal guardian. School personnel shall make reasonable efforts
18 to use school-approved platforms, systems, or applications that
19 allow automatic inclusion of parents or legal guardians in
20 communications with students.
21 D. Schools shall provide training, developed by the State
22 Department of Education, for school personnel on the student
23 communication requirements of this section.
24
Req. No. 13127 Page 2
1 E. Any school personnel who is reported to be in violation of
2 subsection B of this section shall may be put on administrative
3 leave while the school district investigates the incident and
4 notifies the board of education. If the investigation finds that no
5 misconduct occurred, the school personnel shall be reinstated and
6 the incident shall be noted in the school personnel's employee file.
7 If the investigation finds misconduct occurred, the school personnel
8 shall be disciplined according to the school district board of
9 education's policy, up to and including termination of employment,
10 and the incident shall be reported to law enforcement pursuant to
11 Section 1210.163 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes this title.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
13 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
14 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
15 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
16 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
