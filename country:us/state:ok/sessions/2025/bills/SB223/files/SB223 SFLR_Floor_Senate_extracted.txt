17 shall be allowed as credits as provided by law.
18 B. 1. There shall be allowed as a credit against the tax

21 2353 of this title, upon income received as compensation for
22 personal services in such other state; provided, such credit shall
23 not be allowed with respect to any income specified in Section 114
24 of Title 4 of the United States Code, 4 U.S.C., Section 114, upon

20 paragraph shall not be claimed by any taxpayer if the federal
