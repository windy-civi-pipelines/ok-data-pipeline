8 An Act relating to motor vehicles; defining terms;
prohibiting the manufacture, importation,
9 distribution, selling, or installation of certain
supplemental restraint systems; setting punishment
10 for violations; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 12-413.1 of Title 47, unless
16 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. As used in this section:
18 1. "Airbag" means an inflatable restraint device for vehicle
19 occupants that is part of a supplemental restraint system in a motor
20 vehicle;
21 2. "Counterfeit supplemental restraint system component" means
22 a replacement supplemental restraint system component that displays
23 a mark identical or substantially similar to the genuine mark of a
24 motor vehicle manufacturer or supplier of parts to a motor vehicle
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB2159 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 manu facturer without authorization from that manufacturer or
2 supplier;
3 3. "Nonfunctional airbag" means a replacement airbag that:
4 a. was previously deployed or damaged,
5 b. has an electric fault that is detected by the motor
6 vehicles' diagnostic systems when the installation
7 procedure is completed and the motor vehicle is
8 returned to the customer who requested the work to be
9 performed or when ownership is intended to be
10 transferred,
11 c. includes a part or object, including a supplemental
12 restraint system component, installed in a motor
13 vehicle to mislead the owner or operator of the motor
14 vehicle into believing that a functional airbag has
15 been installed, or
16 d. is prohibited in accordance with 49 U.S.C. Section
17 301209(j) from being sold or leased;
18 4. "Supplemental restraint system" means a passive motor
19 vehicle occupant crash protection system designed for use in
20 conjunction with active restraint systems, including, but not
21 limited to, seat belt assemblies, as described in 49 CFR 571.208.
22 Such term shall include:
23 a. each airbag installed in accordance with the motor
24 vehicle manufacturer's design, and
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB2159 SFLR Page 2
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 b. all components required to ensure that an airbag
2 operates as designed by the manufacturer in the event
3 of a crash and in accordance with the federal motor
4 vehicle safety standards for the specific make, model
5 and year of the motor vehicle.
6 B. No person shall knowingly or intentionally manufacture,
7 import, distribute, sell, offer for sale, install, or reinstall a
8 device intended to replace a component of a supplemental restraint
9 system if the device is:
10 1. A counterfeit supplemental restraint system component;
11 2. A nonfunctional airbag; or
12 3. Any object in lieu of a supplemental restraint system
13 component if the object was not designed in accordance with federal
14 safety regulations for the make, model, and year of the motor
15 vehicle in which such device is or will be installed.
16 C. Any person violating any of the provisions of this section,
17 shall, upon conviction thereof, be punished as provided in Section
18 17-101 of Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
