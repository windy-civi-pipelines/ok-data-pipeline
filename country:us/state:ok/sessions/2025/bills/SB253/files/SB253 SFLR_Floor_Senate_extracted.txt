8 An Act relating to the state Medicaid program;
requiring the Oklahoma Health Care Authority to
9 include certain information in annual budget request;
specifying calculation method of certain costs;
10 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-1925.2A of Title 63, unless
16 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority in its annual budget
18 request submitted pursuant to Section 34.36 of Title 62 of the
19 Oklahoma Statutes shall include a supplemental item reflecting the
20 new state and federal funding necessary to meet the additional costs
21 associated with reimbursing nursing facilities and intermediate care
22 facilities for individuals with intellectual disabilities at the
23 most recent audited cost.
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB253 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 B. Audited cost shall be calculated by using the latest cost
2 report submitted to the Oklahoma Health Care Authority.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
