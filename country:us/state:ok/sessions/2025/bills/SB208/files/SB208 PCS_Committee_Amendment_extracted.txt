1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to wildlife; authorizing the
Department of Wildlife Conservation to implement a
9 system for registration and licensing of hunting
guides; excluding certain entities; providing for
10 promulgation of rules; providing for codification;
and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4-112B of Title 29, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. The Department of Wildlife Conservation shall, for the
18 safety and security of landowners and hunters in this state, create
19 a system of registration and licensing for any person or entity
20 providing guiding services in this state, excluding those services
21 provided for commercial hunting facilities and landowners providing
22 guiding services on their own property.
23 B. The Department shall promulgate all rules necessary to
24 implement the provisions of this section.
Req. No. 1793 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
