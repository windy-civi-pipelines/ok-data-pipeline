SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified

11

12
13
of the Oklahoma Statutes shall be on a form prescribed by the
13
14
Secretary of Education and contain a statement in fourteen-point
14
15
boldface font reading:
15
16
“I am aware that I have a First Amendment right, as recognized
16
17

17
18
or making political contributions to a professional employee
18
19

19
20
or political contributions are voluntary and that I may not be

21
discriminated against for my refusal to join or financially support
21
22
a professional employee organization. I hereby authorize my
22
23
employer to deduct dues and/or political contributions from my
23
24
salary in the amounts specified in accordance with my professional
24

1
employee organization’s bylaws. I understand that I may revoke this
1
2
authorization at any time.”

3
B. Authorization containing an employee’s full name, position,
3
4
professional organization, and signature shall be submitted to the
4
5
school district. The school district shall confirm the
5
6
authorization by emailing the employee’s school district-provided
6
7
email address and shall wait for confirmation of the authorization
7
8
before starting any deduction. If the employee does not possess a
8
9
school district-provided email address, the school district may use
9
10
other means it deems appropriate to confirm the authorization.
10
11
C. An authorization shall expire at the start of the first full
11
12
pay period each school year unless it was authorized or reauthorized
12
13
during the previous school year.
