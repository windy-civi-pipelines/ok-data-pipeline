7 An Act relating to criminal disturbance; amending 21
O.S. 2021, Sections 915 and 916, which relate to
8 disturbance of religious meeting; specifying range of
punishment for certain misdemeanor offense;
9 establishing felony offense for certain convictions;
specifying range of punishment for certain felony
10 offense; modifying definitions; modifying elements of
certain offense; updating statutory language; and
11 declaring an emergency.
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 915, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 915. Every person who willfully disturbs, interrupts,
17 or disquiets any assemblage of people met for religious worship, by
18 any of the acts or things hereinafter enumerated, is in Section 916
19 of this title shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a misdemeanor
20 punishable by a fine not to exceed Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00),
21 by imprisonment in the county jail for a term not to exceed one (1)
22 year, or by both such fine and imprisonment. A person who commits a
23 second or subsequent offense shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a
24 felony punishable by a fine not to exceed One Thousand Dollars
ENGR. S. B. NO. 743 Page 1
1 ($1, 000.00), by imprisonment in the custody of the Department of
2 Corrections for a term not to exceed two (2) years, or by both such
3 fine and imprisonment.
4 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 916, is
5 amended to read as follows:
6 Section 916. The following are the acts deemed to constitute
7 disturbance of a religious meeting as prohibited by Section 915 of
8 this title:
9 1. Uttering any profane discourse, committing any rude or
10 indecent act, or making any unnecessary noise, either within the
11 place where such meeting is held, or so near it as to disturb the
12 order and solemnity of the meeting.;
13 2. Exhibiting or engaging in, within one (1) mile, any shows or
14 plays protest, demonstration, show, or play without a license by the
15 proper authority.;
16 3. Engaging in, or aiding or promoting within the like
17 distance, any racing of animals or gaming of any description.; or
18 4. Obstructing in any manner, without authority of law, within
19 the like distance one (1) mile, the free passage along any highway
20 to the place of such the meeting.
21 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
22 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
23 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
24 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
