1 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3-101, as
2 amended by Section 1, Chapter 182, O.S.L. 2023 (70 O.S. Supp. 2024,
3 Section 3-101), is amended to read as follows:
4 Section 3-101. A. The State Board of Education shall consist
5 of seven (7) ten (10) members. The State Superintendent of Public
6 Instruction shall be a member and the chairperson of the Board. The
7 remaining six nine members shall be appointed by the Governor by and
8 with the advice and consent of the Senate Except as otherwise
9 provided for in subsection B of this section, upon assuming office
10 each Governor shall appoint one member from each congressional
11 district and one member from the state at large as follows:
12 1. Five members shall be appointed by the Governor by and with
13 the advice and consent of the Senate;
14 2. Two members shall be appointed by the Pro Tempore of the
15 Oklahoma State Senate; and
16 3. Two members shall be appointed by the Speaker of the House
17 of Representatives.
18 Each appointment shall be made to take effect on April 2. The
19 term of each appointed member shall be four (4) years. Appointed
20 members shall serve until a successor is appointed. Appointed
21 members shall serve at the pleasure of the Governor member's
22 appointing authority. Upon the occurrence of a vacancy the same
23 shall be filled by the Governor, subject to confirmation by the
24 Senate at the next session of the Legislature by the appointing
Req. No. 13681 Page 2
1 auth ority for that member within ninety (90) days of a vacancy
2 occurring. The appointment to fill a vacancy shall be made for the
3 unexpired term.
4 B. On the effective date of this act, all appointed positions
5 of the current State Board of Education shall be deemed vacant and
6 the terms of persons serving on the Board shall be deemed
7 terminated. The Governor appointing authorities shall make initial
8 appointments pursuant to the provisions of this subsection and
9 subsection A within thirty (30) days of the effective date of this
10 act, with one member appointed from each congressional district and
11 one member appointed from the state at large. The Governor's
12 appointments shall be subject to confirmation by the Senate at the
13 next session of the Legislature. The terms for members appointed
14 pursuant to this subsection shall terminate on April 2, 2015 2030.
15 Thereafter, members shall be appointed as provided for in subsection
16 A of this section.
17 C. No person shall be eligible to be appointed to serve on the
18 State Board unless the person has been awarded a high school diploma
19 or certificate of high school equivalency. No person shall be
20 appointed to serve on the State Board if the person serves on a
21 board of education of a school district which is supervised by the
22 State Board pursuant to Section 3-104 of this title. Any member
23 appointed to the State Board shall complete the workshop
24 requirements of a new school board member pursuant to Section 5-110
Req. No. 13681 Page 3
1 of t his title within thirteen (13) months following or preceding the
2 appointment of the member. Notwithstanding any provision of law to
3 the contrary, the State Department of Education shall not charge any
4 member of the State Board of Education a fee for any workshop
5 provided by the Department for board members pursuant to Section 5-
6 110 of this title and shall not pay a fee to any organization or
7 institution of higher education on behalf of a member of the State
8 Board of Education, or reimburse any member of the Board for a fee
9 paid to any organization or institution of higher education, for
10 attendance at a workshop or courses to satisfy the requirements of
11 Section 5-110 of this title.
12 D. Members of the Board shall receive necessary traveling
13 expenses while in the performance of their duties in accordance with
14 the State Travel Reimbursement Act.
15 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3-102, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 3-102. A. The State Board of Education shall meet in
18 regular session once each month. Special meetings may be called by
19 the president or by a majority of the members of the Board.
20 B. The State Board shall, at least forty-eight (48) hours prior
21 to any such regularly scheduled or special meeting, prominently post
22 the agenda at the principal office of the State Board and on the
23 State Board's Internet website. If any agenda item or document
24

1 post ed by the Board is changed, altered, or otherwise different than
2 the posted agenda or documents, the agenda item shall:
3 1. Not be an actionable item and may only be discussed at the
4 meeting which the item was on the agenda for; and
5 2. Be placed as an actionable item on the agenda for the next
6 meeting.
7 C. Upon written request to add an item to an agenda by at least
8 three members of the Board, any member of the Board shall be
9 authorized to place an item for discussion on a Board agenda. The
10 item shall be placed on the agenda for discussion only for the next
11 meeting or a subsequent meeting as decided by the requesting
12 members. After the item has been discussed, the same item may then
13 be placed on a following agenda as an actionable item.
14 SECTION 3. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3-103, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 3-103. A quorum of the State Board of Education shall
17 consist of four six members. No business may be transacted at any
18 meeting unless a quorum is present and every act of said Board shall
19 be approved by a majority of the membership of said Board.
20 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
