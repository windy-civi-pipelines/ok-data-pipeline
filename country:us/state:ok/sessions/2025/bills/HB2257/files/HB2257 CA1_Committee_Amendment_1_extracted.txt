allowed to participate per semester per undergraduate or graduate program”;

“, 4, or 5”;
3. On page 7, line 3, by replacing “November” with “July”;
4. And, on page 7, line 3 ½ by inserting a new Section 3 to read as follows and by amending the title
to conform.
