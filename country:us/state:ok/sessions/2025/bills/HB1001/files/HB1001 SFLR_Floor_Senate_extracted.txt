24 title;

7 this title;
8 17. Child sex trafficking as defined in Section 1030 of this
9 title;
10 18. Lewd molestation of a child as defined in Section 1123 of
11 this title;

17 another person from assault and battery; or

19 title; or

2 convicted of these offenses shall not be eligible for earned credits

5 sentence imposed.
6 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
