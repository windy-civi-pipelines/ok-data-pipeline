7 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 34 O.S. 2021, Section 3, is
8 amended to read as follows:
9 Section 3. A. Each initiative petition and each referendum
10 petition shall be duplicated for the securing of signatures. The
11 Secretary of State shall design a form, subject to change, for
12 signatures that shall be used by proponents of initiatives or
13 referendums when collecting signatures, and each sheet for
14 signatures shall be attached to a copy of the petition. Each copy
15 of the petition and sheets for signatures is hereinafter termed a
16 pamphlet. On the outer page of each pamphlet shall be printed the
17 word “Warning”, and underneath this in ten-point type the words, “It
18 is a felony for anyone to sign an initiative or referendum petition
19 with any name other than his or her own, or knowingly to sign his or
20 her name more than once for the measure, or to sign such petition
21 when he or she is not a legal voter of this state.” A simple
22 statement of the gist of the proposition shall be printed on the top
23 margin of each signature sheet and shall:

4 particular profession or trade not commonly known to the citizens of
5 this state;
6 3. Not contain euphemisms, words, or phrases regarded in
7 popular parlance as code words, or an apparent attempt to deceive
8 voters;
9 4. Not reflect partiality in its composition or contain any
10 argument for or against the measure; and

15 an existing tax, or elimination of existing services.

21 with the requirements of this section. The Secretary may remove any

23 proponents of the petition to submit a gist that complies with all
24 the requirements of this section.
