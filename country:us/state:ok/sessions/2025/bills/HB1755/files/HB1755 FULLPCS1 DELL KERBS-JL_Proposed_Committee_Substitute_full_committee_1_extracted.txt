8 An Act relating to warning labels; providing labels
are sufficient under certain circumstances; and
9 providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-97 of Title 2, unless there is
15 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 Notwithstanding any provision to the contrary, any label
17 approved by the United States Environmental Protection Agency shall
18 be sufficient to satisfy any requirements for a warning regarding
19 health or safety under Oklahoma state law, including without
20 limitation, the duty to warn, or any other common law duty to warn.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
