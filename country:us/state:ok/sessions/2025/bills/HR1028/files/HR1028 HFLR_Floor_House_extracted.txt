2 health care profession, with an estimated population of more than

4 WHEREAS, when nurse staffing levels increase, the risk of

15 and ideals of National Nurses Week, recognizes the significant
16 contributions of nurses to the health care system in Oklahoma, and
17 encourages the citizens of Oklahoma to observe National Nurses Week
18 and understand the importance of nurses in the lives of patients.
