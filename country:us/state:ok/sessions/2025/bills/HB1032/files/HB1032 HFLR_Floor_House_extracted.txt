18 amended by Section 1, Chapter 283, O.S.L. 2024 (59 O.S. Supp. 2024,

20 Section 481. A State Board of Medical Licensure and Supervision
21 hereinafter referred to as the "Board", is hereby re-created, to
22 continue until July 1, 2025 2030, in accordance with the provisions
23 of the Oklahoma Sunset Law. The Board shall be composed of seven
24 (7) allopathic physicians licensed to practice medicine in this
