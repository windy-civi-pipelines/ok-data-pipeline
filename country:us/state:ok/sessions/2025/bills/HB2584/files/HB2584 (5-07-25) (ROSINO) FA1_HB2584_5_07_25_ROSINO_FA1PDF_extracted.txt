I move to amend House Bill No. 2584 as follows:
1. On Page 21, Line 5, by deleting after the word “the” and before the word “shall”, the
words “enactment of this subsection” and inserting the words “effective date of this act”;
2. On Page 26, Line 9, by inserting after the word “assistant” and before the comma “,”, the
words “not practicing under a practice agreement”;
3. On Page 26, Line 9, by deleting after the word “of” and before the second word
“physician”, the word “the” and inserting the word “such”;
4. On Page 26, Lines 14 through 16, by deleting after the word “shall” on Line 14 and before
the period “.” on Line 16, all language and inserting the words “not apply to a physician assistant
practicing under a practice agreement”.
