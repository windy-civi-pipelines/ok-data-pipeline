7 An Act relating to counties and county officers;
amending 19 O.S. 2021, Section 180.62, as amended by
8 Section 3, Chapter 394, O.S.L. 2024 (19 O.S. Supp.
2024, Section 180.62), which relates to basic
9 salaries; increasing maximum wage for county
officers; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 19 O.S. 2021, Section 180.62, as
14 amended by Section 3, Chapter 394, O.S.L. 2024 (19 O.S. Supp. 2024,
15 Section 180.62), is amended to read as follows:
16 Section 180.62. A. The basic salaries of county officers upon
17 which all salaries and future increases or decreases thereof shall
18 not be less than Nineteen Thousand Dollars ($19,000.00) per annum
19 nor shall they exceed Forty-nine Thousand Five Hundred Dollars
20 ($49,500.00) Seventy-four Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($74,500.00)
21 per annum, provided the basic salary for the sheriff, which shall be
22 no less than other salaries for county officers controlled by this
23 section, shall not be less than Forty-four Thousand Dollars
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 1665 Page 1
1 ($44 ,000.00) per annum nor shall it exceed Seventy-four Thousand
2 Five Hundred Dollars ($74,500.00) per annum.
3 B. The board of county commissioners, or if the county has
4 elected to come under the County Budget Act, the budget board
5 members of the county, shall set the salaries for all elected county
6 officials within the limits allowed by law.
7 C. The annual salaries fixed by this act shall be paid either
8 monthly or twice a month, by order of the board of county
9 commissioners, for each month or fraction thereof the incumbent
10 lawfully occupies and holds title to such office.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
12 Passed the House of Representatives the 17th day of March, 2025.
