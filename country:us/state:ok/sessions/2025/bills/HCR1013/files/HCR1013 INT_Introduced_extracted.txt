16 many of its citizens and honoring the role of faith in the history
17 and culture of Oklahoma.
18 THAT the Oklahoma State Legislature expresses that this
19 resolution is not intended to establish any religion or infringe
20 upon the rights of any individual, but rather to celebrate the
21 voluntary expression of belief that has shaped communities across
22 the state.
23 THAT copies of this resolution be distributed to the Governor of
