7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Firefighters Pension
and Retirement System; providing for benefits
9 increase subject to certain exception; defining term;
and providing for codification.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 49-143.8 of Title 11, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. Except as provided in subsection B of this section, and
17 persons receiving benefits pursuant to Section 49-101 of Title 11 of
18 the Oklahoma Statutes, any member of the Oklahoma Firefighters
19 Pension and Retirement System receiving benefits from the System as
20 of June 30, 2025, who continues to receive benefits on or after the
21 effective date of this act shall receive a two percent (2%) increase
22 in benefits.
23 B. 1. For any member of the System who joined prior to January
24 1, 1981, who shall have his or her pension or the pension of his or
Req. No. 1725 Page 1
RBS No. 1725
1 her surviving spouse increased or decreased by one-half (1/2) of all
2 increases or decreases which shall occur in the salary of a regular
3 firefighter in the municipality from which the member is receiving a
4 pension, any increase in benefits shall be used to offset the
5 increase provided in subsection A of this section.
6 2. For the purposes of this section, “regular firefighter”
7 means a salaried firefighter who has reached his or her maximum
8 salary as a private in his or her department and has not been
9 promoted to a position of rank.
10 C. Effective July 1, 2026, any persons receiving benefits
11 pursuant to Section 49-101 of Title 11 of the Oklahoma Statutes
12 shall receive an increase of two percent (2%) of the amount
13 prescribed in Section 49-143.7 of Title 11 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
