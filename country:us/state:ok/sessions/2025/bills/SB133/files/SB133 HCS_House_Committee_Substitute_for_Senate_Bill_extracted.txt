1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR ENGROSSED
5 and
6 Grego of the House
7
8
9 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
10 An Act relating to groundwater permits; amending 82
O.S. 2021, Section 1020.7, which relates to
11 groundwater permit applications; requiring certain
license be presented for persons intending to use
12 groundwater for medical marijuana grow facilities;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
13 emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 82 O.S. 2021, Section 1020.7, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 1020.7. Any person intending to use groundwater shall
20 make application apply to the Oklahoma Water Resources Board for an
21 appropriate permit as provided in Section 1020.11 of this title
22 before commencing any drilling for such purposes and before taking
23 water from any completed well heretofore drilled. Such application
24 to take and use groundwater shall be on a form provided by the Board
Req. No. 13541 Page 1
1 and pursuant to the rules and regulations established by the Board.
2 Any person intending to use groundwater for a medical marijuana grow
3 facility shall present to the Board a valid medical marijuana
4 commercial grower license issued by the Oklahoma Medical Marijuana
5 Authority pursuant to Section 427.14 of Title 63 of the Oklahoma
6 Statutes.
7 The application heretofore filed with the Board shall be used in
8 granting permits for existing wells and the Board shall publish the
9 notice of the hearing thereon.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
11 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
12 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
14 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
