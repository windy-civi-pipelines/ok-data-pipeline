1 WHEREAS, a wide diversity of birds in Oklahoma indicates a

3 WHEREAS, Oklahoma is part of the Central Flyway through which
4 approximately 400 different species of birds migrate during the
5 spring and fall; and
6 WHEREAS, the Oklahoma state bird, the scissor-tailed flycatcher,
7 is one of the many species of birds that migrate to our state to
8 nest and rear their young; and
9 WHEREAS, most birds migrate at night, navigate by using the
10 stars and moon, and can become disoriented by bright artificial
11 light and sky glow, resulting in exhaustion and deadly collisions

13 WHEREAS, collisions with buildings is one of the top causes of
14 mortality of birds in the United States; and
15 WHEREAS, the Lights Out Oklahoma program strategy is aimed to
16 persuade building owners and managers and homeowners to turn off
17 exterior lighting on all buildings at night during the high
18 migration intensity months of April, May, September, and October;
19 and
