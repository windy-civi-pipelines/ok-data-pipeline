14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
16 Department of Commerce in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st
17 Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of Five Hundred
18 Thousand Dollars ($500,000.00) shall be used to support the
19 Community Expansion of Nutrition Assistance Program.
20 SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
21 Department of Commerce in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st
22 Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Hundred
23 Thousand Dollars ($100,000.00) shall be used to provide support to
