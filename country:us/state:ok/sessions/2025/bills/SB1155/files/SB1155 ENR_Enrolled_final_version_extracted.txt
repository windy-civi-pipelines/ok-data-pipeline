Section 1:
ENROLLED SENATE
BILL NO. 1155 By: Hall and Haste of the
Senate
and
Caldwell (Trey) and Kane of
the House
An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Commerce; requiring that portions of certain
appropriated funds be used for certain purposes;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.
SUBJECT: Oklahoma Department of Commerce budget limits
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
Department of Commerce in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st
Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of Five Hundred
Thousand Dollars ($500,000.00) shall be used to support the
Community Expansion of Nutrition Assistance Program.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
Department of Commerce in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st
Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Hundred
Thousand Dollars ($100,000.00) shall be used to provide support to
an Oklahoma-based nonprofit in order to preserve sports heritage and
pride in Oklahoma.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
Department of Commerce in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st
Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Hundred
Ninety-eight Thousand Six Hundred Sixty Dollars ($198,660.00) shall
be used for community action agencies to support Head Start
programs, and shall not be used for administrative expenses.

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.

Section 6:
SECTION 5. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.

An Act
ENROLLED SENATE
BILL NO. 1155 By: Hall and Haste of the
Senate
and
Caldwell (Trey) and Kane of
the House
An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Commerce; requiring that portions of certain
appropriated funds be used for certain purposes;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.
SUBJECT: Oklahoma Department of Commerce budget limits
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
Department of Commerce in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st
Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of Five Hundred
Thousand Dollars ($500,000.00) shall be used to support the
Community Expansion of Nutrition Assistance Program.
SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
Department of Commerce in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st
Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Hundred
Thousand Dollars ($100,000.00) shall be used to provide support to
an Oklahoma-based nonprofit in order to preserve sports heritage and
pride in Oklahoma.
SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
Department of Commerce in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st
Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Hundred
Ninety-eight Thousand Six Hundred Sixty Dollars ($198,660.00) shall
be used for community action agencies to support Head Start
programs, and shall not be used for administrative expenses.

SECTION 4. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
SECTION 5. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
