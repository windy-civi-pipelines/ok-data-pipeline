1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
3 HOUSE JOINT
RESOLUTION 1016 By: Fugate
4
5
6
AS INTRODUCED
7
8 to refer to the people for their approval or
rejection a proposed amendment to Section 3 of
9 Article V of the Constitution of the State of
Oklahoma; modifying authority of the Legislature or
10 Governor with respect to special election dates for
referred measures; providing ballot title; and
11 directing filing.
12
13
14 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND THE SENATE OF THE
15 1ST SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
16 SECTION 1. The Secretary of State shall refer to the people for
17 their approval or rejection, as and in the manner provided by law,
18 the following proposed amendment to Section 3 of Article V of the
19 Constitution of the State of Oklahoma to read as follows:
20 Section 3. Referendum petitions shall be filed with the
21 Secretary of State not more than ninety (90) days after the final
22 adjournment of the session of the Legislature which passed the bill
23 on which the referendum is demanded. The veto power of the Governor
24 shall not extend to measures voted on by the people. All elections
Req. No. 10732 Page 1
1 on m easures referred to the people of the state shall be had at the
2 next election held throughout the state. Any measure referred to
3 the people by the initiative or referendum shall take effect and be
4 in force when it shall have been approved by a majority of the votes
5 cast thereon and not otherwise.
6 The style of all bills shall be: "Be it Enacted By the People
7 of the State of Oklahoma."
8 Petitions and orders for the initiative and for the referendum
9 shall be filed with the Secretary of State and addressed to the
10 Governor of the state, who shall submit the same to the people. The
11 Legislature shall make suitable provisions for carrying into effect
12 the provisions of this article.
13 SECTION 2. The Ballot Title for the proposed Constitutional
14 amendment as set forth in SECTION 1 of this resolution shall be in
15 the following form:
16 BALLOT TITLE
17 Legislative Referendum No. ____ State Question No. ____
18 THE GIST OF THE PROPOSITION IS AS FOLLOWS:
19 This measure amends the Oklahoma Constitution. It amends
20 Section 3 of Article 5. This section currently allows the
21 Legislature or Governor to set a special election date for
22 measures that are proposed by the people for a statewide vote.
23 This measure would remove the Legislature's and Governor's
24 ability to set those special election dates. All measures
Req. No. 10732 Page 2
1 proposed by the people would then appear on the next general
2 election ballot.
3 SHALL THE PROPOSAL BE APPROVED?
4 FOR THE PROPOSAL — YES _____________
5 AGAINST THE PROPOSAL — NO _____________
6 SECTION 3. The Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives,
7 immediately after the passage of this resolution, shall prepare and
8 file one copy thereof, including the Ballot Title set forth in
9 SECTION 2 hereof, with the Secretary of State and one copy with the
10 Attorney General.
