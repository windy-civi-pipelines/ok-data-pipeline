7 An Act relating to health insurance; amending 36 O.S.
2021, Section 6903.1, which relates to exemption of
8 certain domestic health maintenance organizations
from certain provisions of the Health Maintenance
9 Organization Act; updating exemptions; and providing
an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 36 O.S. 2021, Section 6903.1, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 6903.1. Notwithstanding any other provision of this
16 title:
17 1. Domestic health maintenance organizations that contract with
18 the Oklahoma Health Care Authority to provide basic health services
19 to Medicaid recipients and that do not provide basic health care
20 services to any other group of persons shall be are only subject to
21 the benefit coverage criteria and regulations of the Medicaid
22 program and are exempt from the following provisions of Sections
23 6911, 6914, 6915 and 6932 of this title:
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2052 Page 1
1 a. Sections 6058, 6058A, 6059, 6060, 6060.1, 6060.2,
2 6060.3, 6060.3a, 6060.4, 6060.4a, 6060.4b, 6060.5,
3 6060.5a, 6060.6, 6060.7, 6060.8, 6060.8a, 6060.8b,
4 6060.9, 6060.9a, 6060.9b, 6060.9c, and 6060.9d of this
5 title,
6 b. Section 6911 of this title, and
7 c. Section 6915 of this title;
8 2. The exemptions listed therein will apply only to the
9 services provided to Medicaid recipients pursuant to Section 4002.2
10 of Title 56 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
12 Passed the House of Representatives the 13th day of March, 2025.
