11 An Act relating to health insurance; amending 36 O.S.
2021, Section 6903.1, which relates to exemption of
12 certain domestic health maintenance organizations
from certain provisions of the Health Maintenance
13 Organization Act; updating exemptions; and providing
an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 36 O.S. 2021, Section 6903.1, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 6903.1. Notwithstanding any other provision of this
20 title:
21 1. Domestic health maintenance organizations that contract with
22 the Oklahoma Health Care Authority to provide basic health services
23 to Medicaid recipients and that do not provide basic health care
24 services to any other group of persons shall be are only subject to

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 the benefit coverage criteria and regulations of the Medicaid
2 program and are exempt from the following provisions of Sections
3 6911, 6914, 6915 and 6932 of this title:
4 a. Sections 6058, 6058A, 6059, 6060, 6060.1, 6060.2,
5 6060.3, 6060.3a, 6060.4, 6060.4a, 6060.4b, 6060.5,
6 6060.5a, 6060.6, 6060.7, 6060.8, 6060.8a, 6060.8b,
7 6060.9, 6060.9a, 6060.9b, 6060.9c, and 6060.9d of this
8 title,
9 b. Section 6911 of this title, and
10 c. Section 6915 of this title;
11 2. The exemptions listed therein will apply only to the
12 services provided to Medicaid recipients pursuant to Section 4002.2
13 of Title 56 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
