18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 12 O.S. 2021, Section 1764, is
20 amended to read as follows:
21 Section 1764. A fee of Forty-five Dollars ($45.00) Eighty-five
22 Dollars ($85.00) shall be charged and collected for the filing of
23 the affidavit for the commencement of any action for an amount of
24 Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000.00) or less. Any action in excess of

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000.00) shall be subject to the filing
2 fees provided in Title 28 of the Oklahoma Statutes for the same kind
3 of action as filed in district court. For the filing of any
4 counterclaim or setoff, fees shall be charged and collected pursuant
5 to Section 152.1 of Title 28 of the Oklahoma Statutes. Except as
6 otherwise provided in Section 1772 of this title, no other fee or
7 charge shall be collected by any officer for any service rendered
8 pursuant to the provisions of the Small Claims Procedure Act, or for
9 the taking of affidavits for use in connection with any action tried
10 pursuant to the provisions of the Small Claims Procedure Act. If
11 the affidavit and order are served by the sheriff or a licensed
12 private process server, the court clerk shall collect the usual fee
13 for the sheriff, which shall be taxed as costs in the case. The fee
14 paid to a licensed private process server, as approved by the court,
15 shall be taxed as additional costs in the case. After judgment, the
16 court clerk shall issue such process and shall be entitled to
17 collect only such fees and charges as are allowed by law for like
18 services in other actions. All fees collected as authorized by this
19 section and Section 1772 of this title shall be deposited with other
20 fees that are collected by the district court. Any statute
21 providing for an award of attorney fees shall be applicable to the
22 small claims division if the attorney makes an appearance in the
23 case, whether before or after judgment or on hearing for disclosure
24 of assets.
