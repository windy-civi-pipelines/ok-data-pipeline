9 An Act relating to motor vehicles; amending 47 O.S.
2021, Section 1-181, which relates to the definition
10 of transporter; modifying definition; updating
statutory language; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 47 O.S. 2021, Section 1-181, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 1-181. Transporter.
17 Every “Transporter” shall mean every person engaged in the
18 business of delivering vehicles of a type required to be registered
19 hereunder from a manufacturing, assembling, or distributing plant to
20 dealers or sales agents of a manufacturer or from the place of
21 business of a dealer, sales agent, or auto auction to a place of
22 business of the same or another dealer, sales agent, or auto
23 auction, or while engaged in the business of dealing vehicles to a
