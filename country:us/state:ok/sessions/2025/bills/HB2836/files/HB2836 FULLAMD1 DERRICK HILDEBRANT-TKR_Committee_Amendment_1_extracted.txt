COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB2836
Page 3 Section 2 Line 9
Of the Engrossed Bill
By inserting after the "." the following language:
"A veteran may voluntarily waive their right to free services and agree to
pay an upfront fee, provided the waiver is in writing, signed, and states
that the veteran understands free services are available through the
Oklahoma Department of Veterans Affairs and federally charted veterans'
service organizations and provided the upfront fee is refundable or paid
to an organization that has a minimum rating of an "A" from the Better
Business Bureau."
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Derrick
Hildebrant
