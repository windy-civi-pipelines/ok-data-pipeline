COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB2836
Page 3 Section 2 Lines 9
Of the Engrossed Bill
By inserting after the "." the following language:
”A veteran may voluntarily waive their right to free services and agree to
pay an upfront fee, provided the waiver is in writing, signed, and states
that the veteran understands free services are available through the
Oklahoma Department of Veterans Affairs and federally chartered veterans’
service organizations. The waiver must also acknowledge the veteran’s
choice to seek paid assistance and be retained by the service provider for
one (1) year for review by the Oklahoma Attorney General’s Office upon
request."
