10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1685, is
12 amended to read as follows:
13 Section 1685. A. Any person who shall knowingly deprive any
14 animal in subjugation or captivity, whether wild or tame, and
15 whether belonging to the person or to another, of necessary food,
16 drink, shelter, or veterinary care; or who shall cause, procure, or
17 permit any such animal to be deprived by another shall be guilty,
18 upon conviction, of a misdemeanor punishable by imprisonment in the
19 county jail for a term not to exceed one (1) year, by a fine not
20 more than One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00), or by both such
21 imprisonment and fine. Any animal so maltreated shall be considered
22 a neglected animal.
23 B. Any person who shall willfully or maliciously torture
24 knowingly, destroy, or kill, or cruelly beat or injure, maim, or

5 any such animal to be so tortured, destroyed, or killed, or cruelly

16 both such imprisonment and fine. Any animal so maltreated or abused
17 shall be considered an abused or neglected animal.
