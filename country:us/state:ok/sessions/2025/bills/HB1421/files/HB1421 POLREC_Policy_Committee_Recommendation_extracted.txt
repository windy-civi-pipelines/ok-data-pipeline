Section 2:
Section 30.8, which relates to animal shelter
10 operators and commercial pet breeders; changing
required notice time to be given to animal shelter
11 operators or commercial pet breeders by the Oklahoma
Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry prior
12 to their current license expiring; providing for
online renewals; providing timeline for implementing
13 online renewals; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 4 O.S. 2021, Section 30.8, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 30.8. A. An animal shelter operator or commercial pet
20 breeder who is not in violation of the Commercial Pet Breeders and
21 Animal Shelter Licensing Act or any rule adopted under the
22 Commercial Pet Breeders and Animal Shelter Licensing Act may renew
23 the license of the person by:
24
Req. No. 12889 Page 1
1 1. Submitting a renewal application to the Oklahoma Department
2 of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry on the form prescribed by the
3 Department;
4 2. Complying with any other renewal requirements adopted by the
5 Department; and
6 3. Paying the required fee.
7 B. Any person who fails to apply for a renewal in a manner
8 prescribed by the Department, and whose license has expired, may not
9 engage in activities that require a license until the license has
10 been renewed.
11 C. Not later than sixty (60) ninety (90) days before the
12 expiration of the license, the Department shall send written notice
13 of the impending license expiration to the animal shelter operator
14 or commercial pet breeder at the last-known address according to the
15 records of the Department.
16 D. The Department shall provide an online license renewal and
17 payment option by January 1, 2026.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
