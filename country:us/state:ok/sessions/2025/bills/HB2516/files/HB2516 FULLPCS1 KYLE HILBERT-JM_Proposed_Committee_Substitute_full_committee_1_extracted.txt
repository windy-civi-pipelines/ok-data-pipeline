7 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to military infrastructure funding;
creating the Base Infrastructure Needs and
9 Development - Schools Revolving Fund; establishing
the fund at the Oklahoma Military Department;
10 establishing revolving fund characteristics; deeming
funds appropriated; providing expenditure and
11 budgeting requirements and limitations; creating Base
Infrastructure Needs and Development – Schools
12 Program; establishing program at the Oklahoma
Military Department; authorizing certain utilizations
13 of funds; authorizing certain citations; providing
for codification; establishing an effective date; and
14 declaring an emergency.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 301B of Title 44, unless there
20 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
22 fund for the Oklahoma Military Department to be designated the "Base
23 Infrastructure Needs and Development - Schools Revolving Fund",
24 which may also be cited as "BIND – Schools Fund". The fund shall be
Req. No. 12342 Page 1
1 a co ntinuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall
2 consist of all monies directed for deposit to the fund by law. All
3 monies accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated
4 and may be budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma Military Department
5 for the purpose established in Subsection B of this section.
6 Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants issued by
7 the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with
8 the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
9 approval and payment.
10 B. There is hereby established within the Oklahoma Military
11 Department the "Base Infrastructure Needs and Development – Schools
12 Program", which may also be cited as the "BIND – Schools Program".
13 The Department shall utilize funds from the BIND – Schools Fund
14 created in Subsection A of this section, alone or in combination
15 with other available funding, to make infrastructure investments,
16 including, but not limited to, new construction, improving common
17 education facilities located on military bases in this state, that
18 will either:
19 1. Decrease the likelihood of military base and other military
20 infrastructure closures in this state, or
21 2. Increase the likelihood of military base and other military
22 infrastructure expansions in this state.
23 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
24
Req. No. 12342 Page 2
1 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
2 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
3 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
