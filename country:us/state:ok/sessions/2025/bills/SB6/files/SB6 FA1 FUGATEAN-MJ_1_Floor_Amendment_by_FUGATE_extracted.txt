SB6 FA1 FugateAn-MJ
5/2/2025 11:58:55 am
FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend SB6
Page 2 Section 1 Line 1
Of the Engrossed Bill
By striking the word "Tuesday" and inserting in lieu thereof the word
"Saturday".
On Page 2, Section 1, Line 1, by restoring the following language "of
April of each year" and deleting "after the first Monday of November any
year".
On Page 2, Section 1, Line 5, by inserting after the words "shall be
conducted on" the words "the first Saturday".
On Page 2, Section 1, Lines 5-8, by restoring the following stricken
language: "in February of each year, except in any year when a
Presidential Preferential Primary is held in February, then the election
shall be held on the same day as the Presidential Preferential Primary"
On Page 2, Section 1, Lines 8-9, by deleting the following language: "the
fourth Tuesday of August in any month".
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Andy Fugate
