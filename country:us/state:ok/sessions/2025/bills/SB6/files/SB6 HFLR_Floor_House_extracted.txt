15 elections; modifying dates for filing for certain

19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
20 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 13A-103, is
21 amended to read as follows:
22 Section 13A-103. A. 1. The general election of members of the
23 board of education of every school district and technology center
24
SB6 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 scho ol district shall be conducted on the first Tuesday of April of
2 each year after the first Monday of November any year.
3 2. The primary election of members of the board of education of
4 every school district and technology center school district, if
5 necessary, shall be conducted on the second Tuesday in February of
6 each year, except in any year when a Presidential Preferential
7 Primary is held in February, then the election shall be held on the
8 same day as the Presidential Preferential Primary the fourth Tuesday
9 of August in any month.
10 3. If only two candidates qualify to have their names appear on
11 the ballot, the names of both candidates shall appear on the ballot
12 at the board of education general election.
13 4. If more than two candidates qualify to have their names
14 appear on the ballot, the names of all such candidates shall appear
15 on the ballot at the board of education primary election. A
16 candidate receiving more than fifty percent (50%) of the votes cast
17 in the board of education primary election shall be elected to the
18 office. If no candidate receives more than fifty percent (50%) of
19 the votes cast in the board of education primary election, then the
20 two candidates with the highest number of votes shall appear on the
21 ballot at the board of education general election.
22 B. Elections on the question of making a levy or levies for
23 schools under Section 9, Section 9B or Section 10 of Article X of
24 the Oklahoma Constitution shall be held on the second Tuesday in

3 held on the same day as the Presidential Preferential Primary.
4 C. The board of education of every school district or
5 technology center school district may call a special election for
6 the purpose of voting on any matter or question authorized by law.
7 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 13A-105, is
8 amended to read as follows:
9 Section 13A-105. Candidates for member of the board of
10 education of every school district or technology center school
11 district shall file declarations of candidacy in the same place and
12 with the same officials as candidates for county office. The
13 declaration of candidacy to be signed by the candidate shall have an
14 attachment to be signed by the candidate listing the requirements of
15 a candidate for election or reelection to a school board as set
16 forth in Sections 13A-106 and 5-105a of this title and Sections 5-
17 110, 5-110.1, and 5-113 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes, and
18 the candidate shall swear or affirm that he or she is eligible to
19 run for the office or serve in the office if elected. Candidates
20 shall file no earlier than 8:00 a.m. on the first Monday Wednesday
21 in December April through no later than 5:00 p.m. on the following
22 Wednesday next succeeding Friday. For school districts and
23 technology center school districts located in more than one county,
