HOUSE BILL NO. 2674 By: Hilbert

8 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to state government; creating the
Statewide Official Compensation Commission; providing
10 for membership of commission; authorizing meetings
based on meetings of the Board on Legislative
11 Compensation; requiring Statewide Official
Compensation Commission to set salaries for certain
12 public officials; prohibiting modification of salary
amounts in contravention of Section 10 of Article
13 XXIII of the Oklahoma Constitution; amending 74 O.S.
2021, Section 250.4, which relates to state officer
14 salaries; providing for operative effect of salary
provisions; providing for effect of action taken by
15 Statewide Official Compensation Commission; repealing
74 O.S. 2021, Section 250.4, as amended by Section 2
16 of this act, which relates to public official
compensation; providing for codification; and
17 providing effective dates.
18
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
22 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 291.4 of Title 74, unless there
23 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
24
Req. No. 12672 Page 1
1 A. There is hereby created the Statewide Official Compensation
2 Commission.
3 B. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall consist
4 of the same persons who constitute the Board on Legislative
5 Compensation as provided by Section 21 of Article V of the Oklahoma
6 Constitution.
7 C. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall be
8 authorized to meet only on the same date as the Board on Legislative
9 Compensation.
10 D. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall set the
11 salary for each of the following elected officials:
12 1. The Governor;
13 2. The Lieutenant Governor;
14 3. The Attorney General;
15 4. The State Treasurer;
16 5. The State Auditor and Inspector;
17 6. The Superintendent of Public Instruction;
18 7. The State Insurance Commissioner;
19 8. The Commissioner of Labor; and
20 9. The members of the Oklahoma Corporation Commission.
21 E. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall not set
22 the salary for any of the officials identified in subsection D of
23 this section at an amount less than the salary in effect for such
24 officials as of January 1, 2025.
Req. No. 12672 Page 2
1 F. At its authorized meeting, the Statewide Official
2 Compensation Commission shall set the salary for each of the
3 officials identified in subsection D of this section for any term of
4 office for such officials beginning on or after the date as of which
5 the Commission sets the salary amount.
6 G. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall not
7 modify a salary amount in violation of the requirements of Section
8 10 of Article XXIII of the Oklahoma Constitution.
9 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 250.4, is
10 amended to read as follows:
11 Section 250.4. A. Pursuant to provisions of the Constitution
12 of the State of Oklahoma from and after the beginning date of a term
13 of office which commences in, or after, January 2016, and except as
14 otherwise provided by this section, the following officers of the
15 State of Oklahoma shall be annually compensated for their services,
16 payable monthly, as follows:
17 1. The Governor shall receive a salary of One Hundred Forty-
18 seven Thousand Dollars ($147,000.00);
19 2. The Lieutenant Governor shall receive a salary of One
20 Hundred Fourteen Thousand Seven Hundred Thirteen Dollars
21 ($114,713.00);
22 3. The Attorney General shall receive a salary of One Hundred
23 Thirty-two Thousand Eight Hundred Twenty-five Dollars ($132,825.00);
24
Req. No. 12672 Page 3
1 4. The State Superintendent of Public Instruction shall receive
2 a salary of One Hundred Twenty-four Thousand Three Hundred Seventy-
3 three Dollars ($124,373.00);
4 5. Each member of the Corporation Commission shall receive a
5 salary of One Hundred Fourteen Thousand Seven Hundred Thirteen
6 Dollars ($114,713.00);
7 6. The State Treasurer shall receive a salary of One Hundred
8 Fourteen Thousand Seven Hundred Thirteen Dollars ($114,713.00);
9 7. The State Auditor and Inspector shall receive a salary of
10 One Hundred Fourteen Thousand Seven Hundred Thirteen Dollars
11 ($114,713.00);
12 8. The State Insurance Commissioner shall receive a salary of
13 One Hundred Fourteen Thousand Seven Hundred Thirteen Dollars
14 ($114,713.00); and
15 9. The Commissioner of Labor shall receive a salary of One
16 Hundred Five Thousand Fifty-three Dollars ($105,053.00).
17 B. With respect to any public official whose salary is provided
18 for by this section, the provisions of this section shall be
19 operative until the Statewide Official Compensation Commission
20 modifies the salary amount for such official. If the Statewide
21 Official Compensation Commission modifies the salary amount as
22 otherwise prescribed by this section, the order of the Commission
23 shall supersede the provisions of this section with respect to the
24 appliable statewide elected official.
Req. No. 12672 Page 4
1 SECTION 3. REPEALER 74 O.S. 2021, Section 250.4, as
2 amended by Section 2 of this act, is hereby repealed.
3 SECTION 4. Sections 1 and 2 of this act shall become effective
4 November 1, 2025.
5 SECTION 5. Section 3 of this act shall become effective January
6 31, 2030.
