Section 1:
ENROLLED HOUSE
BILL NO. 2674 By: Hilbert of the House
and
Paxton of the Senate
An Act relating to state government; creating the
Statewide Official Compensation Commission; providing
for membership of commission; authorizing meetings
based on meetings of the Board on Legislative
Compensation; requiring Statewide Official
Compensation Commission to set salaries for certain
public officials; prohibiting modification of salary
amounts in contravention of Section 10 of Article
XXIII of the Oklahoma Constitution; amending 74 O.S.
2021, Section 250.4, which relates to state officer
salaries; providing for operative effect of salary
provisions; providing for effect of action taken by
Statewide Official Compensation Commission; repealing
74 O.S. 2021, Section 250.4, which relates to public
official compensation; providing for codification;
and providing effective dates.
SUBJECT: State government
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 291.4 of Title 74, unless there
is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
A. There is hereby created the Statewide Official Compensation
Commission.
B. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall consist
of the same persons who constitute the Board on Legislative
Compensation as provided by Section 21 of Article V of the Oklahoma
Constitution.
C. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall be
authorized to meet only on the same date as the Board on Legislative
Compensation.
D. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall set the
salary for each of the following elected officials:

Section 3:
1. The Governor;

Section 4:
2. The Lieutenant Governor;

Section 5:
3. The Attorney General;

Section 6:
4. The State Treasurer;

Section 7:
5. The State Auditor and Inspector;

Section 8:
6. The Superintendent of Public Instruction;

Section 9:
7. The State Insurance Commissioner;

Section 10:
8. The Commissioner of Labor; and

Section 11:
9. The members of the Oklahoma Corporation Commission.
E. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall not set
the salary for any of the officials identified in subsection D of
this section at an amount less than the salary in effect for such
officials as of January 1, 2025.
F. At its authorized meeting, the Statewide Official
Compensation Commission shall set the salary for each of the
officials identified in subsection D of this section for any term of
office for such officials beginning on or after the date as of which
the Commission sets the salary amount.
G. The Statewide Official Compensation Commission shall not
modify a salary amount in violation of the requirements of Section
10 of Article XXIII of the Oklahoma Constitution.

SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 250.4, is
