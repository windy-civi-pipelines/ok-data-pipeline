10 An Act relating to legal tender; amending 62 O.S.
2021, Section 4500, which relates to Federal gold and
11 silver coins as Oklahoma legal tender; modifying
language; defining terms; providing explanation of
12 specie or legal tender; clarifying characterization
of specie or legal tender for tax and regulation
13 purposes; directing that certain exchanges shall not
result in tax liability; directing that purchase,
14 sale, or exchange of specie shall not result in tax
liability; stipulating specie cannot be compelled as
15 tender unless under law or contract; directing
Attorney General's office to enforce; directing
16 Oklahoma courts on adjudication in certain cases;
permitting specie to be recognized to pay debts; and
17 providing an effective date.
18
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 62 O.S. 2021, Section 4500, is
22 amended to read as follows:
23 Section 4500. Gold and silver coins issued by the United States
24 government are legal tender in the State of Oklahoma. No person may
HB1199 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 comp el another person to tender or accept gold or silver coins that
2 are issued by the United States government, except as agreed upon by
3 contract.
4 A. As used in this act:
5 1. "Legal tender" means a recognized medium of exchange for the
6 payment of debts and taxes; and
7 2. "Specie" means:
8 a. coin having gold or silver content, or
9 b. refined gold or silver bullion that is:
10 (1) coined, stamped, or imprinted with its weight,
11 and
12 (2) valued primarily based on its metal content and
13 not its form.
14 B. Specie or legal tender shall consist of:
15 1. Specie coin issued by the United States government; or
16 2. Other specie that an Oklahoma court rules to be within state
17 authority to make or designate as legal tender.
18 C. Specie or legal tender shall not be characterized as
19 personal property for taxation or regulatory purposes.
20 D. 1. The exchange of one type or form of legal tender for
21 another type or form of legal tender shall not give rise to any tax
22 liability.
23 2. The purchase, sale, or exchange of any type or form of
24 specie shall not give rise to any tax liability.
HB1199 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 E. Unless specifically provided by law or by contract, a person
2 shall not compel another person to tender specie or to accept specie
3 as legal tender.
4 F. The Office of the Attorney General shall enforce this act
5 without prejudice to a private right of action.
6 G. If a valid contract expressly designates a type or form of
7 specie as tender, then an Oklahoma court asked to adjudicate the
8 breach of such a contract shall require, as a remedy for the breach,
9 the specific performance of tendering the type or form of specie
10 specified in the contract.
11 H. Specie may be recognized to pay debts.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
13
