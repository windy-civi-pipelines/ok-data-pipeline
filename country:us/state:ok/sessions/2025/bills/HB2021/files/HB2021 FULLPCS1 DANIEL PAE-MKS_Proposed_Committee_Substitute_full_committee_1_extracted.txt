8 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to schools; creating the Oklahoma
Kids After-School Grant Program (OKAGP); locating the
10 program at the Department of Human Services;
providing parameters on entities eligible to apply;
11 citing certain exemptions; authorizing the
promulgation of rules; creating the Oklahoma Kids
12 After-School Grant Program Revolving Fund;
establishing revolving fund characteristics;
13 declaring funds appropriated; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10001 of Title 70, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Oklahoma Kids
21 After-School Grant Program" (OKAGP).
22 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
23 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10002 of Title 70, unless there
24 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
Req. No. 12455 Page 1
1 There is hereby created at the Department of Human Services the
2 Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant Program (OKAGP). This grant
3 program shall be for non-religious community-based organizations
4 that run after-school programs for children grades kindergarten
5 through twelve. These entities may qualify for an exemption from
6 the Oklahoma Child Care Facilities Licensing Act based on the listed
7 exemptions found in Section 403 of Title 10 of the Oklahoma
8 Statutes. These entities must also have a minimum of five (5)
9 locations in Oklahoma, in which they operate themselves or through
10 an alliance. The Department of Human Services may promulgate rules
11 to implement this grant program.
12 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10003 of Title 70, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 SECTION 4. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a
16 revolving fund for the Department of Human Services to be designated
17 the "Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant Program Revolving Fund". The
18 fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year
19 limitations, and shall consist of all monies received by the
20 Department of Human Services from appropriations or donations. All
21 monies accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated
22 and may be budgeted and expended by the Department for the purpose
23 of awarding grants under the Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant
24 Program. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants
Req. No. 12455 Page 2
1 issu ed by the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by
2 law with the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise
3 Services for approval and payment.
4 SECTION 5. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
