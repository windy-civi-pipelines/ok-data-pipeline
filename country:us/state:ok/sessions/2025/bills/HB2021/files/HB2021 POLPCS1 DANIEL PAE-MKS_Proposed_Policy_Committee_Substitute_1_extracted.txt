8 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to schools; creating the Oklahoma
Kids After-School Grant Program (OKAGP); locating the
10 program at the Department of Human Services;
providing parameters on entities eligible to apply;
11 citing certain exemptions; authorizing the
promulgation of rules; creating the Oklahoma Kids
12 After-School Grant Program Revolving Fund;
establishing revolving fund characteristics;
13 declaring funds appropriated; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10001 of Title 70, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Oklahoma Kids
21 After-School Grant Program" (OKAGP).
22 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
23 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10002 of Title 70, unless there
24 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
Req. No. 12610 Page 1
1 There is hereby created at the Department of Human Services the
2 Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant Program (OKAGP). This grant
3 program shall be for community-based organizations that run after-
4 school programs for children grades kindergarten through twelve.
5 These entities may qualify for an exemption from the Oklahoma Child
6 Care Facilities Licensing Act based on the listed exemptions found
7 in Section 403 of Title 10 of the Oklahoma Statutes. These entities
8 must also have a minimum of five locations in Oklahoma, in which
9 they operate themselves or through an alliance. The Department of
10 Human Services may promulgate rules to implement this grant program.
11 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10003 of Title 70, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
15 for the Department of Human Services to be designated the "Oklahoma
16 Kids After-School Grant Program Revolving Fund". The fund shall be
17 a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall
18 consist of all monies received by the Department of Human Services
19 from appropriations or donations. All monies accruing to the credit
20 of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
21 expended by the Department for the purpose of awarding grants under
22 the Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant Program. Expenditures from
23 said fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
24 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
Req. No. 12610 Page 2
1 Offi ce of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
2 payment.
3 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
