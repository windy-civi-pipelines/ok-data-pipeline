24
Req. No. 13605 Page 1
1 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
2 fund for the Oklahoma Department of Commerce Workforce Commission to
3 be designated the "Workforce Coordination Revolving Fund". The fund
4 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
5 and shall consist of all monies directed for deposit by law from the
6 Statewide Recovery Fund in Section 255 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma
7 Statutes. All monies accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby
8 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Commission for
9 the purpose of creating a workforce data integration system,
10 providing grants for critical workforce needs, and administering
11 these projects. No funds shall be used for expenses that violate
12 the federal guidelines for the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021,
13 Public Law No. 117-2, or be used for projects not in accordance with
14 policy enacted under the efforts of the Education and Economic
15 Development Working Group within the Joint Committee on Pandemic
16 Relief Funding. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon
17 warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
18 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
19 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
20 B. Any funds remaining in the Workforce Coordination Revolving
21 Fund as of July 1, 2024, are hereby considered obligated by the
22 Oklahoma Legislature for the purpose of workforce development
23 initiatives.
24
Req. No. 13605 Page 2
1 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
2 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
3 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
