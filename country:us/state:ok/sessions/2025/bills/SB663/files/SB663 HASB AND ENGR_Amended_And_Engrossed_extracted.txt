ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 663 Page 1
1 BE I T ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
2 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 3, Chapter 22, 2nd
3 Extraordinary Session, O.S.L. 2022, as amended by Section 1, Chapter
4 437, O.S.L. 2024 (62 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 255.4), is amended to
5 read as follows:
6 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
7 fund for the Oklahoma Department of Commerce Workforce Commission to
8 be designated the "Workforce Coordination Revolving Fund". The fund
9 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
10 and shall consist of all monies directed for deposit by law from the
11 Statewide Recovery Fund in Section 255 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma
12 Statutes. All monies accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby
13 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Commission for
14 the purpose of creating a workforce data integration system,
15 providing grants for critical workforce needs, and administering
16 these projects. No funds shall be used for expenses that violate
17 the federal guidelines for the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021,
18 Public Law No. 117-2, or be used for projects not in accordance with
19 policy enacted under the efforts of the Education and Economic
20 Development Working Group within the Joint Committee on Pandemic
21 Relief Funding. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon
22 warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
23 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
24 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 663 Page 2
1 B. Any funds remaining in the Workforce Coordination Revolving
2 Fund as of July 1, 2024, are hereby considered obligated by the
3 Oklahoma Legislature for the purpose of workforce development
4 initiatives.
5 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
6 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
7 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
8 be in full force from and after its passage and approval."
9 Passed the House of Representatives the 6th day of May, 2025.
