12 WHEREAS, there are approximately 800,000 Oklahoma residents with
13 Medicare coverage; and
14 WHEREAS, the Oklahoma House of Representatives wants fair

17 insurance; and

1 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
2 OF THE 1ST SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
3 THAT the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services require that
4 Medicare Part C reimburse providers at the same standard rate as
5 Medicare Part A and B for all services.
