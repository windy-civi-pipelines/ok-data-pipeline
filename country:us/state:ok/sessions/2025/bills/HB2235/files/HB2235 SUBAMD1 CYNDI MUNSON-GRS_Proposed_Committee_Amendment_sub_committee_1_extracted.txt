Section 1:
HB2235 SUBAMD1 Cyndi Munson-GRS
2/17/2025 2:36:06 pm
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB2235
Page 14 Section 2 Lines 7
Of the Engrossed Bill
By inserting after the period "." the following language: "The Legislature
shall appropriate funds to the Tort Claims Liability Revolving Fund for
costs associated with providing group health benefit plan coverage by the
Department to a claimant under the provisions of this paragraph. The
Department may seek reimbursement from the Tort Claims Liability Revolving
Fund for all expenditures related to providing said coverage.";
Page 16, Section 3, Lines 7-8: By deleting all language beginning with the
comma "," on Line 7 through the word "writing," on Line 8;
Page 16, Section 3, Lines 9-10: By deleting all language beginning with
the word "Guidance" on Line 9 through the letter "B" on Line 10 and
inserting in lieu thereof, the words "A copy";
Page 16, Section 3, Line 10: By inserting after the word "Statutes" the
following language: "which sets forth the extent of liability of the state
and its political subdivisions for wrongful criminal felony convictions
and imprisonment pursuant to the Governmental Tort Claims Act; and
Page 16, Section 3, Lines 13-15: By deleting all language beginning with
the word "wrongfully" on Line 13 through the word "Act" on Line 15 and
inserting in lieu thereof, the following language: "persons upon release
from a penal institution".
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Cyndi Munson
Adopted: _____________________________
