8 An Act relating to higher education; providing
legislative intent; amending 70 O.S. 2021, Sections
9 3213.1, 3405.1, 4662, 4665, 4667, and 4668, which

16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
18 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
19 The Legislature encourages the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher
20 Education to exercise all powers necessary or convenient to
21 accomplish the purposes of Article XIII-A of the Oklahoma
22 Constitution and recommends that the State Regents allow up to one
23 change of function for each institution within The Oklahoma State
24 System of Higher Education. Exceptions to functions and associated
