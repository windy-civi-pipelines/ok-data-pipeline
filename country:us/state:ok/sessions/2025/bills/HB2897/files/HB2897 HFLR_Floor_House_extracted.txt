11 An Act relating to medical marijuana; requiring the
display of certain signage in dispensaries and
12 businesses; providing contents and requirements of
sign; requiring owners pay cost of signs; providing
13 for codification; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 427.30 of Title 63, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. Every medical marijuana dispensary or any place medical
21 marijuana or tetrahydrocannabinol (THC) products are sold shall post
22 and display at all times a printed sign, with a minimum height of
23 eight and one-half (8 1/2) inches and a minimum width of eleven (11)
24 inches with a font size of no less than 48 points for the text, near
HB2897 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 the cash register or place of checkout and at least two other highly
2 visible places in the store, which shall read as follows:
3 WARNING: INGESTING THC PRODUCTS WHILE PREGNANT IS NOT HEALTHY
4 FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF AN UNBORN CHILD.
5 B. The signage shall be produced at the expense of the owner of
6 the business establishment.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
8
