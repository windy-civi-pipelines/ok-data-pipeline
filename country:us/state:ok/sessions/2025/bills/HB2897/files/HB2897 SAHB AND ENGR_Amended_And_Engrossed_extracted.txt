7 An Act relating to medical marijuana; requiring the
display of certain signage in dispensaries and
8 businesses; providing contents and requirements of
sign; requiring owners pay cost of signs; providing
9 for codification; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 AMENDMENT NO. 1. Page 1, strike the title, enacting clause and
entire bill and insert
13
14 “An Act relating to medical marijuana; requiring
display of certain signage in licensed medical
15 marijuana dispensary; providing certain contents and
requirements for certain signage; requiring licensee
16 to pay cost of signage; providing for codification;
and providing an effective date.
17
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
20 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
21 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 427.18a of Title 63, unless
22 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
23 A. Every licensed medical marijuana dispensary shall post and
24 display at all times a printed sign with the minimum height of eight
ENGR. S. A. TO ENGR. H. B. NO. 2897 Page 1
1 and one-half (8 1/2) inches and a minimum width of eleven (11)
2 inches, with at least a 48-point font for the text, near the cash
3 register or place of checkout and at least two other highly visible
4 places in the medical marijuana dispensary. The sign shall read as
5 follows:
6 “WARNING: INGESTING THC PRODUCTS WHILE PREGNANT IS NOT HEALTHY
7 FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF AN UNBORN CHILD.”
8 B. The signage shall be produced at the expense of the medical
9 marijuana dispensary licensee.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.”
11 Passed the Senate the 28th day of April, 2025.
