Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to property; limiting total land
owned or encumbered in each county by certain
8 parties; providing for calculation of ownership;
prohibiting the recording of instruments that would
9 violate limit; providing exceptions; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1501 of Title 60, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. The State of Oklahoma shall limit the amount of land owned
17 by the State of Oklahoma, state agencies, and the federal
18 government, and shall limit the amount of land encumbered by any
19 easements, including, but not limited to, conservation easements to
20 no more than ten percent (10%) of the total land of each county in
21 the state.
22 B. The percentage of land owned by the parties mentioned in
23 subsection A of this section shall be calculated by the county clerk
24 of each county in this state. County clerks shall refuse to record
Req. No. 10290 Page 1
1 any instrument which would cause more than ten percent (10%) of the
2 total land in the county to be owned by or encumbered by parties
3 mentioned in subsection A of this section.
4 C. The following are exempt from subsection A of this act:
5 1. Federal military bases;
6 2. Flood control lakes or U.S. Army Corps of Engineers
7 property; and
8 3. Temporary county real estate holdings that were acquired by
9 the county for nonpayment of taxes.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
