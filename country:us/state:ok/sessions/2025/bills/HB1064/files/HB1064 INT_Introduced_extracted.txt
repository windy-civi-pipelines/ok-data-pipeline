Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to property; defining term;
prohibiting certain entities from acquiring title to
8 single-family residential real property; prohibiting
subsidiary entities from acquiring title to single-
9 family residential real property; requiring
divestiture of title within certain prescribed period
10 of time; providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1501 of Title 60, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. As used in this section, "hedge fund" means a lawfully
19 organized business entity, including but not limited to a general
20 partnership, limited partnership, limited liability partnership,
21 corporation, limited liability company or other entity that
22 accumulates capital primarily for the purpose of investing such
23 capital in equity securities of for profit business enterprises, in
24 any form, with the objective of achieving high rates of return for
Req. No. 12130 Page 1
1 the investors of the hedge fund. For purposes of this section,
2 "hedge fund" does not mean a private or public pension fund, an
3 insurance company, banks or other federally or state regulated
4 financial institutions the primary business activity of which does
5 not consist of equity investing, or any other business enterprise
6 the principal purpose of which is something other than investment of
7 accumulated or invested capital in the equity securities or
8 derivatives of equity securities of a for profit business
9 enterprise. For purposes of this section, if a lawfully recognized
10 business entity is exempt from federal securities regulation
11 pursuant to Regulation D of the United States Securities and
12 Exchange Commission, there shall be a rebuttable presumption that
13 such entity is a hedge fund within the meaning of this section.
14 B. No hedge fund shall acquire title to single family
15 residential property located within the state.
16 C. No subsidiary of a hedge fund shall acquire title to single
17 family residential property located within the state.
18 D. If a hedge fund or a subsidiary of a hedge fund holds title
19 to any single family residential real property located in the state
20 as of the effective date of this act, the entity holding title to
21 such property shall sell or otherwise divest itself of the title not
22 later than ten (10) years from the effective date of this act. No
23 transfer required by the provisions of this subsection shall be made
24 to a hedge fund or to a subsidiary of a hedge fund.
Req. No. 12130 Page 2
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
