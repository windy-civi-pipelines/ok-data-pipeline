9 An Act relating to the Rural Economic Action Plan of
1996; requiring certain entities to develop a plan to
10 measure qualitative effects of certain projects;
requiring submission of certain report; requiring use
11 of certain payments for certain expenditures;
providing for codification; and providing an
12 effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2014 of Title 62, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. For entities obtaining funding pursuant to subsection G of
20 Section 2003 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes and subsections A
21 and B of Section 2007 of Title 62 the Oklahoma Statutes, each entity
22 shall develop a plan to measure the qualitative effects of projects
23 funded through the Rural Economic Action Plan of 1996. The plan may
24
SB473 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 util ize inquiries or surveys of the public, local governments, or
2 municipalities to measure the effects.
3 B. On or before January 1, 2028, each entity required to
4 develop a plan pursuant to subsection A of this section shall submit
5 a report to the Oklahoma Department of Commerce detailing the
6 qualitative effects of at least two projects that received funds
7 pursuant to the Rural Economic Action Plan of 1996.
8 C. Any expenditures associated with creating the plan required
9 by subsection A of this section and the report required by
10 subsection B of this section shall be made using the initial
11 planning expenditure payments authorized pursuant to subsection C of
12 Section 2011 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
