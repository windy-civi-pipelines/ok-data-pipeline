An Act
ENROLLED SENATE
BILL NO. 473 By: Rader of the Senate
and
Roberts of the House
An Act relating to the Rural Economic Action Plan of
1996; requiring certain entities to develop a plan to
measure qualitative effects of certain projects;
requiring submission of certain report; requiring use
of certain payments for certain expenditures;
providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
SUBJECT: Rural Economic Action Plan of 1996
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2014 of Title 62, unless there
is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
A. For entities obtaining funding pursuant to subsection G of
Section 2003 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes and subsections A
and B of Section 2007 of Title 62 the Oklahoma Statutes, each entity
shall develop a plan to measure the qualitative effects of projects
funded through the Rural Economic Action Plan of 1996. The plan may
utilize inquiries or surveys of the public, local governments, or
municipalities to measure the effects.
B. On or before January 1, 2028, each entity required to
develop a plan pursuant to subsection A of this section shall submit
a report to the Oklahoma Department of Commerce detailing the
qualitative effects of at least two projects that received funds
pursuant to the Rural Economic Action Plan of 1996.

C. Any expenditures associated with creating the plan required
by subsection A of this section and the report required by
subsection B of this section shall be made using the initial
planning expenditure payments authorized pursuant to subsection C of
Section 2011 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
