1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to professions and occupations;
modifying the Oklahoma Administrative Code; modifying
9 requirements for funeral establishment vendor payment
rules; providing for codification; and providing an
10 effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 397 of Title 59, unless there is
16 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 This section shall modify the Oklahoma Administrative Code
18 Section 235:10-11-1 by the following provisions:
19 1. Penalties for vendor payments may only be imposed after a
20 formal court judgment;
21 2. Penalties may only be imposed on funeral establishments and
22 may not be imposed against the Funeral Director in Charge;
23 3. These rules are only applicable to active funeral
24 establishments;
Req. No. 13292 Page 1
1 4. All funeral establishments must be in compliance with
2 federal bankruptcy laws; and
3 5. All cash payments made in advance must meet the requirements
4 of consumer fund receipts.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
