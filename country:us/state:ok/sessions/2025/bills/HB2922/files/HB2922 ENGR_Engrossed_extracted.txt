7 An Act relating to professions and occupations;
modifying the Oklahoma Administrative Code; modifying
8 requirements for funeral establishment vendor payment
rules; providing for codification; and providing an
9 effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 397 of Title 59, unless there is
15 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 This section shall modify the Oklahoma Administrative Code
17 Section 235:10-11-1 by the following provisions:
18 1. Penalties for vendor payments may only be imposed after a
19 formal court judgment;
20 2. Penalties may only be imposed on funeral establishments and
21 may not be imposed against the Funeral Director in Charge;
22 3. These rules are only applicable to active funeral
23 establishments;
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2922 Page 1
1 4. All funeral establishments must be in compliance with
2 federal bankruptcy laws; and
3 5. All cash payments made in advance must meet the requirements
4 of consumer fund receipts.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
6 Passed the House of Representatives the 24th day of March, 2025.
