7 An Act relating to child custody; requiring notice to
custodial parent of in-patient treatment of
8 noncustodial parent; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
9
10
11
12 AMENDMENT NO. 1. Strike the title, enacting clause, and entire bill
and insert:
13
14
15 "An Act relating to child custody; requiring notice
to custodial parent by noncustodial parent; allowing
16 noncustodial parents the authority to deny disclosing
reasons for noncompliance; providing an exception;
17 providing for charges of indirect contempt of court
in certain circumstances; providing for codification;
18 and providing an effective date.
19
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
22 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
23 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4-107b of Title 43A, unless
24 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 970 Page 1
1 A custodial parent shall receive notice from the noncustodial
2 parent, or the noncustodial parent’s representative, if the
3 noncustodial parent is unable to comply with court-ordered
4 visitation or any other court-ordered requirement for any reason.
5 Unless ordered otherwise by the court, the noncustodial parent shall
6 not be required to disclose the reason the parent is unable to
7 comply. A noncustodial parent who violates the provisions of this
8 section shall be in indirect contempt of court.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025."
10 Passed the House of Representatives the 6th day of May, 2025.
