Resolution
ENROLLED HOUSE
CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION NO. 1005 By: Townley of the House
and
Thompson of the Senate
A Concurrent Resolution recognizing and declaring
February 13, 2025, as Oklahoma Museum Day.
WHEREAS, there are five hundred eighteen (518) museums in
Oklahoma; and
WHEREAS, Oklahoma museums promote lifelong learning for
Oklahomans; and
WHEREAS, Oklahoma museums directly contribute nearly a half a
billion dollars annually to the Oklahoma economy; and
WHEREAS, Oklahoma museums host over six million visitors
annually.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
OF THE 1ST SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING THEREIN:
THAT the Oklahoma House of Representatives and Oklahoma State
Senate do hereby recognize and declare February 13, 2025, to be
Oklahoma Museum Day.

Adopted by the House of Representatives the 13th day of
