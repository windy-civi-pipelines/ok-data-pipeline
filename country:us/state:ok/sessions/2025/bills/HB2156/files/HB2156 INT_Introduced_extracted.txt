Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to property; providing setback
requirements for solar energy facilities; providing
8 for codification and providing an effective date.
9
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1501 of Title 60, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 Solar energy facilities, excluding residential solar energy
17 panels, shall have the following setback requirements:
18 1. Shall not be located within five hundred (500) feet of an
19 occupied structure or occupied residence unless waived in writing by
20 the owner of the structure or residence;
21 2. Shall not be located within one hundred (100) feet of any
22 outer boundary of the facility property;
23 3. Shall not be located within one hundred (100) feet from any
24 public road right-of-way.
Req. No. 11594 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
