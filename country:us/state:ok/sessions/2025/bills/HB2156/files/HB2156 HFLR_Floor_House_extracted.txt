11 An Act relating to property; providing setback
requirements for solar energy and industrial battery
12 storage facilities; providing when setback
requirements shall apply; providing for codification;
13 and declaring an emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1501 of Title 60, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. Solar energy and industrial battery storage facilities,
21 excluding residential solar energy panels, shall have the following
22 setback requirements:
23
24
HB2156 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 1. Shall not be located within five hundred (500) feet of an
2 occupied structure or occupied residence unless waived in writing by
3 the owner of the structure or residence;
4 2. Shall not be located within fifty (50) feet of any outer
5 boundary of the facility property;
6 3. Shall not be located within one hundred (100) feet from the
7 centerline of any public road; and
8 4. Shall not be located within a half (1/2) mile from any
9 public school or hospital.
10 B. This act shall apply to 10 megawatt and larger solar energy
11 and industrial battery storage facilities. Furthermore, this act
12 shall apply to any project in development that does not yet have a
13 power purchase agreement in place on or after the effective date of
14 this act.
15 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
16 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
18 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
19
20 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON ENERGY AND NATURAL RESOURCES
OVERSIGHT, dated 03/05/2025 - DO PASS, As Amended.
