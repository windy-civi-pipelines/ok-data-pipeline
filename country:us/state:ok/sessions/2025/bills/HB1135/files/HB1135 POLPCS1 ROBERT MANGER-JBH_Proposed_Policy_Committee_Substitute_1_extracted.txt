7 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Weigh Station Act of
2012; amending 47 O.S. 2021, Section 1202, which
9 relates to interagency agreements; updating statutory
language; modifying conditions for operation of ports
10 of entry and weigh stations; modifying certain
requirements for fixed facilities; authorizing
11 certain officers to request roadside enforcement
assistance from the Corporation Commission; allowing
12 the Corporation Commission to perform roadside
enforcement if certain condition is met; and
13 providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 47 O.S. 2021, Section 1202, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 1202. A. The Department of Transportation, the
20 Oklahoma Turnpike Authority and the Corporation Commission may enter
21 into interagency agreements concerning the equipment, maintenance
22 and operations of fixed facilities.
23 B. The Department of Transportation, the Authority and the
24 Commission shall endeavor to electronically upgrade weigh stations
Req. No. 12239 Page 1
1 as p ractical to minimize the duplication of inspections for
2 compliant commercial motor vehicles and motor carriers.
3 C. The Commission shall operate all current and future ports of
4 entry and weigh stations eighteen (18) to twenty (20) hours a day
5 and seven (7) days a week upon the availability of funds funding and
6 sufficient staff.
7 D. The Commission shall continue to conduct roadside
8 enforcement in the general area where a fixed facility is planned
9 but no fixed facility currently exists until a fixed facility is
10 located in the general area or July 1, 2016, whichever is earlier.
11 E. When a fixed facility is located in the general area,
12 Commission motor carrier and commercial motor vehicle enforcement
13 shall be limited to the fixed facility and a radius surrounding the
14 facility. If the fixed facility is a weigh station as defined in
15 Section 1201 of this title, the applicable radius shall be seven (7)
16 miles. If the fixed facility is a port of entry weigh station as
17 defined in Section 1201 of this title, the applicable radius shall
18 be twenty-five (25) miles.
19 F. The Commission may assist in perform roadside enforcement in
20 a joint effort at the request of the Oklahoma Highway Patrol, a
21 county sheriff, or a county commissioner who requests assistance
22 with roadside enforcement within his or her county.
23 G. E. The Commission is authorized to conduct audits, reviews,
24 investigations, inspections, roadside enforcement, or other
Req. No. 12239 Page 2
1 enfo rcement actions by enforcement officers, provided those
2 activities are within the scope of the Commission's jurisdiction and
3 are not conducted as roadside enforcement in accordance with the
4 provisions of the Oklahoma Weigh Station Act of 2012 that ports of
5 entry and weigh stations remain operational.
6 H. F. The Commission may enter into interagency cooperative
7 agreements with other state or federal agencies to jointly enforce
8 federal and state laws or rules.
9 I. G. North American Standard Inspections inspections shall be
10 conducted only by individuals holding certification in the level or
11 classification of inspection being conducted.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
