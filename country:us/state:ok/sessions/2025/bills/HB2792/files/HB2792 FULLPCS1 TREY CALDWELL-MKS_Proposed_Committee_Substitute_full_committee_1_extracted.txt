13 An Act relating to the Progressing Rural Economic

23 follows:

Req. No. 13736 Page 1
1 Section 256. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
2 a special fund to be designated the "Progressing Rural Economic
3 Prosperity Fund", also known as the "PREP Fund". The fund shall be
4 a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall
5 consist of all monies received by the Progressing Rural Economic
6 Prosperity Fund from appropriations. Said fund shall be subject to
7 legislative appropriation or transfer as provided by law and shall
8 consist of all such monies as the Legislature may direct to be
9 appropriated or transferred to said fund. Expenditures from said
10 fund pursuant to subsection B of this section shall be made upon
11 warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
12 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
13 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
14 B. The following projects shall continue to receive the benefit
15 of funds originally appropriated in amounts from the Progressing
16 Rural Economic Prosperity Fund that may have lapsed, less any amount
17 that has been expended upon the effective date of this act:
18 1. Twenty-five Million Dollars ($25,000,000.00) appropriated by
19 Section 1 of Enrolled House Bill No. 1019 of the 2nd Extraordinary
20 Session of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
21 2. One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) appropriated by Section
22 2 of Enrolled House Bill No. 1019 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
23 of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;

1 3. Twenty Million Dollars ($20,000,000.00) appropriated by

5 Section 3 of Enrolled House Bill No. 1016 of the 2nd Extraordinary

14 7. Fourteen Million Dollars ($14,000,000.00) appropriated by
15 Section 6 of Enrolled House Bill No. 1016 of the 2nd Extraordinary
