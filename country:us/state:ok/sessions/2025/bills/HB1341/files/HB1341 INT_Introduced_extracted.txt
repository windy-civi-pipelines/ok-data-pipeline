7 AS INTRODUCED
8 An Act relating to the Office of Management and
Enterprise Services; directing for the dissolution of
9 OMES; creating the ODIN Task Force; providing
membership of task force; directing the task force to
10 provide recommendations; directing the Oklahoma House
of Representatives to provide staff; providing for
11 codification; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.
12
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 840-1A of Title 74, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. The Office of Management and Enterprise Services (OMES)
21 shall be dissolved effective January 1, 2026, and duties reassigned
22 to other state agencies.
23 B. There is hereby created until January 1, 2026, in accordance
24 with the provisions of the Oklahoma Sunset law, the OMES Duties
Req. No. 10778 Page 1
1 Inte rvention Now (ODIN) Task Force for the purpose of collaborative
2 research, planning, and providing information to the Legislature and
3 the Governor on how best to reassign the duties of OMES.
4 C. The ODIN Task Force shall consist of fifteen (15) members:
5 1. Three members appointed by the Speaker of the Oklahoma House
6 of Representatives;
7 2. Three members appointed by the President Pro Tempore of the
8 Oklahoma State Senate;
9 3. Three members appointed by the Governor;
10 4. The Executive Director of OMES;
11 5. The Deputy Director and Chief Transformation Office, of
12 OMES;
13 6. The Deputy Director of Legislative and Legal Services of
14 OMES;
15 7. The State Chief Financial Officer;
16 8. The State Comptroller; and
17 9. OMES General Counsel.
18 D. The ODIN Task Force shall provide its recommendations to the
19 Legislature and the Governor by November 1, 2025.
20 E. The Oklahoma House of Representatives shall provide staff to
21 assist in meetings of the ODIN Task Force as needed.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
23 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
24 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
Req. No. 10778 Page 2
1 decl ared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
2 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
