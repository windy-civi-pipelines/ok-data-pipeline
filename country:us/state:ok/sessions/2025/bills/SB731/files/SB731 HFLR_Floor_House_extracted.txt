9 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Evidence Code;
amending 12 O.S. 2021, Section 2803.1, as last
10 amended by Section 1, Chapter 104, O.S.L. 2024 (12
O.S. Supp. 2024, Section 2803.1), which relates to
11 hearsay; modifying certain hearsay exception to
include statements by vulnerable adults; and
12 providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 12 O.S. 2021, Section 2803.1, as
17 last amended by Section 1, Chapter 104, O.S.L. 2024 (12 O.S. Supp.
18 2024, Section 2803.1), is amended to read as follows:
19 Section 2803.1. A. The following statements are admissible in
20 juvenile deprived proceedings and pre-trial and post-trial criminal
21 and juvenile delinquent proceedings including preliminary hearings,
22 prosecutive merit hearings, and hearings on the revocation of
23 probation or acceleration of a deferred judgment:
