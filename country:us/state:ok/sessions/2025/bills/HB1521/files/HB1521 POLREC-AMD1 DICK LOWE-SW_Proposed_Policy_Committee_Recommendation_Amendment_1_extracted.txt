HB1521 POLREC-AMD1 Dick Lowe-SW
3/4/2025 1:52:58 pm
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB1521
Page 13 Section 1 Lines 22
Of the Engrossed Bill
By striking paragraph 2 in its entirety and by adding in lieu thereof the
following language: "Four (4) units or sets of competencies of
mathematics, two (2) of which shall be Algebra I and either Algebra II or
Geometry. A third course shall be at or above the rigor of Algebra I and
may include Algebra II, Geometry, Trigonometry, Math Analysis, Calculus,
Statistics, Math of Finance, Computer Science, college courses approved
for dual credit, or an approved full-time postsecondary career and
technology program or locally approved math-based application course
approved to meet college entrance requirements for mathematics. The
remaining credit is at the discretion of the student and the school. Any
locally developed course or postsecondary program offered by the
Department of Career and Technology Education could fulfill this
requirement if a school district or technology center school district
maintains that those courses use math in an applied or contextual way that
furthers a student’s knowledge of mathematics";
And by updating internal references and modifying the outline to conform.
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Dick Lowe
