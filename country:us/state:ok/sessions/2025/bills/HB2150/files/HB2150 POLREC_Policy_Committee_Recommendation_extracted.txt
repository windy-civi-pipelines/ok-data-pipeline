7 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
8 An Act relating to crimes and punishments; making
certain acts unlawful; providing penalties; defining
9 terms; providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1761.2 of Title 21, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. A person shall not establish an unauthorized camp upon a
17 street, sidewalk, or other public or private property within five
18 hundred (500) feet of public school or private school property.
19 B. Any person who violates the provisions of this section and
20 refuses to vacate the unauthorized camp upon command by a law
21 enforcement officer shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a
22 misdemeanor punishable by a fine not to exceed Fifty Dollars
23 ($50.00), or by imprisonment in the county jail not to exceed thirty
24 (30) days, or by both such fine and imprisonment.
Req. No. 12399 Page 1
1 C. As used in this section:
2 1. "Private school property" means a school that offers a
3 course of instruction for students in one or more grades from
4 prekindergarten through grade twelve and is not operated by a
5 governmental entity;
6 2. "Public school property" means any publicly owned property
7 held for purposes of elementary, secondary or vocational-technical
8 education, and shall not include property owned by public school
9 districts or where such property is leased or rented to an
10 individual or corporation and used for purposes other than
11 educational; and
12 3. "Unauthorized camp" means any tent, shelter, or bedding
13 constructed or arranged for the purpose of or in such a way to
14 permit overnight use on a property not designated as a campsite.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
