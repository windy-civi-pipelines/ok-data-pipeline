Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to crimes and punishments; making
certain acts unlawful; providing penalties; defining
8 terms; providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1761.2 of Title 21, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. A person shall not establish an unauthorized camp upon a
16 street, sidewalk, or other public or private property within five
17 hundred (500) feet of public school or private school property.
18 B. Any person who violates the provisions of this section and
19 refuses to vacate the unauthorized camp upon command by a law
20 enforcement officer shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a
21 misdemeanor punishable by a fine not to exceed Fifty Dollars
22 ($50.00), or by imprisonment in the county jail not to exceed one
23 (1) year, or by both such fine and imprisonment.
24 C. As used in this section:
Req. No. 10517 Page 1
1 1. "Private school property" means a school that offers a
2 course of instruction for students in one or more grades from
3 prekindergarten through grade twelve and is not operated by a
4 governmental entity;
5 2. "Public school property" means any publicly owned property
6 held for purposes of elementary, secondary or vocational-technical
7 education, and shall not include property owned by public school
8 districts or where such property is leased or rented to an
9 individual or corporation and used for purposes other than
10 educational; and
11 3. "Unauthorized camp" means any tent, shelter, or bedding
12 constructed or arranged for the purpose of or in such a way to
13 permit overnight use on a property not designated as a campsite.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
