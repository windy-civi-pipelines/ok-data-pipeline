HB1851 FA1 SchreiberSu-MJ(Untimely Filed)

FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB1851
Page 5 Section 3 Lines 9
Of the Engrossed Bill
by deleting the words "one-step" and inserting in lieu thereof the word
"direct".
On Page 5, Section 3, Lines 10-11, by inserting after the word "website"
and before the words "or contained" the following: ", within the
consumer's account or profile,".
On Page 6, Section 4, Lines 6-7, by deleting after the word "consumer,"
the following: "in a manner that may be retained by the consumer".
On Page 7, Section 5, Line 8, by deleting the words "twenty-five (25)" and
inserting in lieu thereof the following: "fifteen (15)".
On Page 7, Section 5, Line 9, by deleting the word "forty (40)" and
inserting in lieu thereof the following: "forty-five (45)".
On Page 7, Section 5, Lines 9-19, by deleting all language starting with
the words "before the first" through the rest of subsection B, and
inserting in lieu thereof the following: "for automatic renewal contracts
with an initial term of one year that renew for more than one month".
(Continued on next page)
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Suzanne Schreiber
Adopted: _____________________________
______________________________________
Reading Clerk
On Page 8, Section 6, Line 5, by inserting after the word "person" and
before the words "that is regulated" the following "or an affiliate or
subsidiary thereof".
On Page 8, Section 6, Line 8, by deleting paragraph 3 and inserting in
lieu thereof the following: "3. Any person or entity regulated by the
Oklahoma Insurance Department, or an affiliate of that person or entity;".
On Page 8, Section 6, Line 17, by creating a new paragraph 7, which reads
as follows: "An entity regulated by the Oklahoma Department of Labor under
the Alarm, Locksmith, and Fire Sprinkler Industry Act."
