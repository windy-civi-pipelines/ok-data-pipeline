6 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Capitol Improvement
Authority; amending Section 1, Chapter 7, 1st
7 Extraordinary Session, O.S.L. 2023 (73 O.S. Supp.
2024, Section 187A-5), which relates to the
8 utilization of Legacy Capital Financing Fund
authorizations by the Office of Management and
9 Enterprise Services; eliminating certain
authorization; increasing amount of certain
10 authorization.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 7, 1st
15 Extraordinary Session, O.S.L. 2023 (73 O.S. Supp. 2024, Section
16 187A-5), is amended to read as follows:
17 Section 187A-5. A. In addition to any other authorization
18 provided by law, the Oklahoma Capitol Improvement Authority is
19 authorized to utilize available funds from the Legacy Capital
20 Financing Fund created by Enrolled House Bill No. 1002 of the 1st
21 Extraordinary Session of the 59th Oklahoma Legislature Section 187B
22 of this title, in the amounts of:
23 1. Seventy Million Dollars ($70,000,000.00) for the benefit of
24 the Office of Management and Enterprise Services to repair,
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2776 Page 1
1 refu rbish, and improve the real and personal property of the Jim
2 Thorpe Office Building; and
3 2. Nineteen Million Dollars ($19,000,000.00) for the benefit of
4 the Office of Management and Enterprise Services to repair,
5 refurbish, and improve the real and personal property constituting
6 the tunnels underlying the State Capitol Office Complex and
7 associated fixtures and equipment; and
8 3. Twenty-six Million Three Hundred Twenty-two Thousand Three
9 Hundred Dollars ($26,322,300.00) Forty-five Million Three Hundred
10 Twenty-two Thousand Three Hundred Dollars ($45,322,300.00) for the
11 benefit of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services to
12 accept transfer of the Kelley Building and Kelley Annex owned by the
13 Department of Human Services and renovate the facilities for lease
14 to a public operator as a day care facility.
15 B. LCF Recapitalization Payments shall be made related to the
16 distribution of proceeds provided in subsection A of this section,
17 in accordance with the provisions of the Legacy Capital Financing
18 Act.
19 C. The Authority may distribute funds authorized pursuant to
20 subsection A of this section in one or more tranches.
21 D. The Authority may enter memoranda of understanding with
22 agencies, departments, and subdivisions of the state as needed, to
23 facilitate the provisions of this act, provided that such memoranda
24 of understanding do not constitute a legal obligation of the State
