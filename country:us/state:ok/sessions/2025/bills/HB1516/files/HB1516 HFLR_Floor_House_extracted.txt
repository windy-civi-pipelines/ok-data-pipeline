10 An Act relating to insurance; amending 36 O.S. 2021,

Section 2:
Section 3606, which relates to the capacity of minors
11 to contract for insurance; requiring parental or
guardian consent; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 36 O.S. 2021, Section 3606, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 3606. A. Any person of competent legal capacity may
19 contract for insurance.
20 B. A minor not less than fifteen (15) sixteen (16) years of age
21 as at nearest birthday may, notwithstanding such minority and with
22 signed parental or guardian consent, contract for life or accident
23 and health insurance on his or her own life or body or the life or
24 body of any person in whom he or she has an insurable interest, for
HB1516 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 his or her own benefit or for the benefit of his or her father or
2 mother, spouse, child, brother, sister or grandparents. Such a
3 minor shall, notwithstanding such minority, be deemed competent to
4 exercise all rights and powers with respect to or under any contract
5 of life or accident and health insurance on his or her own life or
6 body or with respect to or under any contract such minor effected on
7 the life or body of any person in whom he or she has an insurable
8 interest, as though of full legal age, and may surrender his or her
9 interest therein and give a valid discharge for any benefit accruing
10 or money payable thereunder. The minor shall not, by reason of his
11 or her minority, be entitled to rescind, avoid or repudiate the
12 contract, nor to rescind, avoid or repudiate any exercise of a right
13 or privilege thereunder, except that such minor, not otherwise
14 emancipated, shall not be bound by any unperformed agreement to pay,
15 by promissory note or otherwise, any premium on any such insurance
16 contract.
17 C. A minor not less than sixteen (16) years of age may,
18 notwithstanding such minority and with signed parental or guardian
19 consent, contract for insurance on other subjects of insurance in
20 which he or she has an insurable interest. A minor shall be bound
21 by any settlement made in connection with any insurance contract so
22 issued. The minor shall not, by reason of his or her minority, be
23 entitled to rescind, avoid or repudiate the contract, nor to
24 rescind, avoid or repudiate any exercise of a right or privilege
HB1516 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 ther eunder, except that such minor, not otherwise emancipated, shall
2 not be bound by any unperformed agreement to pay, by promissory note
3 or otherwise, any premium on any such insurance contract.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
