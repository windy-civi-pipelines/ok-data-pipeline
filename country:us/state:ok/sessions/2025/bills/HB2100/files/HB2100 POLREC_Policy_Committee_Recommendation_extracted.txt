8 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
9 An Act relating to environmental and natural
resources; providing that to protect natural
10 resources and landowner rights are a part of the
Conservation Commission Cost-Share Program are exempt
11 from eminent domain; providing purpose of protection
from eminent domain; providing exemption; providing
12 for codification; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-3-118 of Title 27A, unless
19 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 To protect natural resources and landowner rights, private lands
21 that are entered into the Cost-Share Program are exempt from eminent
22 domain. This protection will ensure the longevity of conservation
23 efforts, with exemptions granted only for eminent domain projects
24 essential to safeguarding public health and safety.
