A. There is hereby created, within the Oklahoma Housing Finance
1
2
Agency, the Municipal Workforce Housing Development Grant Program to
2
3
offer financial assistance by grant to municipalities for the
3
4
purpose of increasing the availability of workforce housing in this
4
5
state. Grant funding shall be administered based on the potential
5
6
for a specific plan to increase the availability of workforce
6
7
housing in this state. A grant under this program shall be a one-
7
8
time award.
8
9
B. Municipalities of this state are eligible for funding under
9
10
this program. Applicants shall provide a plan for workforce
10
11
housing.
11
12
C. A grant agreement shall be made between any awardee and the
12
13
Agency. The grant agreement shall provide for the conditions of
13
14
disbursement, shall include a provision requiring the repayment of
14
15
awarded funds if the terms are not met, and shall include a
15
16
requirement for awardees to provide ongoing quarterly reports
16
17
detailing activity related to the implementation of the plan for
17
18
workforce housing.
