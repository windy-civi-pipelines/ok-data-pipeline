14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-111 of Title 47, unless there
14
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15
16 A. Any directive or instruction regarding policy and rules that
16

19 state who is providing the directive or instruction. Any employee
19
20 who gives a directive or instruction that violates state statute or
20

22 pay.
