Section 1:
SB997 FULLPCS1-AMD1 Chris Sneed-MJ
4/23/2025 3:38:40 pm
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend amendment SB997 FULLPCS1
Page 4 Section 2 Lines 24
Of the Engrossed Bill
By deleting the words "a parent company both" and inserting in lieu
thereof the following: "an entity".
On Page 5, Section 2, Lines 3-4, by deleting the words "parent company"
and inserting in lieu thereof the word "entity".
On Page 5, Section 2, Line 4, by inserting after the word "and" and before
the word "subsidiaries" the word "its".
On Page 5, Section 2, Line 5, by deleting the words "parent company" and
inserting in lieu thereof the word "entity".
On Page 5, Section 2, Line 6, by deleting the word "solely" and inserting
lieu thereof the following: "regardless of whether".
On Page 5, Section 2, Line 7, by inserting after the word "affiliates" the
following: "of the entity"
On Page 5, Section 2, Line 8, by deleting the word "meet" and inserting in
lieu thereof the word "meets".
On Page 5, Section 3, Line 20, by deleting paragraph 2 and renumbering
subsequent paragraphs.
On Page 7, Section 3, Line 3, by inserting a new subsection E which shall
read as follows: "E. This act does not apply to a company, including a
third-party vendor, that is in compliance with the Secure and Trusted
Communications Networks Act of 2019 (Public Law 116-124)."
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Chris Sneed
Adopted: _____________________________
