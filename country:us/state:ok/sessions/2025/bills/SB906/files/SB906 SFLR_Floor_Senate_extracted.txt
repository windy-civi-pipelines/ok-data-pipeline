8 An Act relating to the practice of pharmacy; amending
59 O.S. 2021, Section 353.18A, which relates to
9 pharmacy technicians; updating statutory language;
establishing certain pharmacy staffing ratio; and
10 providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 59 O.S. 2021, Section 353.18A, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 353.18A. A. Supportive personnel may perform certain
17 tasks in the practice of pharmacy if such personnel perform the
18 tasks in compliance with rules promulgated by the State Board of
19 Pharmacy.
20 B. 1. No person shall serve as a pharmacy technician without
21 first procuring a permit from the Board.
22 2. An application for an initial or renewal pharmacy technician
23 permit issued pursuant to the provisions of this subsection shall be
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB906 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 subm itted to the Board and provide any other information deemed
2 relevant by the Board.
3 3. An application for an initial or renewal permit shall be
4 accompanied by a permit fee not to exceed Seventy Five Seventy-five
5 Dollars ($75.00) for each period of one (1) year. A permit issued
6 pursuant to this subsection shall be valid for a period to be
7 determined by the Board.
8 4. Every permitted pharmacy technician who fails to complete a
9 renewal form and remit the required renewal fee to the Board by the
10 fifteenth day after the expiration of the permit shall pay a late
11 fee to be fixed by the Board.
12 5. A pharmacy technician permit shall be cancelled canceled
13 thirty (30) days after expiration.
14 6. A person may obtain reinstatement of a cancelled canceled
15 pharmacy technician permit by making application, paying a
16 reinstatement fee, and satisfactorily completing other requirements
17 set by the Board.
18 C. A licensed retail pharmacy shall maintain a pharmacy
19 technician-to-pharmacist ratio of not more than four pharmacy
20 technicians for every one licensed pharmacist.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
