7 A Resolution declaring April as Child Abuse

11 Child Abuse can be prevented and everyone has a role to play; and
12 WHEREAS, every child deserves to be safe, nurtured, and

15 WHEREAS, the estimated cost to treat the effects of child

1 WHEREAS, we lay the foundation for children's later growth and
2 development when the entire community takes responsibility for
3 creating healthy environments in which to raise its children; and
4 WHEREAS, the children in our community who have fallen victim to
5 maltreatment deserve high-quality, evidence-based services delivered
6 by committed and well-supported professionals; and
7 WHEREAS, effective child abuse prevention programs succeed
8 because of community partnerships created among families, social
9 service agencies, schools, businesses, law enforcement agencies,
10 faith-based organizations, and other child-serving agencies; and
11 WHEREAS, it is easier to build healthy and strong children than
12 repair broken adults.
13 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
14 OF THE 1ST SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
15 THAT the Oklahoma House of Representatives declares April as
16 Child Abuse Prevention Month in Oklahoma and urges all citizens,
17 businesses, and social institutions to dedicate themselves to the
18 task of supporting parents in their critical job of raising safe,
19 healthy children and ensuring the health and safety of all children
20 in Oklahoma.
