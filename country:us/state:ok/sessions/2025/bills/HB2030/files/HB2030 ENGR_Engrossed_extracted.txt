12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7401-101 of Title 10, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. The foster care maintenance payment for resource parents who
17 are traditional foster parents shall be at least:
18 1. Twenty-two Dollars and seventy-two cents ($22.72) per day,
19 or Six Hundred Eighty-one Dollars and sixty cents ($681.60) per
20 month, for a child who is zero (0) years to five (5) years of age;
21 2. Twenty-five Dollars and forty-two cents ($25.42) per day, or
22 Seven Hundred Sixty-two Dollars and sixty cents ($762.60) per month,
23 for a child who is six (6) to twelve (12) years of age; and
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2030 Page 1
1 3. Twenty-seven Dollars and sixty-two cents ($27.62) per day,
2 or Eight Hundred Twenty-eight Dollars and sixty cents ($828.60) per
3 month, for a child who is thirteen (13) years of age or older.
4 B. The Director of the Department of Human Services shall
5 review the foster care maintenance payment rates annually, pursuant
6 to 45 C.F.R., Section 1356.21(m), to assure appropriateness and may
7 authorize a foster care maintenance payment in an amount greater
8 than is specified in subsection A of this section.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
10 Passed the House of Representatives the 24th day of March, 2025.
