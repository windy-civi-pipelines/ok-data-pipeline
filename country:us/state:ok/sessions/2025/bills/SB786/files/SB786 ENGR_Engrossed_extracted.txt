7 An Act relating to crimes and punishments; amending
21 O.S. 2021, Section 1220, which relates to
8 transporting intoxicating beverage; prohibiting
certain consumption of marijuana; increasing certain
9 fine; updating statutory language and references; and
providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1220, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 1220. A. Except as provided in subsection C of this
16 section, it 1. It shall be unlawful for any operator to knowingly
17 transport or for any passenger to possess in any moving vehicle upon
18 a public highway, street, or alley any intoxicating alcoholic
19 beverage or low-point beer, as defined by Sections 163.1 and 163.2
20 in Section 1-103 of Title 37 37A of the Oklahoma Statutes, except in
21 the original container which shall not have been opened and from
22 which the original cap or seal shall not have been removed, unless
23 the opened container be is in the rear trunk or rear compartment,
24 which shall include the spare tire compartment in a station wagon or
ENGR. S. B. NO. 786 Page 1
1 pane l truck, or any outside compartment which is not accessible to
2 the driver or any other person in the vehicle while it is in motion.
3 2. It shall be unlawful to consume marijuana or inhale
4 secondhand marijuana smoke due to another person’s consumption of
5 marijuana while operating a motor vehicle on a public highway,
6 street, or alley. A person operating a motor vehicle on a public
7 highway, street, or alley shall not possess any open container that
8 contains marijuana in the passenger area of the motor vehicle.
9 Any person violating the provisions of this section shall be deemed
10 guilty of a misdemeanor, and upon conviction shall be punished as
11 provided in subsection A of Section 566 6-125 of Title 37 37A of the
12 Oklahoma Statutes.
13 B. Any person convicted of violating any provision of
14 subsection A of this section shall, in addition to any fine imposed,
15 pay a special assessment trauma-care fee of One Hundred Dollars
16 ($100.00) Two Hundred Fifty Dollars ($250.00) to be deposited into
17 the Trauma Care Assistance Revolving Fund created in Section 1-2522
18 1-2530.9 of Title 63 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
19 C. The provisions of paragraph 1 of subsection A of this
20 section shall not apply to the passenger area of buses and
21 limousines; however, it shall be unlawful for the driver of the bus
22 or limousine to consume or have in the driver’s immediate possession
23 any intoxicating beverage or low-point beer.
24
ENGR. S. B. NO. 786 Page 2
1 D. No city, town, or county may adopt any order, ordinance,
2 rule, or regulation concerning the consumption or serving of
3 intoxicating beverages or low-point beer in buses or limousines.
4 E. As used in this section:
5 1. “Bus” means a vehicle as defined in Section 1-105 of Title
6 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes chartered for transportation of persons
7 for hire. It shall not mean a school bus, as defined by Section 1-
8 160 of Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes, transporting children or a
9 vehicle operated pursuant to a franchise with a city or town
10 operating over a regularly scheduled route; and
11 2. “Limousine” means a chauffeur-driven motor vehicle, other
12 than a bus or taxicab, as defined by Section 1-174 of Title 47 of
13 the Oklahoma Statutes, designed and used for transportation of
14 persons for compensation.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
