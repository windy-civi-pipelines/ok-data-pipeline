1 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
2 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
3 The State Regents for Higher Education may approve functional
4 exceptions. "Functional exception" means an expansion or exception
5 to assigned institutional function or mission to permit an
6 institution of higher education within The State System of Higher
7 Education to offer courses or degree programs outside the
8 institution's assigned function or mission to meet unmet workforce
9 needs.
10 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3213.1, is
11 amended to read as follows:
12 Section 3213.1. A. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher
13 Education shall make educational program resources at institutions
14 in The Oklahoma State System of Higher Education available to the
15 people in the Ponca City area. This program shall be known as the
16 University Center at Ponca City.
17 B. The State Regents shall draw upon the educational programs
18 of institutions best suited to provide the kind of educational
19 programs needed and shall, in pursuance of Article XIII-A of the
20 Oklahoma Constitution of Oklahoma and appropriate statutes, set the
21 standards of education as they relate to the programs operated to
22 assure that credits earned by students will be fully accepted at
23 institutions of higher education to which the students may transfer
24 the credit to apply toward an educational study objective. Northern
Req. No. 13509 Page 2
1 Okla homa College shall have exclusive authority to offer all lower
2 division courses and programs at the University Center at Ponca City
3 as listed in the current college catalog of Northern Oklahoma
4 College.
5 C. The State Regents may establish appropriate funds and
6 accounts, including a revolving fund, in the Office of State Finance
7 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for servicing the
8 fiscal operations of the University Center at Ponca City. The funds
9 and accounts shall be subject to the direct supervision, management,
10 and control of the board of trustees created by subsection D of this
11 section. The people locally shall provide suitable physical plant
12 accommodations for the University Center at Ponca City.
13 D. There is hereby created a board of nine (9) trustees to be
14 appointed by the Governor by and with the consent of the Senate to
15 serve as the administrative agency for the University Center at
16 Ponca City. The board shall be a body corporate and shall adopt and
17 use an official seal. The board so created shall have the authority
18 to submit a budget annually to the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher
19 Education, administer monies budgeted by the State Regents, provide
20 educational facilities, recommend courses and programs to be offered
21 by participating institutions, select a chief executive officer
22 whose duties include the general coordination of approved programs
23 and services and the selection of other appropriate nonteaching
24 personnel. The board of trustees is authorized to expend all monies
Req. No. 13509 Page 3
1 allo cated to the University Center at Ponca City as may be necessary
2 to perform the duties and responsibilities imposed upon the board by
3 this section. For purposes of acquiring and taking title to real
4 and personal property from sources other than state appropriations,
5 the board is authorized to enter into contracts and to adopt rules
6 and regulations pertaining to such actions. The initial nine (9)
7 members shall serve their terms for the period to which originally
8 appointed, in numbered positions having dates of expiration
9 identical to the dates of expiration of the original appointments:
10 Position No. 1. The term of office of one member shall expire
11 on June 30, 2013, and each nine (9) years thereafter;
12 Position No. 2. The term of office of one member shall expire
13 on June 30, 2014, and each nine (9) years thereafter;
14 Position No. 3. The term of office of one member shall expire
15 on June 30, 2015, and each nine (9) years thereafter;
16 Position No. 4. The term of office of one member shall expire
17 on June 30, 2016, and each nine (9) years thereafter;
18 Position No. 5. The term of office of one member shall expire
19 on June 30, 2017, and each nine (9) years thereafter;
20 Position No. 6. The term of office of one member shall expire
21 on June 30, 2018, and each nine (9) years thereafter;
22 Position No. 7. The term of office of one member shall expire
23 on June 30, 2019, and each nine (9) years thereafter;
24
Req. No. 13509 Page 4
1 Position No. 8. The term of office of one member shall expire
2 on June 30, 2020, and each nine (9) years thereafter; and
3 Position No. 9. The term of office of one member shall expire
4 on June 30, 2021, and each nine (9) years thereafter.
5 The board shall organize and elect a chair, vice-chair vice
6 chair, and secretary annually.
7 E. The board of trustees may establish and maintain plans for
8 tenure and retirement of its employees and for payment of deferred
9 compensation of employees and may provide hospital and medical
10 benefits, accident, health and life insurance and annuity contracts
11 for employees and pay for all or part of the cost with funds
12 available for payment of its operating expenses.
13 F. The board may establish with the State Treasurer any
14 accounts as are necessary to operate retirement, deferred
15 compensation or benefits plans for its employees. The accounts
16 shall be revolving funds not subject to fiscal year limitations.
17 Funds deposited in the accounts may be invested in any of the types
18 of instruments in which the State Treasurer is authorized by law to
19 invest.

21 amended to read as follows:
22 Section 3405.1. A. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher
23 Education shall make resident college credit courses and programs
24 available to the people of Muskogee.

1 B. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education shall
2 provide lower division classes and programs through a branch of
3 Connors State College as the State Regents shall determine are
4 appropriate, which shall be established and located in Muskogee.
5 The branch campus of Connors State College shall be governed by the
6 Board of Regents for the Oklahoma Agricultural and Mechanical
7 Colleges and administered through Connors State College.
8 C. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education shall
9 provide upper division classes and programs and graduate classes at
10 the Masters level through a branch of Northeastern State University
11 as the State Regents shall determine are appropriate, which shall be
12 established and located at Muskogee. The branch campus of
13 Northeastern State University shall be governed by the Board of
14 Regents of Oklahoma Colleges and administered through Northeastern
15 State University.

18 Section 4662. A. On January 1, 1999, there shall be

23 degree programs as the Oklahoma State Regents of Higher Education

7 at Oklahoma State University/Tulsa Oklahoma State University-Tulsa
8 shall be designated as awarded by the Oklahoma State Regents for
9 Higher Education acting through Oklahoma State University; provided
10 that dual degree programs may be offered at Oklahoma State
11 University/Tulsa Oklahoma State University-Tulsa and dual degrees
12 may be awarded through Oklahoma State University and other
13 institutions.
