7 An Act relating to schools; prohibiting private

8 participate in the Oklahoma Parental Choice Tax

12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 28-104 of Title 70, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 Private schools accredited by the State Board of Education or
17 another accrediting association shall not, as a condition of
18 acceptance, enrollment, or application, require that a student's
19 parent or guardian participate in the Oklahoma Parental Choice Tax
20 Credit Program created in Section 28-101 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma
21 Statutes.
