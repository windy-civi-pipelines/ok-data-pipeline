11 An Act relating to schools; prohibiting private
schools from requiring a parent or guardian to
12 participate in the Oklahoma Parental Choice Tax
Credit; providing for codification; providing an
13 effective date; and declaring an emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 28-104 of Title 70, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 Private schools accredited by the State Board of Education or
21 another accrediting association shall not, as a condition of
22 acceptance, enrollment, or application, require that a student's
23 parent or guardian participate in the Oklahoma Parental Choice Tax
