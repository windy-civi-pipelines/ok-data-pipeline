1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to schools; prohibiting private
schools from requiring a parent or guardian to
8 participate in the Oklahoma Parental Choice Tax
Credit; banning certain tuition rate or fee
9 increases; providing for codification; providing an
effective date; and declaring an emergency.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 28-104 of Title 70, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. Private schools accredited by the State Board of Education
17 or another accrediting association shall not, as a condition of
18 acceptance, enrollment, or student eligibility, require that a
19 student's parent or guardian participate in the Oklahoma Parental
20 Choice Tax Credit Program created in Section 28-101 of Title 70 of
21 the Oklahoma Statutes.
22 B. Private schools that accept students whose parents or
23 guardians receive an income tax credit through the Oklahoma Parental
24
Req. No. 11247 Page 1
1 Choi ce Tax Credit Program shall not increase their tuition rates or
2 fees as a result of the Oklahoma Parental Choice Tax Credit Program.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
4 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
5 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
6 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
7 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
