16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7002 of Title 70, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. The Commission for Educational Quality and Accountability
21 shall promulgate rules establishing a micro-credential for certified
22 teachers who complete Certified Academic Language Therapist (CALT)
23 certification or Center for Effective Reading Instruction (CERI).
24
SB840 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 B. The requirements for a CALT micro-credential or a CERI
2 micro-credential shall include at a minimum:
3 1. A master’s degree;
4 2. Completion of a comprehensive therapy-level training;
5 3. Successful passage of a CALT competency examination or CERI
6 competency examination; and
7 4. Twenty-eight (28) days of instruction post the passage of
8 the applicable competency examination.
9 C. A certified teacher who earns a CALT micro-credential or a
10 CERI micro-credential shall be authorized to assess students for and
11 diagnose dyslexia.
12 D. A micro-credential awarded pursuant to this section shall be
13 reflected on a teacher’s certificate to teach.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
15 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
16 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
18 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
19
20 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS AND BUDGET, dated
04/16/2025 - DO PASS, As Amended.
