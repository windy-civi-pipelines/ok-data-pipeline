7 An Act relating to state parks; amending 74 O.S.
2021, Section 2276.1, which relates to Oklahoma State
8 Park Trust Fund; authorizing construction of new
facility; updating statutory language; providing an
9 effective date; and declaring an emergency.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 2276.1, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 2276.1. A. There is hereby created a trust fund to be
16 known as the “Oklahoma State Park Trust Fund”. The Oklahoma Tourism
17 and Recreation Commission shall be the trustees of the Trust Fund
18 trust fund.
19 B. 1. The Commission may utilize five percent (5%) of the
20 principal of the Trust Fund trust fund annually to:
21 a. protect and conserve state park lands, but shall not
22 include routine maintenance expenses of the state
23 parks,
24
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1152 Page 1
1 b. preserve historic properties under the jurisdiction of
2 the Commission,
3 c. provide for one-time capital upgrades and improvements
4 of state park resources, and
5 d. pay fees and expenses associated with the services of
6 a custodian of the Trust Fund trust fund.
7 2. Beginning July 1, 2012, and ending June 30, 2020, the
8 Commission may utilize funds not to exceed Fifteen Million Dollars
9 ($15,000,000.00) from the principal of the Trust Fund trust fund to
10 construct and maintain a lodge and any associated facilities deemed
11 necessary by the Commission at the Lake Murray State Park.
12 3. Beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2030, the
13 Commission may utilize funds not to exceed Five Million Dollars
14 ($5,000,000.00) from the principal of the trust fund to construct
15 and maintain a lodge and any associated facilities deemed necessary
16 by the Commission.
17 C. The Commission shall give priority for funding to the state
18 park from which the revenues were initially derived.
19 D. The Trust Fund trust fund principal shall consist of monies
20 from any and all mineral lease payments, seismograph fees, royalty
21 payments, or other payments associated with oil and gas mineral
22 operations at state parks that are managed by the Oklahoma Tourism
23 and Recreation Department, any funds appropriated or transferred to
24 the Trust Fund trust fund by the Legislature, and any monies or
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1152 Page 2
1 asse ts contributed to the Trust Fund trust fund from any other
2 source, public or private.
3 E. Notwithstanding other provisions of law, income and
4 investment return on Trust Fund trust fund principal shall accrue to
5 the Trust Fund trust fund for use as provided by authorization of
6 the trustees for the purposes listed in subsection B of this
7 section. Except as provided for in subsection B of this section, no
8 income or investment return or principal shall be used for
9 administrative expenses of the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation
10 Department or expenses incurred by the Commission in the
11 administration of the Trust Fund trust fund.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
13 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
14 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
15 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
16 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
