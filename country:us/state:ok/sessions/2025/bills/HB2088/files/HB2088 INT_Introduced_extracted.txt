Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to schools; defining terms; requiring
school district boards of education to adopt policy
8 allowing students educated by other means to
participate in certain activities; requiring
9 publication of policy; providing eligibility criteria
for participation; requiring compliance with academic
10 standards; providing for agreed-upon method for
evaluation of compliance; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
11 Section 27-103, which relates to the Oklahoma
Extracurricular Activities Accountability Act;
12 requiring policy to allow participation of students
educated by other means in certain activities;
13 providing for codification; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 8-201 of Title 70, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. As used in this section:
21 1. "Resident district" means the public school district in
22 which a student resides as defined in Section 1-113 of Title 70 of
23 the Oklahoma Statutes;
24
Req. No. 10550 Page 1
1 2. "School athletic association" shall have the same meaning as
2 defined in Section 27-102 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes; and
3 3. "Students who are educated by other means" means students
4 who are educated pursuant to the other means of education exception
5 provided for in subsection A of Section 10-105 of Title 70 of the
6 Oklahoma Statutes.
7 B. Beginning with the 2025-2026 school year, each school
8 district board of education shall adopt a policy allowing students
9 who are educated by other means to participate in extracurricular
10 activities offered by the student's resident district. The policy
11 shall be published in the school district student handbook and
12 posted on the school district website.
13 C. In order to participate in extracurricular activities
14 pursuant to this section, students who are educated by other means
15 shall:
16 1. Register an intention to participate with the board of
17 education of the resident district no later than the July 1
18 immediately preceding the school year in which participation is
19 intended;
20 2. Pay any participation or activity fee in an amount equal to
21 any fee charged to a resident district participant;
22 3. Adhere to the same standards of behavior, responsibility,
23 performance, and code of conduct as other participants of the
24 resident district;
Req. No. 10550 Page 2
1 4. Adhere to any rules and policies of a school athletic
2 association which provides the coordination, supervision, and
3 regulation of the extracurricular activities and contests of
4 schools;
5 5. Adhere to any physical exams or drug testing provisions
6 required by the board of education of the resident district or the
7 school athletic association which provides the coordination,
8 supervision, and regulation of the extracurricular activities and
9 contests of schools; and
10 6. Adhere to the same academic standards as other participants
11 of the resident district pursuant to the provisions of subsection D
12 of this section.
13 D. During the time period a student who is educated by other
14 means participates in extracurricular activities pursuant to the
15 provisions of this section, the student shall meet academic
16 standards by a method of evaluation agreed upon by the parent or
17 legal guardian of the student and the superintendent of the resident
18 district. The method of evaluation may include a review of the
19 student's work by a certified teacher employed by the resident
20 district, the student's performance on a nationally recognized
21 standardized test, or evaluation of grades earned through
22 correspondence courses.
23 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 27-103, is
24 amended to read as follows:
Req. No. 10550 Page 3
1 Section 27-103. A public school or school district shall not be
2 a member of any school athletic association unless that association
3 has adopted a written policy that requires the following:
4 1. All records of the association to be made accessible
5 consistent with the provisions of the Oklahoma Open Records Act;
6 2. All meetings of the association to be open and conducted in
7 a manner consistent with the provisions of the Oklahoma Open Meeting
8 Act, including specifically the notice and agenda, voting and
9 executive session requirements; and
10 3. An annual financial audit and a compliance audit of all
11 funds of the association in accordance with the auditing standards
12 set forth in the Oklahoma Public School Audit Law. In addition, the
13 association shall have performance audits conducted of the
14 operations of the association. A performance audit shall be
15 conducted no later than December 31, 2014, and by December 31 every
16 five (5) years thereafter; and
17 4. A student who is educated pursuant to the other means
18 exception provided for in subsection A of Section 10-105 of this
19 title may participate in interscholastic activities or contests
20 offered by the student's resident district as defined in Section 1-
21 113 of this title. Student participation shall be subject to the
22 provisions of Section 1 of this act.
23 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
24
Req. No. 10550 Page 4
1 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
2 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
3 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
