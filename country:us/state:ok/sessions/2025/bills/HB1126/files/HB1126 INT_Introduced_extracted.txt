Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to public health and safety; defining
term; prohibiting the manufacture, sell, hold or
8 offer for sale, or distribution of any cultivated
meat; providing punishment for violation; providing
9 for revocation of certain licenses for violation;
providing the State Department of Health certain
10 rulemaking authority; providing exemptions; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-1150 of Title 63, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. For the purposes of this section, the term "cultivated meat"
18 means a meat or meat product that was produced from cultured animal
19 tissue produced from in vitro animal cell cultures outside of the
20 animal from which the cells were derived.
21 B. 1. It shall be unlawful for any person to manufacture,
22 sell, hold or offer for sale, or distribute any cultivated meat
23 product in this state.
24
Req. No. 10578 Page 1
1 2. Any person who violates the provisions of this subsection
2 shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a misdemeanor.
3 C. 1. A seller of food under Sections 1-1118, 1-1118.1, and 1-
4 1119 of Title 63 of the Oklahoma Statutes that sells or distributes
5 cultivated meat products in violation of subsection B of this
6 section shall be subject to all enforcement measures as provided by
7 rule of the State Department of Health.
8 2. The license of a seller of food may be suspended or revoked,
9 as provided by rule of the State Department of Health upon the
10 conviction of an owner or an employee of the licensed seller of food
11 for a violation of subsection B of this section.
12 D. The State Department of Health may adopt rules as necessary
13 to implement this section.
14 E. Nothing in this section shall be construed as preventing any
15 federal, state, or local governmental entity or institution of
16 higher education, or a person that is partnered with a governmental
17 entity or institution of higher education as evidenced by a formal
18 agreement, from conducting research in this state regarding the
19 production of cultivated meat products.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
