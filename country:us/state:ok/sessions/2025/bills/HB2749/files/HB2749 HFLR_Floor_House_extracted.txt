20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
22 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5029A of Title 63, unless there
23 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
24
HB2749 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
2 fund for the Oklahoma Health Care Authority to be designated the
3 "Intergenerational Education Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a
4 continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall
5 consist of all monies directed for deposit to the fund by law. All
6 monies accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated
7 and may be budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma Health Care
8 Authority for the purposes described in subsection B of the section.
9 Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants issued by
10 the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with
11 the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
12 approval and payment.
13 B. There is hereby created at the Oklahoma Health Care
14 Authority the Intergenerational Education Program. The Program
15 shall be a competitive grant program with the purpose of
16 facilitating improvements in nursing facilitates, to expand the
17 number of intergenerational education programs in this state,
18 embodying collaboration between nursing facilities participating in
19 Oklahoma's Medicaid program and public school districts in this
20 state. The Authority shall utilize funds available from the
21 Intergenerational Education Revolving Fund created in subsection A
22 of this section to implement the Program.
23 C. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall establish grant
24 eligibility criteria and an associated application process. Such
HB2749 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 crit eria shall establish minimum standards for facility eligibility,
2 and award amounts.
3 D. Grant awards authorized under the provisions of this act,
4 may be awarded in the form of rate adjustments to qualifying nursing
5 facilities participating in Oklahoma's Medicaid Program.
6 E. No amount of award shall be authorized under this act that
7 would exceed the cumulative total of funds directed for deposit to
8 the Intergenerational Education Revolving Fund created in subsection
9 A of this act.
10 F. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority may promulgate rules and
11 adopt policies and procedures as necessary to implement the
12 provisions of this act.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
14 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
15 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
16 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
17 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
