2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
3 HOUSE JOINT
RESOLUTION 1011 By: Dollens
4
5
6
AS INTRODUCED
7
8 to refer to the people for their approval or
rejection a proposed amendment to the Constitution of
9 the State of Oklahoma by adding a new Section 36 to
Article VI; providing ballot title; and directing
10 filing.
11
12
13 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND THE SENATE OF THE
14 1ST SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
15 SECTION 1. The Secretary of State shall refer to the people for
16 their approval or rejection, as and in the manner provided by law,
17 the following proposed amendment to the Constitution of the State of
18 Oklahoma by adding a new Section 36 to Article VI thereof, to read
19 as follows:
20 Section 36. A. The Legislature shall, by law, create an
21 election recall process for statewide elected officials.
22 B. The list of officials shall include:
23 1. The Governor;
24 2. The Lieutenant Governor;
Req. No. 12129 Page 1
1 3. The State Auditor and Inspector;
2 4. The Attorney General;
3 5. The State Treasurer;
4 6. The State Superintendent of Public Instruction;
5 7. The Labor Commissioner;
6 8. The Insurance Commissioner; and
7 9. The Corporation Commissioners.
8 C. Election recalls shall require the same number of signatures
9 required for an initiative petition.
10 D. Election recalls shall be set by the Governor no later than
11 the General Election following the submission of the required
12 signatures to the Secretary of State.
13 E. The Legislature shall enact the election recall process no
14 later than the end of the legislative session following the approval
15 of this measure by vote of the people.
16 SECTION 2. The Ballot Title for the proposed Constitutional
17 amendment as set forth in SECTION 1 of this resolution shall be in
18 the following form:
19 BALLOT TITLE
20 Legislative Referendum No. ____ State Question No. ____
21 THE GIST OF THE PROPOSITION IS AS FOLLOWS:
22 This measure amends the Oklahoma Constitution by adding a new
23 Section 36 to Article 6. The measure directs the Oklahoma
24 Legislature to create an election recall process for statewide
Req. No. 12129 Page 2
1 elected officials. Officials include the Governor, Lieutenant
2 Governor, State Auditor and Inspector, Attorney General, State
3 Treasurer, State Superintendent of Public Instruction, Labor
4 Commissioner, Insurance Commissioner, and Corporation
5 Commissioners. Election recalls will require the same number of
6 signatures as initiative petitions submitted to the Secretary of
7 State. The Governor will set recalls by the following General
8 Election. The election recall process will be enacted by the end
9 of the following legislative session.
10 SHALL THE PROPOSAL BE APPROVED?
11 FOR THE PROPOSAL — YES _____________
12 AGAINST THE PROPOSAL — NO _____________
13 SECTION 3. The Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives,
14 immediately after the passage of this act, shall prepare and file
15 one copy thereof, including the Ballot Title set forth in SECTION 2
16 hereof, with the Secretary of State and one copy with the Attorney
17 General.
