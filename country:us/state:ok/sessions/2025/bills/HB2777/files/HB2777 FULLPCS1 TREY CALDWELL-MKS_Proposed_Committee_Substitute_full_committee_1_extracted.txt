10 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Opioid Abatement
Revolving Fund; making appropriations to the fund;
12 specifying amounts and source; stating purposes;
providing for noncodification; providing an effective
13 date; and declaring an emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
18 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
19 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Opioid Abatement
20 Revolving Fund created pursuant to Section 30.6 of Title 74 of the
21 Oklahoma Statutes, from any monies not otherwise appropriated from
22 the Opioid Lawsuit Settlement Fund (Fund 383) of the State Treasury,
23 the sum of Twenty Million Dollars ($20,000,000.00) to be budgeted
24 and expended in accordance with law.
Req. No. 13722 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
2 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
3 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Opioid Abatement
4 Revolving Fund created pursuant to Section 30.6 of Title 74 of the
5 Oklahoma Statutes, from any monies not otherwise appropriated from
6 the Opioid Lawsuit Settlement Fund (Fund 383) of the State Treasury,
7 the sum of One Million Two Hundred Fifty-three Thousand Six Hundred
8 Fifty-eight Dollars ($1,253,658.00) to be distributed to non-
9 litigating subdivisions.
10 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
11 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
12 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
14 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
