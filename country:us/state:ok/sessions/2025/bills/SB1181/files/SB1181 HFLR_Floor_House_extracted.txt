10 An Act relating to the Office of Management and
Enterprise Services; making an appropriation;
11 identifying source of funds; stating purpose;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
12 emergency.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Office of
16 Management and Enterprise Services from monies not otherwise
17 appropriated from the ARPA Administrative Costs Fund created in
18 Section 255.5 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of Two
19 Million Four Hundred Eighteen Thousand One Hundred Eighty Dollars
20 ($2,418,180.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to pay for
21 expenses arising from implementing programs and expenditures in
22 accordance with actions taken by the Joint Committee on Pandemic
23 Relief Funding.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
