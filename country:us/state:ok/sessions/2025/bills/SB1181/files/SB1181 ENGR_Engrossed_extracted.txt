7 An Act relating to the Office of Management and
Enterprise Services; making an appropriation;
8 identifying source of funds; stating purpose;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
9 emergency.
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Office of
13 Management and Enterprise Services from monies not otherwise
14 appropriated from the ARPA Administrative Costs Fund created in
15 Section 255.5 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of Two
16 Million Four Hundred Eighteen Thousand One Hundred Eighty Dollars
17 ($2,418,180.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to pay for
18 expenses arising from implementing programs and expenditures in
19 accordance with actions taken by the Joint Committee on Pandemic
20 Relief Funding.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
22 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
23 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
