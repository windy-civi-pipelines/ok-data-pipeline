17 Constitution, there is hereby ordered the following legislative
18 referendum which shall be filed with the Secretary of State and

20 the people for their approval or rejection at the next General
21 Election. Provided, however, that this measure shall not be
22 referred to a vote of the people if the provisions of Enrolled House
23 Bill No. 1047 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature
24 become enacted as law.
