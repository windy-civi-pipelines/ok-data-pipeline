5 authorized to offer to the public by this act or the terms of its
6 license, upon written notice from the Commission, such activity
7 shall cease forthwith. Such activity shall constitute a basis upon
8 which the Commission may suspend or revoke the licensee's license.
9 The Commission shall promulgate any rules and regulations necessary
10 to enforce the provisions of this subsection.
11 H. This act is game-specific and shall not be construed to
12 allow the operation of any other form of gaming unless specifically
13 allowed by this act. This act shall not permit the operation of
14 slot machines, house-banked card games, house-banked table games
15 involving dice or roulette wheels, or games where winners are
16 determined by wagering on the outcome of a sports contest; provided,
17 however, that wagering on the outcome of sports contests may be
18 conducted in accordance with Section 4 of this act.
