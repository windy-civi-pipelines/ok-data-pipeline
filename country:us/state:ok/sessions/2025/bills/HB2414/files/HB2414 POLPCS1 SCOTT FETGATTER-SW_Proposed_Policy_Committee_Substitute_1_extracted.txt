8 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to school boards; amending 26 O.S.
2021, Section 13A-106, which relates to school board
10 candidate eligibility; prohibiting certain persons
from being eligible to run for certain district board
11 membership; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 13A-106, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 13A-106. A. To be eligible to be a candidate for
18 member of the board of education of a school district or technology
19 center school district, a person must have resided in the district
20 or, if the board seat is in an independent district, have resided in
21 that district for at least six (6) months preceding the first day of
22 the filing period, and have been a registered voter registered with
23 the county election board at an address located within the
24 geographical boundaries of the district for six (6) months preceding
Req. No. 12681 Page 1
1 the first day of the filing period. Beginning May 1, 1990, no
2 person shall be eligible to be a candidate for or elected to be a
3 member of the board of education of a school district or technology
4 center school district unless the person has been awarded a high
5 school diploma or certificate of high school equivalency. In school
6 districts that are divided into election districts, a candidate must
7 have resided in the district for at least six (6) months preceding
8 the first day of the filing period and have been a registered voter
9 registered with the county election board at an address located
10 within the geographical boundaries of the election district for six
11 (6) months preceding the first day of the filing period. Beginning
12 May 1, 1990, no person shall be eligible to be a candidate for or
13 elected to be a member of the board of education unless the person
14 has been awarded a high school diploma or certificate of high school
15 equivalency.
16 B. Any person who was previously employed by a school district
17 and whose employment was terminated for cause by the school district
18 shall not be an eligible candidate for member of the board of
19 education of the school district in which the person's employment
20 was terminated for cause.
21 C. To be eligible to vote in a school district election or a
22 technology center school district election, a person must be
23 registered with the county election board at an address located
24 within the geographical boundaries of the district. To be eligible
Req. No. 12681 Page 2
1 to v ote in an election district election within a school district, a
2 person must be registered with the county election board at an
3 address located within the geographical boundaries of the election
4 district.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
