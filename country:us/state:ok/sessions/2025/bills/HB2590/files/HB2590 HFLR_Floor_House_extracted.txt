10 An Act relating to public finance; imposing
requirement on Office of Management and Enterprise
11 Services; requiring certain form for use in contracts
related to management of federal funds; prescribing
12 required content of form; requiring filing of form
with certain entities and public officials; providing
13 for codification; and providing an effective date.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 255.10 of Title 62, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. The Office of Management and Enterprise Services shall
20 develop a form for use by each agency, board, commission, department
21 or other entity organized within the executive branch of state
22 government for use by such governmental entity in identifying
23 prospective vendors for contracts to manage federal funds if such
24 funds management is permissible pursuant to the federal law enabling
HB2590 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 and transferring such funds for expenditure by the state
2 governmental entity. Each such governmental entity shall complete
3 the form and submit it to the Office of Management and Enterprise
4 Services as required by the Office of Management and Enterprise
5 Services.
6 B. The form shall include, but shall not be limited to, the
7 following:
8 1. The legal name of the entity proposing to manage the federal
9 funds pursuant to contract, including any trade name or similar name
10 which is not the formal legal name of the entity that will enter
11 into the contract;
12 2. The jurisdiction in which the prospective vendor entity was
13 originally incorporated or otherwise created pursuant to the laws of
14 the applicable jurisdiction;
15 3. The legal names of the principal officers of the prospective
16 vendor entity which proposes to manage the federal funds, which may
17 include, but shall not be limited to the chief executive officer,
18 the chief financial officer, the chief information or technology
19 officer and the chief operating officer;
20 4. A listing of the prior experience of the prospective vendor
21 entity or its officers or employees with regard to the management of
22 federal funds on behalf of any state or local government entity;
23 5. The most recently completed financial audit of the books and
24 records of the prospective vendor entity prior to the date as of

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 whic h the form prescribed pursuant to the provisions of this section
2 is submitted to the Office of Management and Enterprise Services, in
3 cases involving a vendor with no prior experience in the management
4 of federal funds. No vendor shall be required to disclose
5 information which is proprietary or protected from disclosure by
6 private agreement or that is considered to be a trade secret.
7 6. Any and all pending civil cases, whether in federal court,
8 state court or the judicial system of any other country or nation
9 which identifies the prospective vendor entity as a defendant in a
10 proceeding related directly to the management of federal funds.
11 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 255.11 of Title 62, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 The forms submitted to the Office of Management and Enterprise
15 Servies as required by this act shall also be submitted to the
16 Governor, the Speaker of the Oklahoma House of Representatives, the
17 President Pro Tempore of the Oklahoma State Senate, and the Chairs
18 and Vice-Chairs of the standing committees of both chambers of the
19 Oklahoma Legislature responsible for appropriation and budget
20 matters.
21 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
22
23 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON GOVERNMENT OVERSIGHT, dated
03/10/2025 - DO PASS, As Amended and Coauthored.
