7 An Act relating to the Department of Mental Health
and Substance Abuse Services; requiring that portions
8 of certain appropriated funds be used for certain
purposes; authorizing request for early transfers of
9 certain funds for specific purpose; providing an
effective date; and declaring an emergency.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Department of
14 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services in Enrolled House Bill
15 No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the
16 sum of Ten Million Eight Hundred Twelve Thousand Four Hundred Eight
17 Dollars ($10,812,408.00) shall be used for program growth and to
18 replace funding no longer available due to the reduction of federal
19 matching dollars.
20 SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the Department of
21 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services in Enrolled House Bill
22 No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the
23 sum of Nine Million Four Hundred Eighty Thousand Four Hundred
24 Twenty-one Dollars ($9,480,421.00) shall be used to meet the
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1140 Page 1
1 obli gations required in the consent decree pursuant to case number
2 23-cv-81-GKF-JFJ filed in the United States District Court for the
3 Northern District of Oklahoma.
4 SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the Department of
5 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services in Enrolled House Bill
6 No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the
7 sum of Eighteen Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars
8 ($18,500,000.00) shall be used for the continuum of care for
9 children in crisis.
10 SECTION 4. From the funds appropriated to the Department of
11 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services in Enrolled House Bill
12 No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the
13 sum of One Million Three Hundred Thousand Dollars ($1,300,000.00)
14 shall be used to maintain services for veterans and their families.
15 SECTION 5. The Commissioner of Mental Health and Substance
16 Abuse Services may request through the Director of the Office of
17 Management and Enterprise Services the early transfer by the
18 Oklahoma Tax Commission of tax collections to the General Revenue
19 Fund for the purpose of early allocation to the Department’s
20 disbursing funds to alleviate cash-flow problems.
21 SECTION 6. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
22 SECTION 7. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
23 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
