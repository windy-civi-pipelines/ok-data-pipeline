COMMITTEE AMENDMENT

State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend amendment HB1466 FULLPCS1
Page 3 Section 1 Lines 12
Of the Engrossed Bill
By deleting after the word "may" but before the word "the" the following
language: "implement any recommendations from" and by adding in lieu
thereof the following language: "change a school districts’ accreditation
status from a warning to probation, or from probation to non-accredited
based on a recommendation by".
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
