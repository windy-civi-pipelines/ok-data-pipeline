10 An Act relating to distribution of estates; amending
58 O.S. 2021, Section 693, which relates to
11 disposition of monies to minor; requiring executor or
administrator to make certain application to court;
12 clarifying release of certain monies; updating
statutory language; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 58 O.S. 2021, Section 693, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 693. A. Whenever a final account and order of
19 distribution shall direct the payment of monies to a minor, and no
20 person shall within ninety (90) days thereafter become the legal and
21 qualified guardian for the minor, so that the executor or
22 administrator may be discharged, the court may direct the executor
23 or administrator shall make an application to the court for
24

24 thereafter, so that the executor or administrator may be discharged,

1 the court may direct the executor or administrator to prepare an
2 order directing the county treasurer to make the deposit of funds in
3 a specified institution and for a specified term. Upon receipt of
4 the order, the court clerk shall make a temporary deposit in the
5 case, and forward the court’s order to the county treasurer for
6 deposit of the funds in a specified institution for a specified
7 term, with the same effect as though taken from the person; and the
8 treasurer shall hold the monies in trust for the person until a
9 legal or personal representative shall demand and accept the same;
10 provided, that all such monies in the hands of the treasurer at the
11 expiration of the treasurer’s term of office must be turned over to
12 the successor in office.
13 C. In the event no person qualified to receive money deposited
14 with the court clerk makes demand therefor within thirty (30) days
15 after receipt by the court clerk and the deposit is in excess of One
16 Hundred Dollars ($100.00), the court clerk is authorized and
17 directed to invest such funds in one or more savings accounts or
18 certificates of deposit in a bank or savings and loan association
19 whose deposits are insured by an agency of the federal government.
20 When the person legally entitled thereto makes request upon the
21 court clerk, the account or fund, together with all accumulations,
22 shall be paid over to the person legally entitled thereto upon the
23 court clerk taking a receipt in full for such payment, which receipt
24 shall be filed in and become a part of the records of the case.
