3 Section 3622. The Legislature hereby finds that the production
4 of films live audience episodic television in Oklahoma not only
5 provides jobs for Oklahomans and dollars for Oklahoma businesses,
6 but also enhances the state's image nationwide with a focus on
7 authentic storytelling targeting Middle America. Recognizing The
8 Legislature also finds that the high costs of film production are
9 driving motion picture and television production out of the country,
10 most notably to Canada, and that the film entertainment industry is
11 always seeking attractive economical locations that can help cut the
12 costs of production, the. The Legislature further finds that the
13 State of Oklahoma, with the appropriate incentive, can become an
14 attractive a strategic site for film live audience episodic
15 television production and that Oklahoma is presently among several
16 states with minimal incentives to attract the film industry. It is
17 therefore the intent of the Legislature that Oklahoma provide an a
18 pilot program incentive that will stand out among those of other
19 states and increase film specifically for live audience episodic
20 television production in this state, carving out a niche in the
21 industry that Oklahoma can be known for.
