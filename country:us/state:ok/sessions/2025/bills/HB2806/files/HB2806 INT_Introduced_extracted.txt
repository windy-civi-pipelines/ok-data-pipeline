1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to state government; imposing
requirement with respect to legislative measures and
8 proposed amendments to measures; requiring hyperlink
or similar method for access to content of certain
9 statutory provisions; providing exception for
enrolled versions; providing for codification; and
10 providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 452.11 of Title 74, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. The Oklahoma House of Representatives and the Oklahoma State
18 Senate shall develop and implement a system for purposes of the
19 drafting and consideration of any measure or any amendment to a
20 measure presented for any vote during the legislative process that
21 meets the requirements of subsection B of this section.
22 B. If the measure or proposed amendment to a measure contains a
23 reference to any Oklahoma statute the text of which does not appear
24 in the measure or the amendment to a measure, a hyperlink or
Req. No. 11291 Page 1
1 equi valent technology shall be inserted into the measure to allow a
2 reader of the measure or amendment to a measure to activate the
3 hyperlink or equivalent connective technology in order to provide
4 access to the entire text of the statute.
5 C. The provisions of this section shall be applicable to all
6 versions of a measure except the enrolled version.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
