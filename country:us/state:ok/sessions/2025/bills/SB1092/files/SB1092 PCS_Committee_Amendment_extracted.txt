16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 426.2 of Title 63, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. The Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority shall require all
20 medical marijuana commercial growers licensed as an indoor grow
21 facility operating within the corporate limits of a municipality to
22 submit an odor control plan on or before January 1, 2027. An odor
23 control plan shall include:
24 1. Facility information including, but not limited to:
Req. No. 1612 Page 1
1 a. the name of the facility,
2 b. the contact information of the owner or operator,
3 c. the facility’s physical and mailing address,
4 d. the facility type,
5 e. the facility’s hours of operation, and
6 f. a description of the facility’s operations;
7 2. Facility odor emissions information including, but not
8 limited to:
9 a. the facility floor plan,
10 b. odor-emitting activities, and
11 c. the timing and duration of odor-emitting activities;
12 and
13 3. Odor mitigation best management practices, which shall
14 include:
15 a. odor mitigation practices to be deployed to control
16 odor-emitting activities, sources, and locations,
17 b. how such practices will be utilized, and
18 c. during what times such practices will be utilized.
19 B. If a medical marijuana business receives three or more odor
20 nuisance complaints within a thirty-day period, the business shall
21 be required to submit an odor control plan to the Authority.
22 C. The Authority shall promulgate rules as necessary to
23 implement the provisions of this section.

18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. The Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority shall require all
20 medical marijuana commercial growers licensed as an indoor grow
21 facility operating within the corporate limits of a municipality to
22 submit an odor control plan on or before January 1, 2027. An odor
23 control plan shall include:
24 1. Facility information including, but not limited to:
Req. No. 1612 Page 1

1 a. the name of the facility,
2 b. the contact information of the owner or operator,
3 c. the facility’s physical and mailing address,
4 d. the facility type,
5 e. the facility’s hours of operation, and
6 f. a description of the facility’s operations;
7 2. Facility odor emissions information including, but not
8 limited to:
9 a. the facility floor plan,
10 b. odor-emitting activities, and
11 c. the timing and duration of odor-emitting activities;
12 and
13 3. Odor mitigation best management practices, which shall
14 include:
15 a. odor mitigation practices to be deployed to control
16 odor-emitting activities, sources, and locations,
17 b. how such practices will be utilized, and
18 c. during what times such practices will be utilized.
19 B. If a medical marijuana business receives three or more odor
20 nuisance complaints within a thirty-day period, the business shall
21 be required to submit an odor control plan to the Authority.
22 C. The Authority shall promulgate rules as necessary to
23 implement the provisions of this section.
