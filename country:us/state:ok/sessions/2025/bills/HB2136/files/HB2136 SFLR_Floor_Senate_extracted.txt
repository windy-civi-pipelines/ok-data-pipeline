12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 271 of Title 44, unless there is
15 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
17 for the Oklahoma Military Department to be designated the "Oklahoma
18 National Guard Supplemental Retirement Pay Revolving Fund". The
19 fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year
20 limitations, and shall consist of all monies received by the
21 Oklahoma Military Department from funds provided by law. All monies
22 accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may
23 be budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma Military Department for the
24 purpose of implementing the provisions of Section 2 of this act.
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB2136 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 Expe nditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants issued by
2 the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with
3 the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
4 approval and payment.
5 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
6 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 272 of Title 44, unless there is
7 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
8 A. The provisions of this section shall not be operative and no
9 payments shall be made from the Oklahoma National Guard Supplemental
10 Retirement Pay Revolving Fund until November 1, 2027.
11 B. The provisions of this section shall only be applicable to a
12 member of the Oklahoma National Guard who has accrued enough service
13 credit prior to the effective date of this act in order to retire
14 from the system maintained by the Defense Finance and Accounting
15 Service, or its successor in interest, for the benefit of members of
16 state National Guards, but who has not attained the required age
17 prior to the effective date of this act in order to be paid the full
18 amount of such benefit. No member of the Oklahoma National Guard
19 shall be eligible to receive a payment pursuant to the provisions of
20 this section unless the member has completed twenty-five (25) years
21 of military service and retires as a member of the Oklahoma National
22 Guard.
23 C. The provisions of this section shall not be applicable to
24 any person who retires with an active duty military pension.
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB2136 SFLR Page 2
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 D. The amount of the payment shall be determined as follows:
2 1. Determine the amount of retirement the member would receive
3 if the member had reached the age to accrue federal retirement
4 benefits;
5 2. If the members of the active duty military retirement system
6 were eligible for a cost-of-living adjustment for the year for which
7 the computation of the payment amount authorized by this section is
8 to be made, the cost-of-living adjustment amount in an annualized
9 form shall be added to the result of the computation in paragraph 1
10 of this subsection each year; and
11 3. The resulting amount shall be paid to the Oklahoma National
12 Guard member in twelve equal monthly amounts with allowance as
13 required for rounding.
14 E. Any benefit payable to an eligible member of the Oklahoma
15 National Guard shall only be payable during the member's lifetime,
16 and no benefit authorized pursuant to the provisions of this section
17 shall be payable to a beneficiary other than amounts paid to a
18 member prior to the member's death which are payable to another
19 person pursuant to the provisions of a joint tenancy bank account
20 with a right of survivorship naming a person or persons as
21 beneficiary, a bank account with a transfer on death or payable on
22 death feature, an express trust, including, but not limited to, a
23 trust created by the payee member during his or her lifetime, a will
24 or the statute of intestate succession for distribution of the
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB2136 SFLR Page 3
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 asse ts of a person who does not otherwise provide for the
2 disposition of his or her assets after death.
3 F. The payment otherwise authorized pursuant to the provisions
4 of this act may only be made to a person who has become eligible to
5 receive retirement benefits from the retirement system under the
6 supervision of the Defense Finance and Accounting Service, or its
7 successor in interest, as of the date the first payment pursuant to
8 this act is authorized.
9 G. The payments authorized by this section shall be paid to the
10 eligible member until the member begins to receive federal
11 retirement benefits from the Defense Finance and Accounting Service,
12 or its successor in interest, but shall not be paid for any period
13 of time after such time period.
14 H. In the event the funds required for full payment to all
15 eligible members pursuant to the provisions of this section are not
16 sufficient, the payments to the eligible members shall be prorated
17 by dividing the total number of eligible members by the total amount
18 of available funds. The resulting quotient shall be multiplied by
19 the payment amount otherwise due to the eligible member and the
20 result of that computation shall be the reduced dollar amount paid
21 to each member for the applicable period. For any period of time
22 during which payments are prorated pursuant to this subsection, the
23 next increment of available funds shall be used to make payments to
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB2136 SFLR Page 4
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 the eligible members whose payment amounts were prorated to
2 compensate for the reduction made in the prior payment period.
3 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
