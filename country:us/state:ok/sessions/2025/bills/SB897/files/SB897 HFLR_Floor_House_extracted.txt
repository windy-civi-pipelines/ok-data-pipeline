9 An Act relating to the Corporation Commission;
amending 17 O.S. 2021, Section 180.10, which relates
10 to the Corporation Commission Plugging Fund;
modifying termination date of fund; updating
11 statutory language; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 17 O.S. 2021, Section 180.10, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 180.10. A. There is hereby created in the State
18 Treasury a fund for the Corporation Commission to be designated the
19 “Corporation Commission Plugging Fund”. The plugging fund shall
20 consist of monies received by the Corporation Commission as required
21 by law to be deposited to the credit of said the fund. The fund
22 shall be a continuing fund not subject to fiscal year limitations
23 and shall not be subject to legislative appropriations.
24 Expenditures from the plugging fund shall be made pursuant to the

1 laws of this state and the statutes relating to the Corporation
2 Commission. For each fiscal year, the Commission may expend not
3 more than eight percent (8%) of the total amount deposited to the
4 credit of the plugging fund during the previous fiscal year for the
5 purpose of responding to occurrences of seeping natural gas as
6 provided for in Section 317.1 of Title 52 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
7 In addition, expenditures from the plugging fund may be made
8 pursuant to the Oklahoma Central Purchasing Act, Section 85.1 et
9 seq. of Title 74 of the Oklahoma Statutes, for purposes of
10 immediately responding to emergency situations, within the
11 Commission’s jurisdiction, having potentially critical environmental
12 or public safety impact. Warrants for expenditures from the fund
13 shall be drawn by the State Treasurer, based on claims signed by an
14 authorized employee of the Corporation Commission and approved for
15 payment by the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise
16 Services. The provisions of this act or rules promulgated pursuant
17 thereto, shall not be construed to relieve or in any way diminish
18 the surety bonding requirements required by Section 318.1 of Title
19 52 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
20 B. Prior to July 1, 2026 July 1, 2031, the plugging fund shall
21 be maintained at Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000.00). If the
22 plugging fund falls below the five-million-dollar maintenance level,
23 the Corporation Commission shall notify the Oklahoma Tax Commission
24 that the plugging fund has fallen below the required maintenance

1 leve l and that the excise tax which has been levied by subsection A

5 pursuant to Section 1103 of Title 68 of the Oklahoma Statutes is to
6 be imposed. Such additional excise tax shall be imposed and
