8 An Act relating to Teachers’ Retirement System of
Oklahoma; providing for benefits increase; and
9 providing for codification.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 17-116.23 of Title 70, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 Any person receiving benefits from the Teachers’ Retirement
17 System of Oklahoma as of June 30, 2025, who continues to receive
18 benefits on or after the effective date of this act shall receive a
19 two percent (2%) increase in benefits.
