10 An Act relating to Medicaid; directing the Oklahoma
Health Care Authority to expand coverage for Medicaid
11 services or programs; providing for codification;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
12 emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4020 of Title 56, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall expand Medicaid
20 coverage for Medicaid services or programs for in-home physical
21 therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy to ninety-six
22 percent (96%) of what Medicare coverage pays.
23 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
