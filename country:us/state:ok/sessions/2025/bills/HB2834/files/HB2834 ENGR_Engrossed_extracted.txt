14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4020 of Title 56, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall expand Medicaid
19 coverage for Medicaid services or programs for in-home physical
20 therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy to ninety-six
21 percent (96%) of what Medicare coverage pays.
