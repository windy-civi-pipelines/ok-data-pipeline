5 Oklahoma unless an authorization form, prescribed and furnished by
6 Service Oklahoma, or notarized affidavit authorizing the finger
7 imaging of the person and signed by the legal custodial parent,
8 legal guardian, or legal custodian of the person, is in the
9 possession of Service Oklahoma.

11 other than Service Oklahoma the Department of Public Safety shall
12 have access to any information collected through the use of

15 Oklahoma State Bureau of Investigation shall have access to such
16 imaging for the purpose of identifying a person who is deceased,
17 missing, or endangered. Each application for an order authorizing
18 the access to any information collected through the use of
19 computerized finger imaging shall be made in writing upon oath or
20 affirmation to a judge of competent jurisdiction. Each application
21 shall establish probable cause for belief that a named individual is
22 committing, has committed, or is about to commit a particular
23 violation of law.

9 required by this chapter, excepting those acts as
10 provided in paragraph 2 of this section,

13 under conditions provided in subparagraph e of

16 information on a driver license or identification

19 years, or by both such fine and imprisonment.

5 by Service Oklahoma the Department, such person shall not be
6 prosecuted for a violation under this section unless the person
7 exceeds the scope or duration of the Department’s authorization.
8 Upon termination of such investigation or operation or upon request
9 of the Department, Service Oklahoma shall forthwith cause such
10 license to be returned to Service Oklahoma the chief administrator
11 of the law enforcement agency that requests the issuance of the
12 license and the recipient of the license shall be jointly
13 responsible to ensure the license is promptly returned to the

16 duties set forth in this paragraph. The Department may enter into
17 interagency agreements with law enforcement agencies that request
18 issuance of such a license to set forth the terms of the
19 authorization for use of the license, the terms for custody and
20 control of the license, and the terms for duration and revocation of
