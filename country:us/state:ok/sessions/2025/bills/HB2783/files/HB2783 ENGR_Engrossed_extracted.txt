7 An Act relating to Tobacco Settlement Endowment
Trust; amending 62 O.S. 2021, Section 2308, which
8 relates to the Board of Directors of the Tobacco
Settlement Endowment Trust Fund; and providing that
9 appointees serve at the pleasure of their appointing
authority.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 62 O.S. 2021, Section 2308, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 2308. A. Section 40 of Article X of the Oklahoma
16 Constitution created the Board of Directors of the Tobacco
17 Settlement Endowment Trust Fund.
18 B. The members of the Board of Directors shall receive no
19 salary for serving on the Board. All members shall receive
20 necessary travel expenses for the performance of their official
21 duties in accordance with the provisions of the State Travel
22 Reimbursement Act, provided that members of the Board of Directors
23 shall be paid necessary travel expenses pursuant to the State Travel
24 Reimbursement Act by their appointing authority until the Board of
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2783 Page 1
1 Dire ctors adopts its initial budget and monies are made available
2 for expenditures pursuant to the budget.
3 C. The terms of the Board of Directors shall be staggered as
4 provided by subsection B of Section 2 of Enrolled Senate Bill No.
5 372 of the 1st Session of the 48th Oklahoma Legislature, as amended
6 by Section 4 of Enrolled House Bill No. 1003 of the 1st Session of
7 the 48th Oklahoma Legislature serve at the pleasure of their
8 appointing authority, not to exceed a seven-year term of office.
9 D. The Board of Directors shall annually elect a chair and any
10 other officers, provided that no member shall be elected chair for
11 more than two (2) consecutive years.
12 E. The initial meeting of the Board of Directors shall be
13 called by the Attorney General and the State Treasurer.
14 Passed the House of Representatives the 20th day of May, 2025.
