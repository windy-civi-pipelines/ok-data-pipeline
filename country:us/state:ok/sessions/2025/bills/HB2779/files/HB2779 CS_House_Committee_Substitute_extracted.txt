11 An Act relating to the Department of Public Safety;
requiring that certain funds be utilized for certain
12 purpose; determining the purposed; providing an
effective date; and declaring an emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
17 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
18 From the funds appropriated to the Department of Public Safety
19 in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st Regular Session of the
20 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of Seven Hundred Fifty Thousand
21 Dollars ($750,000.00) shall be used for the School Secure Program
22 transferred from the State Board of Education to the Department of
23 Public Safety.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.

1 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
2 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby

4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
