8 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to public retirement systems;
authorizing cost-of-living increases for certain
10 members of the Oklahoma Firefighters Pension and
Retirement System, the Oklahoma Police Pension and
11 Retirement System, the Uniform Retirement System for
Justices and Judges, the Oklahoma Law Enforcement
12 Retirement System, the Teachers' Retirement System of
Oklahoma, and the Oklahoma Public Employees
13 Retirement System; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
14
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 49-143.8 of Title 11, unless
20 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 A. Except as provided in subsection B of this section and
22 except for persons receiving benefits pursuant to Section 49-101 of
23 Title 11 of the Oklahoma Statutes, any member receiving benefits
24 from the Oklahoma Firefighters Pension and Retirement System as of
Req. No. 12674 Page 1
RBH No. 12674
1 June 30, 2026, who continues to receive benefits on or after July 1,
2 2026, shall receive an increase in benefits as follows:
3 1. Zero percent (0%) if the member retired after June 30, 2023;
4 2. Two percent (2%) for the first Sixty Thousand Dollars
5 ($60,000.00) of the member’s gross annual retirement benefit if the
6 member retired after June 30, 2018, but before July 1, 2023; and
7 3. Four percent (4%) for the first Sixty Thousand Dollars
8 ($60,000.00) of the member’s gross annual retirement benefit if the
9 member retired before July 1, 2018.
10 B. Any increase in benefits a person is eligible to receive
11 pursuant to repealed Section 49-136 of Title 11 of the Oklahoma
12 Statutes after June 30, 2020, shall be used to offset the increase
13 in benefits provided in subsection A of this section.
14 C. Effective July 1, 2026, any person receiving benefits
15 pursuant to Section 49-101 of Title 11 of the Oklahoma Statutes
16 shall each receive:
17 1. No increase if the member retired after June 30, 2023;
18 2. Two percent (2%) for the first Sixty Thousand Dollars
19 ($60,000.00) of the member’s gross annual retirement benefit if the
20 member retired after June 30, 2018, but before July 1, 2023; and
21 3. Four percent (4%) for the first Sixty Thousand Dollars
22 ($60,000.00) of the member’s gross annual retirement benefit if the
23 member retired before July 1, 2018.
