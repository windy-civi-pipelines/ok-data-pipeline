7 An Act relating to the State Department of Health;
requiring that portions of certain appropriated funds
8 be used for certain purposes; stating purpose of
certain disbursement; requiring the use of certain
9 data; directing the Department to develop method of
disbursement; authorizing promulgation of rules;
10 providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
16 of Health in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the
17 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Hundred Fifty Thousand
18 Dollars ($150,000.00) shall be used to maintain sickle cell
19 outreach.
20 SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
21 of Health in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the
22 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of Two Hundred Thousand Dollars
23 ($200,000.00) shall be used for the operations of the Oklahoma State
24
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1141 Page 1
1 Athl etic Commission, pursuant to the provisions of the Oklahoma
2 State Athletic Commission Act.
3 SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
4 of Health in Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of the 1st Session of the
5 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of Three Million Dollars
6 ($3,000,000.00) shall be disbursed to health centers authorized
7 under Section 330 of the Public Health Service Act, and incorporated
8 in the State of Oklahoma. Any disbursement shall be for the purpose
9 of increasing access to primary health care which may include
10 medical, dental, and/or mental health and substance abuse treatment
11 services in or near designated Medically Underserved Areas or
12 Medically Underserved Populations. Any disbursement for increased
13 access to primary health care services shall be supported by data
14 according to proportions of patient volume and access for the
15 uninsured. The method of disbursement shall be developed by the
16 State Department of Health. The State Commissioner of Health is
17 authorized to promulgate the method of disbursement into rules.
18 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective July 1, 2025.
19 SECTION 5. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
20 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
21 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
22 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
